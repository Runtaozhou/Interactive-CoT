## 1. Problem statement

<fact1>Ludwig works 7 days a week</fact1> and he usually works half of the day during Friday, Saturday, and Sunday. If <fact2>his daily salary is $10</fact2>, how much does he earn every week?

## 2. Facts

<fact1>Days worked per week: 7 days</fact1>
<fact2>Daily salary: $10</fact2>

## 3. Steps

<step1> Calculate Ludwig's earnings from Monday to Thursday </step1>
<formula> daily salary × number of days </formula>
<calculation> $10 × 4 = $40 </calculation>
<var> weekday_earnings : $40 </var>

<step2> Calculate Ludwig's daily earnings for Friday, Saturday, and Sunday </step2>
<formula> daily salary ÷ 2 </formula>
<calculation> $10/2 = $5 </calculation>
<var> weekend_daily_earnings : $5 </var>

<step3> Calculate Ludwig's total earnings for Friday, Saturday, and Sunday </step3>
<formula> weekend_daily_earnings × number of weekend days </formula>
<calculation> $5 × 2 = $10 </calculation>
<var> weekend_total_earnings : $10 </var>

<step4> Calculate Ludwig's total weekly earnings </step4>
<formula> weekday_earnings + weekend_total_earnings </formula>
<calculation> $40 + $10 = $50 </calculation>
<var> weekly_earnings : $50 </var>

<wrongstep> 3 </wrongstep>

## 5. Output

<output> 50 </output>
