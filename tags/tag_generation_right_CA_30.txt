## 1. Problem statement

Karl sells clothing in his store. <fact1>He sells a T-shirt that costs $5</fact1>, <fact2>some pants that cost $4</fact2>, and <fact3>some skirts that cost $6</fact3>, <fact4>he also sells some refurbished t-shirts that cost half the original price</fact4>. How much is his total income if he sold <fact5>two T-shirts</fact5>, <fact6>one pair of pants</fact6>, <fact7>four skirts</fact7>, and <fact8>six refurbished T-shirts</fact8>?

## 2. Facts

<fact1>T-shirt cost: $5</fact1>
<fact2>Pants cost: $4</fact2>
<fact3>Skirt cost: $6</fact3>
<fact4>Refurbished T-shirt cost: half of original price</fact4>
<fact5>Number of T-shirts sold: 2</fact5>
<fact6>Number of pants sold: 1</fact6>
<fact7>Number of skirts sold: 4</fact7>
<fact8>Number of refurbished T-shirts sold: 6</fact8>

## 3. Steps

<step1> Calculate the cost of a refurbished T-shirt </step1>
<formula> Original price / 2 </formula>
<calculation> $5 / 2 = $2.5 </calculation>
<var> refurbished_tshirt_cost: $2.5 </var>

<step2> Calculate the income from refurbished T-shirts </step2>
<formula> Refurbished T-shirt cost × Number of refurbished T-shirts sold </formula>
<calculation> $2.5 × 6 = $15 </calculation>
<var> refurbished_tshirt_income: $15 </var>

<step3> Calculate the income from T-shirts </step3>
<formula> T-shirt cost × Number of T-shirts sold </formula>
<calculation> $5 × 2 = $10 </calculation>
<var> tshirt_income: $10 </var>

<step4> Calculate the income from pants </step4>
<formula> Pants cost × Number of pants sold </formula>
<calculation> $4 × 1 = $4 </calculation>
<var> pants_income: $4 </var>

<step5> Calculate the income from skirts </step5>
<formula> Skirt cost × Number of skirts sold </formula>
<calculation> $6 × 4 = $24 </calculation>
<var> skirt_income: $24 </var>

<step6> Calculate the total income </step6>
<formula> Refurbished T-shirt income + T-shirt income + Pants income + Skirt income </formula>
<calculation> $15 + $10 + $4 + $24 = $53 </calculation>
<var> total_income: $53 </var>

<wrongstep> 4 </wrongstep>

## 5. Output

<output>53</output>
