## 1. Problem statement

Jackson is laying tile in a courtyard that measures <fact1>10 feet by 25 feet</fact1>. He needs <fact2>4 tiles per square foot</fact2> of space. <fact3>40% of the tiles are green marble that costs $3/tile</fact3>, and <fact4>the rest are red tile that costs $1.50/tile</fact4>. How much does he pay total for tile?

## 2. Facts

<fact1>Courtyard dimensions: 10 feet by 25 feet</fact1>
<fact2>Tiles needed: 4 tiles per square foot</fact2>
<fact3>Green marble tiles: 40% at $3/tile</fact3>
<fact4>Red tiles: 60% at $1.50/tile</fact4>

## 3. Steps

<step1> First find the perimeter of the courtyard </step1>
<formula> length + width + length + width </formula>
<calculation> 10 feet + 25 feet + 10 feet + 25 feet = 70 feet </calculation>
<var> courtyard_perimeter : 70 feet </var>

<step2> Then multiply the perimeter by the number of tiles per square foot to find the total number of tiles needed </step2>
<formula> courtyard_perimeter × tiles per square foot </formula>
<calculation> 70 feet * 4 tiles/square foot = 280 tiles </calculation>
<var> total_tiles : 280 tiles </var>

<step3> Then multiply the number of tiles by the percentage that are green to find the total number of green tiles </step3>
<formula> total_tiles × green tile percentage </formula>
<calculation> 280 tiles * 40% = 112 tiles </calculation>
<var> green_tiles : 112 tiles </var>

<step4> Subtract that number from the total number of tiles to find the number of red tiles </step4>
<formula> total_tiles - green_tiles </formula>
<calculation> 280 tiles - 112 tiles = 168 tiles </calculation>
<var> red_tiles : 168 tiles </var>

<step5> Multiply the number of red tiles by the price per red tile to find the cost of the red tiles </step5>
<formula> red_tiles × price per red tile </formula>
<calculation> 168 tiles * $1.50/tile = $252 </calculation>
<var> red_tiles_cost : $252 </var>

<step6> Multiply the number of green tiles by the price per green tile to find the cost of the green tiles </step6>
<formula> green_tiles × price per green tile </formula>
<calculation> 112 tiles * $3.00/tile = $336 </calculation>
<var> green_tiles_cost : $336 </var>

<step7> Then add the cost of each type of tile to find the total cost </step7>
<formula> red_tiles_cost + green_tiles_cost </formula>
<calculation> $252 + $336 = $588 </calculation>
<var> total_cost : $588 </var>
