## 1. Problem statement

When <fact1>Diane turns 30</fact1>, she will be half the age of Alex and twice as old as Allison. <fact2>Diane is 16 years old now</fact2>. What is the sum of the ages of Alex and Allison now?

## 2. Facts

<fact1>Diane's future age: 30 years</fact1>
<fact2>Diane's current age: 16 years</fact2>

## 3. Steps

<step1> Calculate Alex's age when Diane turns 30 </step1>
<formula> Diane's future age × 2 </formula>
<calculation> 30 × 2 = 60 years old </calculation>
<var> alex_future_age : 60 years </var>

<step2> Calculate how many years until Diane turns 30 </step2>
<formula> Diane's future age - Diane's current age </formula>
<calculation> 30 - 16 = 14 years </calculation>
<var> years_until_30 : 14 years </var>

<step3> Calculate Alex's current age </step3>
<formula> alex_future_age - years_until_30 </formula>
<calculation> 60 - 14 = 46 </calculation>
<var> alex_current_age : 46 years </var>

<step4> Calculate Allison's current age </step4>
<formula> Diane's future age - years_until_30 </formula>
<calculation> 30 - 14 = 16 years old </calculation>
<var> allison_current_age : 16 years </var>

<step5> Calculate the sum of Alex and Allison's current ages </step5>
<formula> alex_current_age + allison_current_age </formula>
<calculation> 46 + 16 = 62 years old </calculation>
<var> sum_of_ages : 62 years </var>

<wrongstep> 4 </wrongstep>

## 5. Output

<output> 62 </output>
