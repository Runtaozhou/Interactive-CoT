## 1. Problem statement

Leo collects stickers. <fact1>Two years ago, he had 100 stickers in his collection.</fact1> <fact2>Last year, Leo collected 50 stickers.</fact2> <fact3>This year, he collected twice the number of stickers as the previous year.</fact3> How many stickers does Leo have in his collection?

## 2. Facts

<fact1>Starting stickers (two years ago): 100</fact1>
<fact2>Stickers collected last year: 50</fact2>
<fact3>Stickers collected this year: 2 times previous year</fact3>

## 3. Steps

<step1> Calculate how many stickers Leo had after last year </step1>
<formula> Starting stickers + Stickers collected last year </formula>
<calculation> 100 + 50 = 150 </calculation>
<var> stickers_after_last_year: 150 </var>

<step2> Calculate how many stickers Leo collected this year </step2>
<formula> 2 × Stickers collected last year </formula>
<calculation> 2 × 50 = 100 </calculation>
<var> stickers_collected_this_year: 100 </var>

<step3> Calculate the total number of stickers Leo has now </step3>
<formula> Stickers after last year + Stickers collected this year </formula>
<calculation> 150 + 100 = 250 </calculation>
<var> total_stickers: 250 </var>

## 4. Wrongsteps

<wrongstep> 2 </wrongstep>

## 5. Output

<output>250</output>
