## 1. Problem statement

At Kaleb's Restaurant, a group with <fact1>six adults</fact1> and <fact2>two children</fact2> came in to eat. If each <fact3>adult meal cost six dollars</fact3> and each <fact4>children's meal was $4</fact4>, and <fact5>every person ordered a soda for $2</fact5> how much was the bill?

## 2. Facts

<fact1>Number of adults: 6</fact1>
<fact2>Number of children: 2</fact2>
<fact3>Cost per adult meal: $6</fact3>
<fact4>Cost per children's meal: $4</fact4>
<fact5>Cost per soda: $2</fact5>

## 3. Steps

<step1>Calculate the cost for children's meals</step1>
<formula>Number of children × Cost per children's meal</formula>
<calculation>2 children * $4 = $8</calculation>
<var>children_meal_cost: $8</var>

<step2>Calculate the total number of people ordering drinks</step2>
<formula>Number of adults + Number of children</formula>
<calculation>6 + 2 = 8</calculation>
<var>total_people: 8</var>

<step3>Calculate the cost for all drinks</step3>
<formula>Total people × Cost per soda</formula>
<calculation>8 people * $2 = $16</calculation>
<var>drink_cost: $16</var>

<step4>Calculate the total cost of the meal</step4>
<formula>Adult meal cost + Children meal cost + Drink cost</formula>
<calculation>$6 + $8 + $16 = $30</calculation>
<var>total_bill: $30</var>

<wrongstep>4</wrongstep>

## 5. Output

<output>30</output>
