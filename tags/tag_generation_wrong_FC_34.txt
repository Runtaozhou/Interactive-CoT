## 1. Problem statement

A school needs to paint the classroom, which is <fact1>9 meters long</fact1>, <fact2>8 meters wide</fact2>, and <fact3>3 meters high</fact3>. The classroom has <fact4>4 windows</fact4>, <fact5>2 blackboards</fact5>, and <fact6>2 doors</fact6>. The windows are <fact7>1m by 1.5m</fact7>, the blackboards are <fact8>6m by 1m</fact8>, and the doors are <fact9>2m by 1m</fact9>. It is known that the paint fee is <fact10>$4.80 per square meter</fact10>. How much does it cost to paint a classroom?

## 2. Facts

<fact1>Classroom length: 9 meters</fact1>
<fact2>Classroom width: 8 meters</fact2>
<fact3>Classroom height: 3 meters</fact3>
<fact4>Number of windows: 4</fact4>
<fact5>Number of blackboards: 2</fact5>
<fact6>Number of doors: 2</fact6>
<fact7>Window dimensions: 1m by 1.5m</fact7>
<fact8>Blackboard dimensions: 6m by 1m</fact8>
<fact9>Door dimensions: 2m by 1m</fact9>
<fact10>Paint fee: $4.80 per square meter</fact10>

## 3. Steps

<step1> Calculate the total length to be painted around the classroom excluding the floor </step1>
<formula> (length + width) * 2 + height * 2 * 2 </formula>
<calculation> (9 + 8) * 2 + 3 * 2 * 2 = 34 meters </calculation>
<var> total_length : 34 meters </var>

<step2> Calculate the area of one window </step2>
<formula> window length * window width </formula>
<calculation> 1 * 1.5 = 1.5 square meters </calculation>
<var> one_window_area : 1.5 square meters </var>

<step3> Calculate the total area of all windows </step3>
<formula> number of windows * one window area </formula>
<calculation> 4 * 1.5 = 6 square meters </calculation>
<var> total_windows_area : 6 square meters </var>

<step4> Calculate the area of one blackboard </step4>
<formula> blackboard length * blackboard width </formula>
<calculation> 6 * 1 = 6 square meters </calculation>
<var> one_blackboard_area : 6 square meters </var>

<step5> Calculate the total area of all blackboards </step5>
<formula> number of blackboards * one blackboard area </formula>
<calculation> 2 * 6 = 12 square meters </calculation>
<var> total_blackboards_area : 12 square meters </var>

<step6> Calculate the area of one door </step6>
<formula> door length * door width </formula>
<calculation> 2 * 1 = 2 square meters </calculation>
<var> one_door_area : 2 square meters </var>

<step7> Calculate the total area of all doors </step7>
<formula> number of doors * one door area </formula>
<calculation> 2 * 2 = 4 square meters </calculation>
<var> total_doors_area : 4 square meters </var>

<step8> Calculate the length that needs to be painted </step8>
<formula> total length - total windows area - total blackboards area - total doors area </formula>
<calculation> 34 - 6 - 12 - 4 = 12 meters </calculation>
<var> paintable_length : 12 meters </var>

<step9> Calculate the total cost of painting the classroom </step9>
<formula> paintable length * paint fee per square meter </formula>
<calculation> 12 * 4.8 = $57.6 </calculation>
<var> total_cost : $57.6 </var>
