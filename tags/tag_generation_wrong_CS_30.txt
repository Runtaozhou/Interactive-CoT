## 1. Problem statement

A van is delivering <fact1>180 bottles of drinks</fact1> to a neighborhood, and each bottle contains either cider or beer or a mixture of two. Out of the <fact1>180 bottles</fact1>, <fact2>40 contain only cider</fact2>, <fact3>80 contain only beer</fact3>, and the rest are a mixture of the two drinks. If the delivery man gives half the number of each bottle of drink to the first house, how many bottles does the first house get?

## 2. Facts

<fact1>Total bottles: 180</fact1>
<fact2>Bottles with only cider: 40</fact2>
<fact3>Bottles with only beer: 80</fact3>

## 3. Steps

<step1> Calculate the total number of bottles containing only cider or only beer </step1>
<formula> cider_bottles + beer_bottles </formula>
<calculation> 40 + 80 = 120 </calculation>
<var> single_drink_bottles : 120 </var>

<step2> Find the number of bottles containing a mixture of the two drinks </step2>
<formula> total_bottles - single_drink_bottles </formula>
<calculation> 180 - 120 = 60 </calculation>
<var> mixed_bottles : 60 </var>

<step3> Calculate the number of cider-only bottles given to the first house </step3>
<formula> cider_bottles × 1/2 </formula>
<calculation> 40 × 1/2 = 20 </calculation>
<var> first_house_cider : 20 </var>

<step4> Calculate the number of beer-only bottles given to the first house </step4>
<formula> beer_bottles × 1/2 </formula>
<calculation> 80 × 1/2 = 40 </calculation>
<var> first_house_beer : 40 </var>

<step5> Calculate the total number of single-drink bottles given to the first house </step5>
<formula> first_house_beer + first_house_cider </formula>
<calculation> 40 + 20 = 60 </calculation>
<var> first_house_single_drinks : 60 </var>

<step6> Calculate the number of mixed-drink bottles given to the first house </step6>
<formula> mixed_bottles × 1/2 </formula>
<calculation> 60 × 1/2 = 25 </calculation>
<var> first_house_mixed : 25 </var>

<step7> Calculate the total number of bottles given to the first house </step7>
<formula> first_house_single_drinks + first_house_mixed </formula>
<calculation> 60 + 25 = 85 </calculation>
<var> first_house_total : 85 </var>

## 5. Output

<output> 85 </output>
