## 1. Problem statement

An avant-garde sushi house sells jellyfish for a certain amount and eel for <fact1>nine times</fact1> that amount. If the combined cost of one order each kind of sushi is <fact2>$200</fact2>, how much does the eel cost?

## 2. Facts

<fact1>Eel cost: 9 times jellyfish cost</fact1>
<fact2>Combined cost: $200</fact2>
