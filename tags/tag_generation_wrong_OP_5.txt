## 1. Problem statement

Suzanne wants to raise money for charity by running a <fact1>5-kilometer race</fact1>. Her parents have pledged to donate <fact2>$10 for her first kilometer</fact2> and <fact3>double the donation for every successive kilometer</fact3>. If Suzanne finishes the race, how much money will her parents donate?

## 2. Facts

<fact1>Race distance: 5 kilometers</fact1>
<fact2>First kilometer donation: $10</fact2>
<fact3>Donation increase: double for each successive kilometer</fact3>

## 3. Steps

<step1> Calculate the donation for the 2nd kilometer </step1>
<formula> First kilometer donation ÷ 2 </formula>
<calculation> $10 / 2 = $5 </calculation>
<var> second_km_donation : $5 </var>

<step2> Calculate the donation for the 3rd kilometer </step2>
<formula> Second kilometer donation × 2 </formula>
<calculation> $5 * 2 = $10 </calculation>
<var> third_km_donation : $10 </var>

<step3> Calculate the donation for the 4th kilometer </step3>
<formula> Third kilometer donation × 2 </formula>
<calculation> $10 * 2 = $20 </calculation>
<var> fourth_km_donation : $20 </var>

<step4> Calculate the donation for the 5th kilometer </step4>
<formula> Fourth kilometer donation × 2 </formula>
<calculation> $20 * 2 = $40 </calculation>
<var> fifth_km_donation : $40 </var>

<step5> Sum all donations to find the total amount </step5>
<formula> First kilometer donation + second_km_donation + third_km_donation + fourth_km_donation + fifth_km_donation </formula>
<calculation> $10 + $5 + $10 + $20 + $40 = $85 </calculation>
<var> total_donation : $85 </var>

<wrongstep> 1 </wrongstep>

## 5. Output

<output> 85 </output>
