<fact1>Bridge weight limit: 100 kilograms</fact1>
<fact2>Kelly's weight: 34 kilograms</fact2>
<fact3>Kelly weighs 15% less than Megan</fact3>
<fact4>Mike weighs 5 kilograms more than Megan</fact4>
