## 1. Problem statement

A house's living room, dining room and kitchen take up <fact1>1,000 sq ft</fact1>. The house also has a guest bedroom and a master bedroom suite. If the guest bedroom is <fact2>a quarter of the size of the master bedroom suite</fact2>, and the house is <fact3>2,300 sq ft total</fact3>, how large is the master bedroom suite?

<fact1>Living room, dining room and kitchen area: 1,000 sq ft</fact1>
<fact2>Guest bedroom size: 0.25 of master bedroom suite</fact2>
<fact3>Total house area: 2,300 sq ft</fact3>
