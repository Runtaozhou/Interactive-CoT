## 1. Problem statement

<fact1>Dean's mother gave him $28 to go to the toy store.</fact1> Dean bought <fact2>6 toy cars</fact2> and <fact3>5 teddy bears</fact3>. Each <fact4>toy car cost $2</fact4> and each <fact5>teddy bear cost $1</fact5>. His mother then feels generous and decides to <fact6>give him an extra $10</fact6>. How much money does Dean have left?

## 2. Facts

<fact1>Initial money: $28</fact1>
<fact2>Number of toy cars: 6</fact2>
<fact3>Number of teddy bears: 5</fact3>
<fact4>Cost per toy car: $2</fact4>
<fact5>Cost per teddy bear: $1</fact5>
<fact6>Extra money: $10</fact6>

## 3. Steps

<step1>Calculate the cost of the teddy bears</step1>
<formula>Number of teddy bears × Cost per teddy bear</formula>
<calculation>5 bears × $1/bear = $5</calculation>
<var>teddy_bear_cost: $5</var>

<step2>Calculate the total money Dean has</step2>
<formula>Initial money + Extra money</formula>
<calculation>$28 + $10 = $38</calculation>
<var>total_money: $38</var>

<step3>Calculate how much money Dean has left</step3>
<formula>Total money - Teddy bear cost</formula>
<calculation>$38 - $5 = $33</calculation>
<var>money_left: $33</var>
