## 1. Problem statement

The bus driver drives <fact1>an average of 2 hours each day, 5 days a week</fact1>. From <fact2>Monday to Wednesday he drove at an average speed of 12 kilometers per hour</fact2>, and from <fact3>Thursday to Friday at an average speed of 9 kilometers per hour</fact3>. How many kilometers did the driver travel during these 5 days?

## 2. Facts

<fact1>Working hours: 2 hours each day, 5 days a week</fact1>
<fact2>Speed Monday to Wednesday: 12 kilometers per hour</fact2>
<fact3>Speed Thursday to Friday: 9 kilometers per hour</fact3>

## 3. Steps

<step1> Calculate the total working hours from Monday to Wednesday </step1>
<formula> number of days × hours per day </formula>
<calculation> 3 * 2 = 6 hours </calculation>
<var> mon_to_wed_hours : 6 hours </var>

<step2> Calculate the distance traveled from Monday to Wednesday </step2>
<formula> hours × speed </formula>
<calculation> 6 * 12 = 72 kilometers </calculation>
<var> mon_to_wed_distance : 72 kilometers </var>

<step3> Calculate the total working hours for Thursday and Friday </step3>
<formula> number of days × hours per day </formula>
<calculation> 2 * 2 = 4 hours </calculation>
<var> thu_to_fri_hours : 4 hours </var>

<step4> Calculate the distance traveled on Thursday and Friday </step4>
<formula> hours × speed </formula>
<calculation> 4 * 9 = 36 kilometers </calculation>
<var> thu_to_fri_distance : 36 kilometers </var>

<step5> Calculate the total distance traveled during the five days </step5>
<formula> mon_to_wed_distance + thu_to_fri_distance </formula>
<calculation> 72 + 36 = 108 kilometers </calculation>
<var> total_distance : 108 kilometers </var>
