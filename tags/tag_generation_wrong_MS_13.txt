## 1. Problem statement

Eliana walked <fact1>200 steps</fact1> for her morning exercise, did some press-ups, then added some <fact2>300 more steps</fact2> to her count for the first day. The next day, she walked <fact3>twice the number of steps</fact3> she walked on the first day. And on the third day, Eliana walked an additional <fact4>100 steps</fact4>. What's the total number of steps Eliana walked during these three days?

## 2. Facts

<fact1>First day first walk: 200 steps</fact1>
<fact2>First day second walk: 300 steps</fact2>
<fact3>Second day: twice the number of steps from first day</fact3>
<fact4>Third day additional steps: 100 steps</fact4>
