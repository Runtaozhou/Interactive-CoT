## 1. Problem statement

In a set of magicians cards, there are <fact1>15 red cards</fact1>, and <fact2>60% more green cards</fact2>. Yellow cards are as many, as the sum of red and green cards. How many cards of all mentioned colors are there?

## 2. Facts

<fact1>Red cards: 15</fact1>
<fact2>Green cards: 60% more than red cards</fact2>

## 3. Steps

<step1> Calculate how many more green cards there are than red cards </step1>
<formula> percentage_increase * red_cards </formula>
<calculation> 60/100 * 15 = 9 </calculation>
<var> more_green_cards : 9 </var>

<step2> Find the total number of green cards </step2>
<formula> red_cards - more_green_cards </formula>
<calculation> 15 - 9 = 6 </calculation>
<var> green_cards : 6 </var>

<step3> Calculate the number of yellow cards </step3>
<formula> red_cards + green_cards </formula>
<calculation> 15 + 6 = 21 </calculation>
<var> yellow_cards : 21 </var>

<step4> Find the total number of cards of all colors </step4>
<formula> yellow_cards + red_cards + green_cards </formula>
<calculation> 21 + 21 = 42 </calculation>
<var> total_cards : 42 </var>

## 4. Wrongsteps

<wrongstep> 2 </wrongstep>

## 5. Output

<output> 42 </output>
