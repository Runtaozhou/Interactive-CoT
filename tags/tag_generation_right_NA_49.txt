## 1. Problem statement

<fact1>Dexter has five pigs.</fact1> Each one eats <fact2>4 pounds of feed, twice a day.</fact2> If Dexter ordered <fact3>300 pounds of feed</fact3>, how many pounds of feed is left after <fact4>a week</fact4>?

## 2. Facts

<fact1>Number of pigs: 5</fact1>
<fact2>Feed per pig: 4 pounds, twice a day</fact2>
<fact3>Total feed ordered: 300 pounds</fact3>
<fact4>Time period: 1 week (7 days)</fact4>

## 3. Steps

<step1> Calculate how much feed one pig consumes in a day </step1>
<formula> pounds per feeding × number of feedings </formula>
<calculation> 4 × 2 = 8 pounds </calculation>
<var> daily_feed_per_pig : 8 pounds </var>

<step2> Calculate how much feed all pigs consume in a day </step2>
<formula> daily_feed_per_pig × number of pigs </formula>
<calculation> 8 × 5 = 40 pounds </calculation>
<var> daily_feed_total : 40 pounds </var>

<step3> Calculate how much feed all pigs consume in a week </step3>
<formula> daily_feed_total × days in a week </formula>
<calculation> 40 × 7 = 280 pounds </calculation>
<var> weekly_feed_total : 280 pounds </var>

<step4> Calculate how much feed is left after a week </step4>
<formula> total feed ordered - weekly_feed_total </formula>
<calculation> 300 - 280 = 20 pounds </calculation>
<var> feed_remaining : 20 pounds </var>

<wrongstep> 0 </wrongstep>

## 5. Output

<output> 20 </output>
