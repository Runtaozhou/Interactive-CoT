## 1. Problem statement

Tim spends <fact1>6 hours each day at work answering phones</fact1>. It takes him <fact2>15 minutes to deal with a call</fact2>. How many calls does he deal with during his <fact3>5 day work week</fact3>?

## 2. Facts

<fact1>Work hours per day: 6 hours</fact1>
<fact2>Time per call: 15 minutes</fact2>
<fact3>Work days per week: 5 days</fact3>

## 3. Steps

<step1> Convert hours to minutes to find total minutes per day </step1>
<formula> hours × 60 </formula>
<calculation> 6 * 60 = 360 minutes </calculation>
<var> minutes_per_day : 360 minutes </var>

<step2> Calculate the number of calls per day </step2>
<formula> minutes_per_day × time_per_call </formula>
<calculation> 360 * 15 = 5400 calls </calculation>
<var> calls_per_day : 5400 calls </var>

<step3> Calculate the total number of calls per week </step3>
<formula> calls_per_day × days_per_week </formula>
<calculation> 5 * 5400 = 27000 calls </calculation>
<var> calls_per_week : 27000 calls </var>
