## 1. Problem statement

The American swallow can lift into the air and carry a maximum of <fact1>5 pounds</fact1> of weight, while the European swallow can lift and carry <fact2>twice the weight</fact2> as the American swallow. If there was a flock of <fact3>90 swallows</fact3>, containing <fact4>twice as many American as European swallows</fact4>, what is the maximum combined weight the flock can carry?

## 2. Facts

<fact1>American swallow maximum weight: 5 pounds</fact1>
<fact2>European swallow capacity: 2 times American swallow</fact2>
<fact3>Total number of swallows: 90</fact3>
<fact4>Ratio of American to European swallows: 2 to 1</fact4>

## 3. Steps

<formula> x + 2x = 90 </formula>

<var> American_swallows : 2x </var>

<step8> Calculate the combined weight the flock can carry </step8>
<formula> total_weight = European_total + American_total </formula>
