## 1. Problem statement

<fact1>Eddie can bake 3 pies a day.</fact1> <fact2>His sister can bake 6 pies a day</fact2> while <fact3>his mother can bake 8 pies a day.</fact3> <fact4>How many pies will they be able to bake in 7 days?</fact4>

## 2. Facts

<fact1>Eddie's daily pie production: 3 pies</fact1>
<fact2>Sister's daily pie production: 6 pies</fact2>
<fact3>Mother's daily pie production: 8 pies</fact3>
<fact4>Number of days: 7</fact4>

## 3. Steps

<step1> Calculate how many pies Eddie can bake in 7 days </step1>
<formula> Eddie's daily production × number of days </formula>
<calculation> 3 × 7 = 21 pies </calculation>
<var> eddie_pies : 21 pies </var>

<step2> Calculate how many pies Eddie's sister can bake in 7 days </step2>
<formula> Sister's daily production × number of days </formula>
<calculation> 6 × 7 = 42 pies </calculation>
<var> sister_pies : 42 pies </var>

<step3> Calculate how many pies Eddie's mother can bake in 7 days </step3>
<formula> Mother's daily production × number of days </formula>
<calculation> 8 × 7 = 56 pies </calculation>
<var> mother_pies : 56 pies </var>

<step4> Add up all the pies to find the total </step4>
<formula> eddie_pies + sister_pies + mother_pies </formula>
<calculation> 3*7 + 42 + 56 = 119 pies </calculation>
<var> total_pies : 119 pies </var>
