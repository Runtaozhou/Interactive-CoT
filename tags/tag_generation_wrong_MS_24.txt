## 1. Problem statement

<fact1>Tracy, John and Jake found their combined weight to be 158 kilograms.</fact1> <fact2>Tracy weighs 52 kg</fact2> and <fact3>Jake weighs 8kg more than Tracy</fact3>, what is the range of their weights?

## 2. Facts

<fact1>Combined weight: 158 kilograms</fact1>
<fact2>Tracy's weight: 52 kg</fact2>
<fact3>Jake weighs 8 kg more than Tracy</fact3>

## 3. Steps

<step1> Calculate the combined weight of Tracy and Jake </step1>
<formula> Tracy's weight + Tracy's weight </formula>
<calculation> 52 + 52 = 104 kg </calculation>
<var> tracy_jake_weight : 104 kg </var>

<step2> Calculate John's weight by subtracting Tracy and Jake's combined weight from the total </step2>
<formula> Total weight - Tracy and Jake's weight </formula>
<calculation> 158 - 104 = 54 kg </calculation>
<var> john_weight : 54 kg </var>

<step3> Find the range by subtracting the smallest weight from the largest weight </step3>
<formula> Largest weight - Smallest weight </formula>
<calculation> 60 - 54 = 6 kg </calculation>
