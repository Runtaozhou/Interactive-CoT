## 1. Problem statement

## 2. Facts

<fact1>Cupcakes per batch: 65</fact1>
<fact2>Number of batches: 45</fact2>
<fact3>Cupcakes taken from each batch: 5</fact3>
<fact4>Number of friends: 19</fact4>
