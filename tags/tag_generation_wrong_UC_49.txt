## 1. Problem statement

It takes a dog groomer <fact1>10 minutes to dry a short-haired dog like a beagle</fact1>. It takes him <fact2>twice as long to dry a full-haired, fluffy dog like a german shepherd</fact2>. One morning, he has <fact3>6 short-haired dogs to dry</fact3> and <fact4>9 full-haired dogs to dry</fact4>. How many hours does it take him to dry all of the dogs?

## 2. Facts

<fact1>Short-haired dog drying time: 10 minutes</fact1>
<fact2>Full-haired dog drying time: twice as long as short-haired</fact2>
<fact3>Number of short-haired dogs: 6</fact3>
<fact4>Number of full-haired dogs: 9</fact4>

## 3. Steps

<step1> Calculate the time needed to dry a full-haired dog </step1>
<formula> short_hair_time × 2 </formula>
<calculation> 10 * 2 = 20 minutes </calculation>
<var> full_hair_time : 20 minutes </var>

<step2> Calculate the total time needed for all short-haired dogs </step2>
<formula> number_of_short_hair_dogs × short_hair_time </formula>
<calculation> 6 * 10 = 60 minutes </calculation>
<var> total_short_hair_time : 60 minutes </var>

<step3> Calculate the total time needed for all full-haired dogs </step3>
<formula> number_of_full_hair_dogs × full_hair_time </formula>
<calculation> 9 * 20 = 180 minutes </calculation>
<var> total_full_hair_time : 180 minutes </var>

<step4> Calculate the total time needed for all dogs </step4>
<formula> total_short_hair_time + total_full_hair_time </formula>
<calculation> 60 + 180 = 240 minutes </calculation>
<var> total_minutes : 240 minutes </var>

<step5> Convert the total time from minutes to hours </step5>
<formula> total_minutes ÷ minutes_per_hour </formula>
<calculation> 240 / 50 = 4.8 hours </calculation>
<var> total_hours : 4.8 hours </var>

<output> 4.8 </output>
