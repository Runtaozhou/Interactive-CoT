## 1. Problem statement

A small theater company sells tickets to a show. They have a <fact1>400 seat theater</fact1> and fill to <fact2>80% capacity</fact2>. Each ticket cost <fact3>$30</fact3>. They repeated the same performance <fact4>2 other days</fact4>. How much did they make?

## 2. Facts

<fact1>Theater capacity: 400 seats</fact1>
<fact2>Fill rate: 80%</fact2>
<fact3>Ticket price: $30</fact3>
<fact4>Additional performance days: 2</fact4>

## 3. Steps

<step1> Calculate the daily revenue from ticket sales </step1>
<formula> theater capacity × ticket price </formula>
<calculation> 400 * 30 = $12,000 </calculation>
<var> daily_revenue : $12,000 </var>

<step2> Calculate the total number of days of performance </step2>
<formula> additional days + 1 </formula>
<calculation> 2 + 1 = 3 days </calculation>
<var> total_days : 3 days </var>

<step3> Calculate the total revenue from all performances </step3>
<formula> daily_revenue × total_days </formula>
<calculation> 12000 * 3 = $36,000 </calculation>
<var> total_revenue : $36,000 </var>

## 4. Wrongsteps

<wrongstep> 1 </wrongstep>

## 5. Output

<output> 36,000 </output>
