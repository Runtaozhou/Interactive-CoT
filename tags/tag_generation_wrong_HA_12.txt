## 1. Problem statement

<fact1>Elmer has a pond that initially contained 2400 pollywogs</fact1>, but these pollywogs matured into toads and left the pond at a constant rate of <fact2>50 pollywogs per day</fact2>. In addition, Elmer has a son named Melvin, who loves to catch pollywogs, remove them from the pond, and release them into a nearby stream. For <fact3>the first 20 days</fact3>, Melvin caught <fact4>10 pollywogs per day</fact4> and released them into the stream. How many days did it take for all of the pollywogs to disappear from the pond?

## 2. Facts

<fact1>Initial pollywogs: 2400</fact1>
<fact2>Natural departure rate: 50 pollywogs per day</fact2>
<fact3>Duration of Melvin's catching: 20 days</fact3>
<fact4>Melvin's catching rate: 10 pollywogs per day</fact4>
