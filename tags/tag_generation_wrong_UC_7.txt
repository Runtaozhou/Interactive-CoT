<fact1>Road trip duration: 14 hours</fact1>
<fact2>Stops for stretching: every 2 hours</fact2>
<fact3>Additional food stops: 2</fact3>
<fact4>Additional gas stops: 3</fact4>
<fact5>Duration of each pit stop: 20 minutes</fact5>
