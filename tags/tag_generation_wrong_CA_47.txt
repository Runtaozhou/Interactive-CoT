## 1. Problem statement

<fact1>Joshua collected 80 rocks</fact1> while Jose collected <fact2>14 fewer rocks</fact2>. Albert has collected <fact3>20 more rocks than Jose</fact3>. How many more rocks did Albert collect than Joshua?

## 2. Facts

<fact1>Joshua's rocks: 80 rocks</fact1>
<fact2>Jose collected fewer than Joshua: 14 rocks</fact2>
<fact3>Albert collected more than Jose: 20 rocks</fact3>

## 3. Steps

<step1> Calculate how many rocks Jose collected </step1>
<formula> Joshua's rocks - difference </formula>
<calculation> 80 - 14 = 66 rocks </calculation>
<var> jose_rocks : 66 rocks </var>

<step2> Calculate how many rocks Albert collected </step2>
<formula> Jose's rocks + difference </formula>
<calculation> 66 + 20 = 86 rocks </calculation>
<var> albert_rocks : 86 rocks </var>

<step3> Calculate how many more rocks Albert collected than Joshua </step3>
<formula> Albert's rocks - Joshua's rocks </formula>
<calculation> 86 - 80 = 7 rocks </calculation>
<var> difference_albert_joshua : 7 rocks </var>
