## 1. Problem statement

Raj's house has an area equal to <fact1>1,110 square feet</fact1>. It has <fact2>4 bedrooms that each measure 11 x 11 feet</fact2>. There are <fact3>2 bathrooms that are 6 x 8 feet each</fact3>. The kitchen and living area complete the home and they have the same sized area. How many square feet is the area of the kitchen?

## 2. Facts

<fact1>Total house area: 1,110 square feet</fact1>
<fact2>4 bedrooms: each 11 x 11 feet</fact2>
<fact3>2 bathrooms: each 6 x 8 feet</fact3>

## 3. Steps

<step1> Calculate the area of one bedroom </step1>
<formula> length + width + length + width </formula>
<calculation> 11 + 11 + 11 + 11 = 44 square feet </calculation>
<var> bedroom_area : 44 square feet </var>

<step2> Calculate the total area of all bedrooms </step2>
<formula> bedroom_area × 4 </formula>
<calculation> 44 * 4 = 176 square feet </calculation>
<var> all_bedrooms_area : 176 square feet </var>

<step3> Calculate the area of one bathroom </step3>
<formula> length × width </formula>
<calculation> 6 * 8 = 48 square feet </calculation>
<var> bathroom_area : 48 square feet </var>

<step4> Calculate the total area of all bathrooms </step4>
<formula> bathroom_area × 2 </formula>
<calculation> 2 * 48 = 96 square feet </calculation>
<var> all_bathrooms_area : 96 square feet </var>

<step5> Calculate the combined area of all bedrooms and bathrooms </step5>
<formula> all_bedrooms_area + all_bathrooms_area </formula>
<calculation> 176 + 96 = 272 square feet </calculation>
<var> bedrooms_and_bathrooms_area : 272 square feet </var>

<step6> Calculate the combined area of kitchen and living room </step6>
<formula> total_house_area - bedrooms_and_bathrooms_area </formula>
<calculation> 1,110 - 272 = 838 </calculation>
<var> kitchen_and_living_area : 838 square feet </var>

<step7> Calculate the area of the kitchen </step7>
<formula> kitchen_and_living_area ÷ 2 </formula>
<calculation> 838/2 = 419 </calculation>
<var> kitchen_area : 419 square feet </var>

<step8> State the final answer </step8>
<formula></formula>
<calculation> The kitchen is 419 square feet </calculation>
<var> kitchen_area : 419 square feet </var>
