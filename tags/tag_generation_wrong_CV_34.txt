## 1. Problem statement

<fact1>Three siblings are born 5 years apart, each.</fact1> If <fact2>the eldest child is 20 years old now</fact2>, what's the total of the ages of the three siblings <fact3>10 years from now</fact3>?

## 2. Facts

<fact1>Age gap between siblings: 5 years</fact1>
<fact2>Eldest child's current age: 20 years old</fact2>
<fact3>Time period: 10 years from now</fact3>

## 3. Steps

<step1> Calculate the eldest child's age 10 years from now </step1>
<formula> current_age + time_period </formula>
<calculation> 20 + 10 = 30 years old </calculation>
<var> eldest_future_age : 30 years old </var>

<step2> Calculate the second child's current age </step2>
<formula> eldest_current_age - age_gap </formula>
<calculation> 25 - 5 = 20 years old </calculation>
<var> second_current_age : 20 years old </var>

<step3> Calculate the second child's age 10 years from now </step3>
<formula> second_current_age + time_period </formula>
<calculation> 20 + 10 = 30 years old </calculation>
<var> second_future_age : 30 years old </var>

<step4> Calculate the total age of the first and second-born 10 years from now </step4>
<formula> eldest_future_age + second_future_age </formula>
<calculation> 30 + 30 = 60 </calculation>
<var> first_two_future_ages : 60 years </var>

<step5> Calculate the third child's current age </step5>
<formula> second_current_age - age_gap </formula>
<calculation> 20 - 5 = 15 years old </calculation>
<var> third_current_age : 15 years old </var>

<step6> Calculate the third child's age 10 years from now </step6>
<formula> third_current_age + time_period </formula>
<calculation> 15 + 10 = 25 years old </calculation>
<var> third_future_age : 25 years old </var>

<step7> Calculate the total age of all three siblings 10 years from now </step7>
<formula> first_two_future_ages + third_future_age </formula>
<calculation> 60 + 25 = 85 years </calculation>
<var> total_future_age : 85 years </var>
