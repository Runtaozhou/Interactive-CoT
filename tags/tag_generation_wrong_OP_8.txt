## 1. Problem statement

<fact1>Steve's new book sells 1,000,000 copies.</fact1> He got an advance to pay for <fact2>100,000 copies.</fact2> He gets <fact3>$2 for each copy</fact3> of the book sold. His agent takes <fact4>10% of that.</fact4> How much money did he keep not counting the money from the advance?

## 2. Facts

<fact1>Book sales: 1,000,000 copies</fact1>
<fact2>Advance covers: 100,000 copies</fact2>
<fact3>Steve's payment per copy: $2</fact3>
<fact4>Agent's commission: 10%</fact4>

## 3. Steps

<step1> Calculate the total number of copies by adding the advance copies to the sales </step1>
<formula> sales + advance copies </formula>
<calculation> 1,000,000 + 100,000 = 1,100,000 </calculation>
<var> total_copies : 1,100,000 </var>

<step2> Calculate the total money made from selling the books </step2>
<formula> total_copies × payment per copy </formula>
<calculation> 1,100,000 * 2 = $2,200,000 </calculation>
<var> total_money : $2,200,000 </var>

<step3> Calculate how much the agent took </step3>
<formula> total_money × agent's commission </formula>
<calculation> 2,200,000 * 0.1 = $220,000 </calculation>
<var> agent_commission : $220,000 </var>

<step4> Calculate how much Steve kept after the agent's commission </step4>
<formula> total_money - agent_commission </formula>
<calculation> 2,200,000 - 220,000 = $1,980,000 </calculation>
<var> steve_kept : $1,980,000 </var>
