## 1. Problem statement

Emily wants to know how much it rained last week. She sees that <fact1>it rained 2 inches on Monday morning and 1 more inch later that day</fact1>. <fact2>It rained twice that much on Tuesday</fact2>. <fact3>It did not rain on Wednesday but on Thursday it rained 1 inch</fact3>. <fact4>The biggest storm was on Friday when the total was equal to Monday through Thursday combined</fact4>. What was the daily average rain total for the week?

## 2. Facts

<fact1>Monday rain: 2 inches in morning, 1 more inch later</fact1>
<fact2>Tuesday rain: twice Monday's amount</fact2>
<fact3>Wednesday rain: 0 inches, Thursday rain: 1 inch</fact3>
<fact4>Friday rain: equal to Monday through Thursday combined</fact4>

## 3. Steps

<step1> Calculate the total rain on Monday </step1>
<formula> morning rain + afternoon rain </formula>
<calculation> 2 inches + 1 inches = 3 inches </calculation>
<var> monday_rain : 3 inches </var>

<step2> Calculate the total rain on Tuesday </step2>

<formula> wednesday_rain + thursday_rain </formula>

<var> wed_thurs_rain : 1 inch </var>

<step4> Calculate the total rain on Friday </step4>
<formula> monday_rain + tuesday_rain + wed_thurs_rain </formula>
<calculation> 3 inches + 6 inches + 1 inch = 10 inches </calculation>
<var> friday_rain : 10 inches </var>

<step5> Calculate the total rain for the week </step5>
<formula> monday_rain + tuesday_rain + wed_thurs_rain + friday_rain </formula>
<calculation> 3 inches + 6 inches + 1 inch + 10 inches = 20 inches </calculation>
<var> total_rain : 20 inches </var>

<calculation> 20 inches/4 days = 5 inches </calculation>
