## 1. Problem statement

Micah bought envelopes to send, and depending on the weight of the envelope he will need more stamps. If an envelope weighs more than <fact1>5 pounds</fact1>, he will need <fact2>5 stamps</fact2>. If it weighs less than that, it will only need <fact3>2 stamps</fact3>. If he bought <fact4>52 stamps</fact4> with <fact5>6 envelopes</fact5> that weigh less than 5 pounds, how many envelopes in total did Micah need to buy?

## 2. Facts

<fact1>Weight threshold: 5 pounds</fact1>
<fact2>Stamps needed for heavy envelopes: 5 stamps</fact2>
<fact3>Stamps needed for light envelopes: 2 stamps</fact3>
<fact4>Total stamps bought: 52 stamps</fact4>
<fact5>Number of light envelopes: 6 envelopes</fact5>

## 3. Steps

<step1> Calculate the number of stamps needed for the light envelopes </step1>
<formula> light_envelopes ร stamps_per_light_envelope </formula>
<calculation> 6 * 2 = 12 stamps </calculation>
<var> light_envelope_stamps : 12 stamps </var>

<step2> Find the remaining stamps after accounting for light envelopes </step2>
<formula> total_stamps - light_envelope_stamps </formula>
<calculation> 52 - 12 = 40 stamps </calculation>
<var> remaining_stamps : 40 stamps </var>

<step3> Account for international envelope stamps and calculate heavy envelopes </step3>
<formula> (remaining_stamps - international_stamps) รท stamps_per_heavy_envelope </formula>
<calculation> (40 - 10) / 5 = 6 envelopes </calculation>
<var> international_stamps : 10 stamps </var>
<var> heavy_envelopes : 6 envelopes </var>

<step4> Calculate the total number of envelopes </step4>
<formula> light_envelopes + heavy_envelopes </formula>
<calculation> 6 + 6 = 12 envelopes </calculation>
<var> total_envelopes : 12 envelopes </var>
