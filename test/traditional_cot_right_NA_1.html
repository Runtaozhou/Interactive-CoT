Problem Statement:

It takes 1 hour for refrigerated dough to come to room temperature. It takes 15 minutes to shape the dough and 2 hours to proof. The bread takes 30 minutes to bake and 15 minutes to cool. If the bakery opens at 6:00 am, what is the latest time the head baker can make it to the store to start working?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: First, we need to determine how long it takes for the dough to reach room temperature and complete proofing. These are the initial preparation steps that must be completed before baking can begin.
Time for dough to reach room temperature: 1 hour
Proofing time: 2 hours
Total initial preparation time: 1 + 2 = 3 hours
Step 2: Next, we calculate the time needed for the final preparation steps: shaping the dough, baking the bread, and cooling it before the bakery opens.
Shaping time: 15 minutes
Baking time: 30 minutes
Cooling time: 15 minutes
Total final preparation time: 15 + 30 + 15 = 60 minutes
Step 3: To make our calculations easier, let's convert the final preparation time from minutes to hours.
60 minutes รท 60 = 1 hour
Step 4: Now we can calculate the total preparation time by adding the initial and final preparation times.
Total preparation time: 3 hours + 1 hour = 4 hours
Step 5: Since the bakery opens at 6:00 am and we need 4 hours of preparation time, we can determine the latest time the baker can arrive.
Latest arrival time: 6:00 am - 4 hours = 2:00 am

Final Answer:

2:00 am
