Problem Statement

John raises emu. He has 4 pens for emu and each pen has 6 emus in it. Each female emu lays 1 egg per day. If half the emu are females how many eggs does he get a week?

Problem Summary
  • Number of pens: 4
  • Number of emus per pen: 6
  • Eggs per female emu per day: 1
  • Proportion of female emus: 1/2
Goal: Calculate the total number of eggs John gets in a week
