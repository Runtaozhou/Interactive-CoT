Problem Statement

The teacher decided to rewards his students with extra recess on report card day if they got good grades. Students normally get 20 minutes for recess. He told the students that every A got them 2 extra minutes of recess. Every B got them one extra minute. Every C got them zero extra minutes, but every D got them 1 less minute. When report cards came out there were 10 As, 12 Bs, 14 Cs, and 5 Ds. In total, how much recess would the students get that day?

Problem Summary
  • Normal recess time: 20 minutes
  • Extra minutes per A: 2 minutes
  • Extra minutes per B: 1 minute
  • Extra minutes per C: 0 minutes
  • Minutes lost per D: 1 minute
  • Number of As: 10
  • Number of Bs: 12
  • Number of Cs: 14
  • Number of Ds: 5
Goal: Calculate the total recess time for the students that day
