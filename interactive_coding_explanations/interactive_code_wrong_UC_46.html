Problem Statement

Jeremy is driving 600 miles to visit his parents. His average speed for this trip is 50 miles per hour. Every two hours of driving, he makes a rest stop for 15 minutes. His car gets 18 miles per gallon of gas, and his gas indicator comes on when he's used 15 gallons, at which point he stops to refill, which takes 10 minutes. How long, in minutes, does he take to get to his parents' house, if he starts out with a full tank of gas?

Problem Summary
  • Distance: 600 miles
  • Average speed: 50 miles per hour
  • Rest stop: every 2 hours for 15 minutes
  • Fuel efficiency: 18 miles per gallon
  • Refill indicator: after 15 gallons
  • Refill time: 10 minutes

What we need to find

How long, in minutes, does Jeremy take to get to his parents' house.

Python Solution
1 # Calculate the total driving time in minutes
2 driving_time = 600 / 50 * 50
3 # Calculate the number of rest stops
4 rest_stops = 600 / 50 / 2
5 # Calculate the total time spent on rest stops
6 rest_time = rest_stops * 15
7 # Calculate the distance between refuels
8 refuel_distance = 18 * 15
9 # Calculate the number of refuels needed
10 refuels = 600 / refuel_distance
11 # Calculate the total time spent refueling
12 refuel_time = refuels * 10
13 # Calculate the total trip time
14 total_time = driving_time + rest_time + refuel_time
