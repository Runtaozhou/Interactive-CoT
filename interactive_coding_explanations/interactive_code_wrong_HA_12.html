Problem Statement

Elmer has a pond that initially contained 2400 pollywogs, but these pollywogs matured into toads and left the pond at a constant rate of 50 pollywogs per day. In addition, Elmer has a son named Melvin, who loves to catch pollywogs, remove them from the pond, and release them into a nearby stream. For the first 20 days, Melvin caught 10 pollywogs per day and released them into the stream. How many days did it take for all of the pollywogs to disappear from the pond?

Problem Summary
  • Initial pollywogs: 2400
  • Natural disappearance rate: 50 pollywogs per day
  • Duration of Melvin's catching: 20 days
  • Melvin's catching rate: 10 pollywogs per day

What we need to find:

How many days it took for all of the pollywogs to disappear from the pond.

Python Solution
1 # Calculate combined rate of disappearance for first 20 days
2 combined_rate = 50 + 10
3 # Calculate pollywogs removed in first 20 days
4 pollywogs_removed_20_days = combined_rate * 20
5 # Calculate pollywogs remaining after 20 days

8 new_combined_rate = 50 + 5

10 additional_days = pollywogs_remaining / new_combined_rate
11 # Calculate total days for all pollywogs to disappear
12 total_days = 20 + additional_days
