Problem Statement

It takes 1 hour for refrigerated dough to come to room temperature. It takes 15 minutes to shape the dough and 2 hours to proof. The bread takes 30 minutes to bake and 15 minutes to cool. If the bakery opens at 6:00 am, what is the latest time the head baker can make it to the store to start working?

Problem Summary
  • Time for dough to reach room temperature: 1 hour
  • Time to shape dough: 15 minutes
  • Proofing time: 2 hours
  • Baking time: 30 minutes
  • Cooling time: 15 minutes
  • Bakery opening time: 6:00 am

What we need to find:

The latest time the head baker can arrive at the store to start working.

Python Solution
1 # Calculate the time needed for dough to reach room temperature and proof
2 initial_prep_time = 1 + 2
3 # Calculate the total time for shaping, baking, and cooling
4 final_prep_time_minutes = 15 + 30 + 15
5 # Convert the final preparation time from minutes to hours
6 final_prep_time_hours = final_prep_time_minutes / 60
7 # Calculate the total preparation time
8 total_prep_time = initial_prep_time + final_prep_time_hours
9 # Calculate the latest arrival time for the baker
10 latest_arrival_time = 6 - total_prep_time
