Problem Statement

Leo has to write a 400-word story for his literature class. 10 words fit on each line of his notebook 20 lines fit on each page. Lucas has filled one and a half pages. How many words does he have left to write?

Problem Summary
  • Total words required: 400
  • Words per line: 10
  • Lines per page: 20
  • Pages filled: 1.5

What we need to find

How many words Lucas has left to write.

Python Solution
1 # Calculate how many lines are in half a page
2 lines_in_half_page = 20 / 2
3 # Calculate total lines written
4 total_lines_written = 20 + lines_in_half_page
5 # Calculate words written so far
6 words_written = total_lines_written * 10
7 # Calculate words left to write
8 words_left = 400 - words_written
