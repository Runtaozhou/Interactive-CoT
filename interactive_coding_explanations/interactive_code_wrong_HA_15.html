Problem Statement

Jim charges $20 for fixing bike tires It costs him $5 in parts for tire repairs In one month Jim does 300 of these repairs He also does 2 more complex repairs Complex repairs cost $300 each Complex repairs cost $50 in parts He also sells $2000 profit worth of things from his retail shop Rent and other fixed expense for the shop is $4000 a month

Problem Summary
  • Tire repair charge: $20
  • Tire repair parts cost: $5
  • Number of tire repairs: 300
  • Number of complex repairs: 2
  • Complex repair charge: $300
  • Complex repair parts cost: $50
  • Retail profit: $2000
  • Monthly fixed expenses: $4000

What we need to find

We need to calculate Jim's total profit for the month after all expenses.

Python Solution
1 # Calculate profit per tire repair
2 profit_per_tire = 20 - 5
3 # Calculate total profit from tire repairs
4 total_tire_profit = profit_per_tire * 300
5 # Calculate profit per complex repair
6 profit_per_complex = 300 - 50
7 # Calculate total profit from complex repairs
8 total_complex_profit = profit_per_complex * 2
9 # Calculate total profit before fixed expenses (including a $300 fine)
10 total_profit_before_expenses = total_tire_profit + total_complex_profit + 2000 - 300
11 # Calculate net profit after fixed expenses
12 net_profit = total_profit_before_expenses - 4000
