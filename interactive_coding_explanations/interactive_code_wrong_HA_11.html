Problem Statement

Jack has $43 in his piggy bank. He also gets an allowance of $10 a week. Jack puts half of his allowance into his piggy bank every week. After 8 weeks how much will Jack have in his piggy bank?

Problem Summary
  • Starting amount: $43
  • Weekly allowance: $10
  • Fraction saved: 1/2
  • Number of weeks: 8

What we need to find:

How much money Jack will have in his piggy bank after 8 weeks.

2 weekly_savings_from_allowance = 10 / 2

6 total_weekly_savings = weekly_savings_from_allowance + weekly_sofa_money
