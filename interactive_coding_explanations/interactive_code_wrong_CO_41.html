Problem Statement

On the weekend, Tony will walk to the store. On weekdays, he runs to the store. When he walks, he goes 2 MPH. When he runs he goes 10 MPH. The store is 4 miles away. If he goes on Sunday, Tuesday, and Thursday, what is the average time in minutes that he spends to get to the store?

Problem Summary
  • Walking speed: 2 MPH
  • Running speed: 10 MPH
  • Distance to store: 4 miles

What we need to find:

The average time in minutes that Tony spends to get to the store when he goes on Sunday, Tuesday, and Thursday.

Python Solution
1 # Calculate time to get to store on Sunday (walking)
2 sunday_time_hours = 4 / 2
3 # Convert Sunday time from hours to minutes
4 sunday_time_minutes = sunday_time_hours * 60
5 # Calculate time to get to store on Tuesday and Thursday (running)
6 weekday_time_hours = 4 / 10
7 # Convert weekday time from hours to minutes
8 weekday_time_minutes = weekday_time_hours * 60
9 # Calculate total time for all three days
10 total_time_minutes = sunday_time_minutes + weekday_time_minutes + 48
11 # Calculate average time
12 average_time_minutes = total_time_minutes / 3
