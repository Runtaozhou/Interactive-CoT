Problem Statement

Baking in batches of 65 cupcakes, Carla made 45 batches of cupcakes for her daughter's birthday party. She then took 5 cupcakes from each batch, and fed them to her dogs. If Carla's daughter had 19 friends and they shared the remaining cupcakes equally among them, including the daughter, calculate the number of cupcakes that each of Carla's daughter's friends ate.

Problem Summary
  • Cupcakes per batch: 65
  • Number of batches: 45
  • Cupcakes taken from each batch: 5
  • Number of friends: 19

What we need to find:

Calculate the number of cupcakes that each of Carla's daughter's friends ate.

Python Solution
1 # Calculate cupcakes per batch after sampling
2 cupcakes_per_batch_after_sampling = 65 - 5
3 # Calculate total cupcakes after removing samples
4 total_cupcakes = 50 * cupcakes_per_batch_after_sampling
5 # Calculate total people at the party
6 total_people = 19 + 1
7 # Calculate cupcakes per person
8 cupcakes_per_person = total_cupcakes / total_people
