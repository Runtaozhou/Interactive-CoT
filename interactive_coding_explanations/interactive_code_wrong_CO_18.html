Problem Statement

Suzie and 5 of her friends decide to rent an Airbnb at Lake Tahoe for 4 days from Thursday to Sunday. The rental rate for weekdays is $420 per day. The weekend rental rate is $540 per day. They all decide to split the rental evenly. How much does each person have to pay?

Problem Summary
  • Number of people: 6 (Suzie and 5 friends)
  • Weekday rate: $420 per day
  • Weekend rate: $540 per day
  • Rental duration: 4 days (Thursday to Sunday)

What we need to find:

How much each person has to pay for the Airbnb rental.

Python Solution
1 # Calculate cost for weekdays (Thursday and Friday)
2 weekday_cost = 420 * 2
3 # Calculate cost for weekend days (Saturday and Sunday)
4 weekend_cost = 540 * 1
5 # Calculate total cost of the rental
6 total_cost = weekday_cost + weekend_cost
7 # Calculate cost per person
8 cost_per_person = total_cost / 6
