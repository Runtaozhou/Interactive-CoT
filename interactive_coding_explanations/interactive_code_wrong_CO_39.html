Problem Statement

Danivan Drugstore has 4500 bottles of hand sanitizer gel in inventory at the beginning of the week. On Monday 2445 bottles were sold, on Tuesday 900 bottles were sold, and 50 bottles each day for the rest of the week were sold (from Wednesday until Sunday). On Saturday, the supplier delivers an order for 650 bottles. How many bottles of sanitizer gel does the Drugstore have at the end of the week?

Problem Summary
  • Initial inventory: 4500 bottles
  • Monday sales: 2445 bottles
  • Tuesday sales: 900 bottles
  • Daily sales Wednesday-Sunday: 50 bottles
  • Saturday delivery: 650 bottles

What we need to find:

How many bottles of sanitizer gel the Drugstore has at the end of the week.

Python Solution
1 # Calculate sales on Monday and Tuesday
2 mon_tue_sales = 2445 + 900
3 # Calculate sales from Wednesday to Sunday
4 wed_sun_sales = 4 * 50
5 # Calculate total sales for the week
6 total_sales = mon_tue_sales + wed_sun_sales
7 # Calculate remaining stock after sales
8 remaining_stock = 4500 - total_sales
9 # Calculate final stock after Saturday delivery
10 final_stock = remaining_stock + 650
