Problem Statement

Tobias went to a swimming pool for 3 hours. Swimming every 100 meters took him 5 minutes, but every 25 minutes he had to take a 5-minute pause. How many meters did Tobias swim during his visit to the swimming pool?

Problem Summary
  • Swimming pool visit duration: 3 hours
  • Swimming time per 100 meters: 5 minutes
  • Pause duration: 5 minutes every 25 minutes of swimming

What we need to find:

How many meters Tobias swam during his visit to the swimming pool.

Python Solution
1 # Calculate the duration of one sequence (swimming + pause)
2 sequence_duration = 25 + 5 # 25 minutes swimming + 5 minutes pause = 30 minutes/sequence
3 # Calculate total minutes and number of sequences
4 total_minutes = 3 * 50 # 3 hours * 50 minutes/hour = 150 minutes
5 number_of_sequences = total_minutes / sequence_duration # 150 minutes / 30 minutes/sequence = 5 sequences
6 # Calculate total pause time
7 total_pause_time = number_of_sequences * 5 # 5 pauses * 5 minutes/pause = 25 minutes
8 # Calculate actual swimming time
9 swimming_time = total_minutes - total_pause_time # 150 minutes - 25 minutes = 125 minutes
10 # Calculate number of 100m segments Tobias can swim
11 number_of_100m_segments = swimming_time / 5 # 125 minutes / 5 minutes/100 meters = 25 times
12 # Calculate total distance swam
13 total_distance = number_of_100m_segments * 100 # 25 times * 100 meters/time = 2500 meters
