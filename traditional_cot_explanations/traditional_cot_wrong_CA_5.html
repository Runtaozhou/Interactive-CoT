Problem Statement:

Jackie can do 5 push-ups in 10 seconds. How many push-ups can she do in one minute if she takes two 8-second breaks?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the total time for Jackie's push-up routine.

One minute equals 60 seconds.

Total time = 1 × 60 = 60 seconds

Step 2: Calculate the total time spent on breaks.

Jackie takes two 8-second breaks.

Break time = 2 × 8 = 20 seconds

Step 3: Calculate the time available for doing push-ups.

We subtract the break time from the total time.

Push-up time = 60 - 20 = 40 seconds

Step 4: Calculate the time needed for one push-up.

Jackie can do 5 push-ups in 10 seconds.

Time per push-up = 10 / 5 = 2 seconds

Step 5: Calculate the total number of push-ups Jackie can do.

We divide the available push-up time by the time needed for one push-up.

Total push-ups = 40 / 2 = 20 push-ups

Final Answer:

20
