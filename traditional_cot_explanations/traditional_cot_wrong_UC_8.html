We need to add the time for the dough to reach room temperature (1 hour) and the proofing time (2 hours):

1 hour + 2 hours = 3 hours

We need to add the time for shaping (15 minutes), baking (30 minutes), and cooling (15 minutes):

15 minutes + 30 minutes + 15 minutes = 60 minutes
