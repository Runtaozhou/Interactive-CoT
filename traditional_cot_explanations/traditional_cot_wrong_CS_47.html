Problem Statement:

Miss Molly surveyed her class of 30 students about their favorite color. Half of the class answered green, one-third of the girls answered pink, and the rest of the class answered yellow. If there are 18 girls in the class, how many students like yellow best?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate how many students answered green.

Since half of the class chose green as their favorite color:

Number of students who like green = 30 × 1/2 = 15 students

Step 2: Calculate how many girls answered pink.

One-third of the girls chose pink as their favorite color:

Number of girls who like pink = 18 × 1/3 = 6 girls

Step 3: Add up students who answered green or pink.

Total number of students who like either green or pink = 15 + 6 = 21 students

Step 4: Find how many students answered yellow by subtracting from total.

Number of students who like yellow = Total students - (Green + Pink)

Number of students who like yellow = 30 - 20 = 10 students

Final Answer:

10
