Problem Statement:

Timmy's parents have a 2 car garage, which has both cars inside it. Also inside is a riding lawnmower, a bicycle for Timmy as well as each of his parents, a tricycle for Timmy's little brother Joey, and a unicycle that Timmy's dad practices riding on. How many wheels in total are in this garage?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the total number of wheels from the cars.

Each car has 4 wheels, and there are 2 cars in the garage.

Number of wheels from cars = 4 × 2 = 8 wheels

Step 2: Add the wheels from the riding lawnmower.

A riding lawnmower typically has 4 wheels.

Total wheels so far = 8 + 4 = 12 wheels

Step 3: Add the wheels from all the bicycles.

There are 3 bicycles (one for Timmy and one for each of his parents), and each bicycle has 2 wheels.

Number of wheels from bicycles = 3 × 2 = 6 wheels

Total wheels so far = 12 + 6 = 18 wheels

Step 4: Add the wheels from the tricycle and unicycle.

A tricycle has 3 wheels, and a unicycle has 1 wheel.

Total wheels = 18 + 3 + 1 = 20 wheels

Final Answer:

20
