Problem Statement:

Mike watches TV for 4 hours every day. On the days he plays video games he plays for half as long as he watches TV. If he plays video games 3 days a week how long does he spend watching TV and playing video games?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate how many hours Mike watches TV in a week.

We know Mike watches TV for 4 hours every day, so we multiply this by the number of days in a week:

Daily TV hours × Days in a week = 4 × 7 = 28 hours

Mike watches TV for 28 hours per week.

Step 2: Calculate how many hours Mike plays video games in a week.

We know Mike plays video games 3 days a week, and on those days he plays for the same amount of time as his daily TV watching:

Video game days × Daily TV hours = 3 × 4 = 12 hours

Mike plays video games for 12 hours per week.

Step 3: Calculate the total time spent on both activities.

Now we add the weekly TV hours and the weekly video game hours:

Weekly game hours + Weekly TV hours = 12 + 28 = 40 hours

Mike spends a total of 40 hours per week watching TV and playing video games.

Final Answer:

40
