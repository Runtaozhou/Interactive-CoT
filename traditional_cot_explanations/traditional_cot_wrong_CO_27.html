Problem Statement:

Lisa has 36 candies. On Mondays and Wednesdays, she eats 2 candies for each day and on the other days of the week she eats 1 candy for each day. How many weeks does it take for Lisa to eat all of the candies?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate how many candies Lisa eats on Mondays and Wednesdays combined.

Lisa eats 2 candies each on Monday and Wednesday.

Total candies eaten on these days = 2 candies × 2 days = 4 candies

Step 2: Calculate how many candies Lisa eats on the other days of the week.

Lisa eats 1 candy each on the other 5 days of the week (Tuesday, Thursday, Friday, Saturday, and Sunday).

Total candies eaten on these days = 1 candy × 4 days = 4 candies

Step 3: Calculate the total number of candies Lisa eats in one week.

Total weekly consumption = Candies eaten on Monday and Wednesday + Candies eaten on other days

Total weekly consumption = 4 + 4 = 8 candies per week

Step 4: Calculate how many weeks it takes to eat all candies.

Number of weeks = Total candies ÷ Weekly consumption

Number of weeks = 36 ÷ 8 = 4.5 weeks

Final Answer:

4.5
