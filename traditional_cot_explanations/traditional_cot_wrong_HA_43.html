Problem Statement:

Monika went out for the day and spent some money. She went to the mall and spent $250. Then, she went to the movies and watched 3 movies back to back that each cost $24. Then she stopped by the farmer's market on her way home and got 20 bags of beans at $1.25/bag. How much money did Monika spend throughout her day?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate how much Monika spent at the movies.

We need to multiply the number of movies by the cost per movie:

3 movies × $24 per movie = $72

Step 2: Add the cost of drinks that Monika bought at the movies.

We need to add the movie cost to the cost of drinks (one drink per movie):

$72 + (3 movies × $3 per drink) = $72 + $9 = $81

Step 3: Calculate how much Monika spent at the farmers market.

We need to multiply the number of bags by the cost per bag:

20 bags × $1.25 per bag = $25

Step 4: Calculate the total amount Monika spent throughout the day.

We need to add all the expenses:

Mall cost + Total movie cost + Farmers market cost = $250 + $81 + $25 = $356

Final Answer:

356
