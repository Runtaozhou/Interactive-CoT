Problem Statement:

For a school fundraiser, Tory needs to sell 50 packs of cookies. So far, he has sold 12 packs to his grandmother, 7 packs to his uncle, and 5 packs to a neighbor. How many more packs of cookies does Tory need to sell?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Count how many packs Tory sold to his grandmother. Tory sold 12 packs to his grandmother.
Step 2: Calculate the total number of packs Tory has sold so far.
Grandmother's packs + Neighbor's packs = 12 packs + 5 packs = 17 packs
So Tory has sold a total of 17 packs so far.
Step 3: Calculate how many more packs Tory needs to sell.
Total packs needed - Total packs sold = 50 packs - 17 packs = 33 packs
Therefore, Tory needs to sell 33 more packs of cookies.

Final Answer:

33
