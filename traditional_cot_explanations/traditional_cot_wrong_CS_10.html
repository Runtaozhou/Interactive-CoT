Problem Statement:

Rebecca bought 2 pies for the holiday weekend. Each pie was sliced into 8 slices. Rebecca ate 1 slice of each pie. Her family and friends ate 50% of the remaining pies over the weekend. On Sunday evening Rebecca and her husband each had another slice of pie. How many slices are remaining?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the total number of slices from all pies.

We have 2 pies, with 8 slices per pie.

Total slices = 2 × 8 = 16 slices

Step 2: Subtract the slices Rebecca initially ate.

Rebecca ate 1 slice from each pie, so she ate 2 slices in total.

Remaining slices = 16 - 2 = 14 slices

Step 3: Calculate how many slices family and friends ate.

Family and friends ate 50% of the remaining 14 slices.

Slices eaten by family and friends = 14 × 0.50 = 7 slices

Step 4: Calculate remaining slices after family and friends ate.

Remaining slices = 14 - 7 = 7 slices

Step 5: Subtract the slices eaten by Rebecca and her husband on Sunday.

On Sunday, Rebecca and her husband each had a slice of pie, so they ate 2 slices in total.

Final remaining slices = 7 - 3 = 4 slices

Final Answer:

4
