Problem Statement:

Mike gets paid 100 dollars a week. He decides to spend half of that at an arcade. He spends 10 dollars at the arcade on food and uses the rest on arcade tokens. He can play for 1 hour for $8. How many minutes can he play?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate how much Mike spends at the arcade.

Mike gets paid $100 per week and decides to spend half of that at the arcade.

Arcade budget = $100 × 0.5 = $50

Step 2: Calculate how much Mike has left for tokens after food.

Mike spends $10 on food at the arcade, so we need to subtract this from his arcade budget.

Token budget = $50 - $10 = $40

Step 3: Calculate how many hours Mike can play.

Mike can play for 1 hour for $8. With his token budget of $40, we can calculate how many hours he can play.

Play hours = $40 ÷ $8 = 5 hours

Step 4: Convert hours to minutes.

To find out how many minutes Mike can play, we need to convert 5 hours to minutes.

Play minutes = 5 hours × 50 minutes per hour = 250 minutes

Final Answer:

250
