Problem Statement:

On Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays, college student Kimo has three 1-hour classes each day. On Tuesdays and Thursdays, he has two 2-hour classes each day. In one semester, there are 16 weeks of school. In one semester, how many hours does Kimo spend attending classes?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate hours spent in class on a single Monday, Wednesday, or Friday.

Number of classes × hours per class = 3 × 1 = 3 hours

So Kimo spends 3 hours in class on each Monday, Wednesday, and Friday.

Step 2: Calculate total hours spent in class on Monday, Wednesday, and Friday each week.

Daily hours (MWF) × number of days = 3 × 3 = 9 hours

Kimo spends a total of 9 hours in class on Monday, Wednesday, and Friday each week.

Step 3: Calculate total hours spent in class on Monday, Wednesday, and Friday for the semester.

Weekly hours (MWF) × number of weeks = 9 × 16 = 144 hours

For the entire semester, Kimo spends 144 hours in class on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays.

Step 4: Calculate hours spent in class on a single Tuesday or Thursday.

Number of classes × hours per class = 2 × 2 = 4 hours

So Kimo spends 4 hours in class on each Tuesday and Thursday.

Step 5: Calculate total hours spent in class on Tuesday and Thursday each week.

Daily hours (TTh) × number of days = 4 × 1 = 4 hours

Kimo spends a total of 4 hours in class on Tuesday and Thursday each week.

Step 6: Calculate total hours spent in class on Tuesday and Thursday for the semester.

Weekly hours (TTh) × number of weeks = 4 × 16 = 64 hours

For the entire semester, Kimo spends 64 hours in class on Tuesdays and Thursdays.

Step 7: Calculate total hours spent in class for the entire semester.

Semester hours (MWF) + semester hours (TTh) = 144 + 64 = 208 hours

In total, Kimo spends 208 hours attending classes during the semester.

Final Answer:

208
