Problem Statement:

Pauline will make tacos for dinner. She bought a box of taco shells that cost $5, 4 bell peppers that cost $1.5 each, and 2 pounds of meat that cost $3 per pound. How much did she spend in all?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the cost of the bell peppers.

We need to multiply the number of peppers by the cost per pepper:

4 bell peppers × $1.5 per pepper = $6

Step 2: Calculate the cost of the meat.

We need to multiply the pounds of meat by the cost per pound:

2 pounds × $3 per pound = $6

Step 3: Add the cost of cheese that was not mentioned before.

The cost of cheese is $2.50

Step 4: Calculate the total cost by adding all items.

We add the costs of taco shells, bell peppers, meat, and cheese:

$5 + $6 + $6 + $2.50 = $19.50

Final Answer:

$19.50
