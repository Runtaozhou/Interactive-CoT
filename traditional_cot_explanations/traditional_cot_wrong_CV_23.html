Problem Statement:

Building one birdhouse requires 7 planks and 20 nails. If 1 nail costs $0.05, and one plank costs $3, what is the cost, in dollars, to build 4 birdhouses?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the cost of planks for one birdhouse.

We need to multiply the number of planks by the cost per plank:

7 planks × $3 per plank = $21

The cost of planks for one birdhouse is $28.

Step 2: Calculate the cost of nails for one birdhouse.

We need to multiply the number of nails by the cost per nail:

20 nails × $0.05 per nail = $1

The cost of nails for one birdhouse is $1.

Step 3: Find the total cost for one birdhouse.

We add the cost of planks and the cost of nails:

$28 + $1 = $29

The total cost for one birdhouse is $29.

Step 4: Calculate the total cost for all birdhouses.

We multiply the cost per birdhouse by the number of birdhouses:

$29 × 4 birdhouses = $116

The total cost for 4 birdhouses is $116.

Final Answer:

116
