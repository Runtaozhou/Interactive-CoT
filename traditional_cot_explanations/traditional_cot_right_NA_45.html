Problem Statement:

Tim's website got 100 visitors a day for the first 6 days and then on the last day of the week it got twice as many visitors as every other day combined. If he gets $.01 per visit how much did he make that week?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the total number of visitors for the first 6 days.

We know that Tim's website received 100 visitors per day for the first 6 days.

Total visitors for first 6 days = 100 visitors × 6 days = 600 visitors

Step 2: Calculate the number of visitors on the 7th day.

On the 7th day, the website received twice as many visitors as all other days combined.

Visitors on day 7 = 2 × visitors from first 6 days = 2 × 600 = 1,200 visitors

Step 3: Calculate the total number of visitors for the week.

Now we add the visitors from the first 6 days and the 7th day to find the total for the week.

Total visitors for the week = 600 + 1,200 = 1,800 visitors

Step 4: Calculate the total income for the week.

Tim earns $0.01 per visit, so we multiply the total number of visitors by this amount.

Total income = 1,800 visitors × $0.01 per visitor = $18

Final Answer:

18
