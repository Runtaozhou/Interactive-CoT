Problem Statement:

Lisa has 36 candies. On Mondays and Wednesdays, she eats 2 candies for each day and on the other days of the week she eats 1 candy for each day. How many weeks does it take for Lisa to eat all of the candies?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate how many candies Lisa eats on Mondays and Wednesdays combined.

On Mondays and Wednesdays, Lisa eats 2 candies per day.

Number of candies = 2 candies per day × 2 days = 4 candies

Step 2: Calculate how many candies Lisa eats on the other days of the week.

On the other 5 days of the week (Tuesday, Thursday, Friday, Saturday, and Sunday), Lisa eats 1 candy per day.

Number of candies = 1 candy per day × 5 days = 5 candies

Step 3: Calculate the total number of candies Lisa eats in one week.

Total weekly consumption = Candies eaten on Mondays and Wednesdays + Candies eaten on other days

Total weekly consumption = 4 candies + 5 candies = 9 candies per week

Step 4: Calculate how many weeks it takes to eat all candies.

Number of weeks = Total number of candies ÷ Candies eaten per week

Number of weeks = 36 candies ÷ 9 candies per week = 4 weeks

Final Answer:

4
