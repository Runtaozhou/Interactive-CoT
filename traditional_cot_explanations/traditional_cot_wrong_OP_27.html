Problem Statement:

Emily has 4 kids named Amy, Jackson, Corey, and James. Amy is 5 years older than Jackson and 2 years younger than Corey. If James is 10 and is 1 year younger than Corey, how old is Jackson?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate Corey's age based on James's age.

Since James is 10 years old and is 1 year younger than Corey, we can calculate Corey's age:

Corey's age = James's age + 1

Corey's age = 10 + 1 = 11 years old

Step 2: Calculate Amy's age based on Corey's age.

We know that Amy is 2 years younger than Corey, so:

Amy's age = Corey's age - 2

Amy's age = 11 - 2 = 9 years old

Step 3: Calculate Jackson's age based on Amy's age.

We know that Amy is 5 years older than Jackson, so:

Jackson's age = Amy's age - 5

Jackson's age = 9 - 5 = 4 years old

Final Answer:

2
