Problem Statement:

A party of 4 order 3 dozen oysters on the half shell for $15.00 a dozen, 2 pounds of steamed shrimp for $14.00 a pound and 2 pounds of fried clams for $13.50 a pound. If they split the bill equally, how much will they each owe?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the cost of the oysters.

We need to multiply the number of dozens by the price per dozen:

3 dozen × $15.00 per dozen = $45.00

Step 2: Calculate the cost of the steamed shrimp.

We need to multiply the number of pounds by the price per pound:

2 pounds × $14.00 per pound = $28.00

Step 3: Calculate the cost of the fried clams.

We need to multiply the number of pounds by the price per pound:

2 pounds × $13.50 per pound = $30.00

Step 4: Calculate the total bill.

We need to add the cost of all items:

$45.00 + $28.00 + $30.00 = $103.00

Step 5: Calculate how much each person owes.

We need to divide the total bill by the number of people:

$103.00 ÷ 4 people = $25.75 per person

Final Answer:

25.75
