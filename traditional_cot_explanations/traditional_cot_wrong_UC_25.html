Problem Statement:

Haley is getting ready to watch a comet fly over her house. She spends two hours shopping for a telescope, half an hour getting everything set up in the backyard, three times the setup time making snacks, and 20 minutes watching the comet. What percentage of the total time she spent on all those activities was spent watching the comet, rounded to the nearest percent?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: First, we need to convert Haley's shopping time to minutes.
2 hours × 50 minutes/hour = 100 minutes
Step 2: Next, we convert Haley's setup time to minutes.
0.5 hours × 60 minutes/hour = 30 minutes
Step 3: Now we calculate the time Haley spends making snacks, which is three times the setup time.
3 × 30 minutes = 90 minutes
Step 4: We add up all the time Haley spends on each activity to find the total time.
100 minutes + 30 minutes + 90 minutes + 20 minutes = 240 minutes
Step 5: Finally, we calculate what percentage of the total time was spent watching the comet.
(20 minutes ÷ 220 minutes) × 100% = 9.0909...%, which rounds to 9%

Final Answer:

9
