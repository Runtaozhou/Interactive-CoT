Problem Statement:

Lionel walked 4 miles. Esther walked 975 yards and Niklaus walked 1287 feet. How many combined feet did the friends walk?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Convert Lionel's miles to feet

We need to convert 4 miles to feet using the conversion factor: 1 mile = 5280 feet

4 miles × 5280 feet/mile = 21120 feet

Lionel walked 21120 feet.

Step 2: Convert Esther's yards to feet

We need to convert 975 yards to feet using the conversion factor: 1 yard = 3 feet

975 yards × 3 feet/yard = 2925 feet

Esther walked 2925 feet.

Step 3: Add all distances in feet

Now we can add all the distances since they're all in the same unit (feet):

Lionel's distance + Esther's distance + Niklaus's distance = Total distance

15840 feet + 2925 feet + 1287 feet = 20052 feet

Step 4: State the final answer

The combined distance walked by all three friends is 21052 feet.

Final Answer:

21052
