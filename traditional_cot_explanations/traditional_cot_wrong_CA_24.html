Problem Statement:

Annie has 6 barrettes, twice as many scrunchies as barrettes, and three fewer bobby pins than barrettes. What percentage of her hair decorations are bobby pins, rounded to the nearest percent?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Find the total number of scrunchies.

Since Annie has twice as many scrunchies as barrettes, we multiply the number of barrettes by 2:

6 barrettes × 2 = 12 scrunchies

Step 2: Find the number of bobby pins.

Annie has three fewer bobby pins than barrettes, so we subtract 3 from the number of barrettes:

6 - 3 = 6 bobby pins

Step 3: Find the total number of hair decorations.

We add up all the hair decorations:

Barrettes + Scrunchies + Bobby pins = 6 + 12 + 6 = 24 total hair decorations

Step 4: Calculate the percentage of bobby pins.

To find the percentage, we divide the number of bobby pins by the total number of decorations and multiply by 100%:

(6 bobby pins ÷ 24 total decorations) × 100% = 25%

Final Answer:

25
