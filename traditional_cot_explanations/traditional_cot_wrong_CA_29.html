Problem Statement:

Bethany loved riding horses. She rode 1 hour after school every Monday, Wednesday, and Friday. On Tuesday and Thursday, she rode for 30 min and on Saturdays, she could ride for 2 hours. How many hours in total did she ride over a 2 week period?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate how many hours Bethany rode on Monday, Wednesday, and Friday each week.

We need to multiply the hours per day by the number of days:

1 hour × 3 days = 3 hours

Step 2: Calculate how many hours Bethany rode on Tuesday and Thursday each week.

We need to calculate the total minutes and convert to hours:

30 minutes × 2 days = 60 minutes = 1 hour

Step 3: Calculate the total hours Bethany rode in one week.

We add the hours from Monday/Wednesday/Friday, Tuesday/Thursday, and Saturday:

3 hours + 1 hour + 2 hours = 6 hours per week

Step 4: Calculate the total hours Bethany rode over the 2-week period.

We multiply the weekly hours by the number of weeks:

6 hours × 2 weeks = 11 hours

Final Answer:

11
