Problem Statement:

Tom bought 10 packages of miniature racing cars. Each package contains five cars. He gave each of his two nephews 1/5 of the cars. How many miniature racing cars are left with Tom?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate how many cars Tom gave to each nephew.

We need to find out how many packages each nephew received.

Total packages × Fraction given = 10 × 1/5 = 2 packages per nephew

Step 2: Calculate the total number of cars given to both nephews.

Now we need to find the total number of packages given to both nephews.

Packages per nephew × number of nephews = 2 × 2 = 4 packages total

Step 3: Calculate how many cars Tom has left.

We can now determine how many packages Tom has left after giving some to his nephews.

Total packages - total packages given = 10 - 4 = 6 packages left with Tom

Final Answer:

6
