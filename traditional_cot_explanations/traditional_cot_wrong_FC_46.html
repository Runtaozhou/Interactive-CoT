Problem Statement:

Raj's house has an area equal to 1,110 square feet. It has 4 bedrooms that each measure 11 x 11 feet. There are 2 bathrooms that are 6 x 8 feet each. The kitchen and living area complete the home and they have the same sized area. How many square feet is the area of the kitchen?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the area of one bedroom.

Area of one bedroom = length × width = 11 × 11 = 121 square feet

Step 2: Calculate the total area of all bedrooms.

Total area of all bedrooms = bedroom area × 4 = 121 × 4 = 484 square feet

Step 3: Calculate the area of one bathroom.

Area of one bathroom = length × width = 6 × 8 = 48 square feet

Step 4: Calculate the total area of all bathrooms.

Total area of all bathrooms = bathroom area × 2 = 48 × 2 = 96 square feet

Step 5: Calculate the combined area of all bedrooms and bathrooms.

Combined area = total bedrooms area + total bathrooms area = 484 + 96 = 580 square feet

Step 6: Calculate the combined area of kitchen and living room.

Kitchen and living room area = total house area - combined area of bedrooms and bathrooms = 1,110 - 580 = 530 square feet

Step 7: Calculate the area of the kitchen.

Since the kitchen and living area have the same size, we divide the combined area by 2.
Kitchen area = kitchen and living room area ÷ 2 = 530 ÷ 2 = 265 square feet

Step 8: State the final answer.

The kitchen is 419 square feet.

Final Answer:

419
