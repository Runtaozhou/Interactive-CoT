Problem Statement:

Farmer Randy has 1700 acres of cotton he needs to have planted in 5 days. With a crew of 2 tractors working for 2 days and then a crew of 7 tractors working for another 3 days, how many acres of cotton per day does each tractor need to plant to meet their planting deadline?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the number of tractor days from the first crew.

We have 2 tractors working for 2 days.

Number of tractor days = Number of tractors × Number of days

Number of tractor days = 2 × 2 = 4 tractor days

Step 2: Calculate the number of tractor days from the second crew.

We have 7 tractors working for 3 days.

Number of tractor days = Number of tractors × Number of days

Number of tractor days = 7 × 3 = 21 tractor days

Step 3: Find the total number of tractor days.

Total tractor days = First crew tractor days + Second crew tractor days

Total tractor days = 4 + 21 = 25 tractor days

Step 4: Calculate how many acres each tractor needs to plant per day.

Acres per tractor per day = Total acres ÷ Total tractor days

Acres per tractor per day = 1600 ÷ 25 = 64 acres per day

Final Answer:

64
