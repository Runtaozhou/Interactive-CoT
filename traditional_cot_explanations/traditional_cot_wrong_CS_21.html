Problem Statement:

Donna worked 2 hours every morning walking dogs in her neighborhood for $10.00 an hour. 5 days a week, after school, she worked at a card shop for 2 hours and made $12.50 an hour. On the weekends, she usually made $10.00 an hour babysitting and was guaranteed 4 hours every Saturday from her neighbor. How much money did she make over 7 days?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate how much Donna makes each morning walking dogs.

Donna earns $10.00 per hour and walks dogs for 2 hours each morning.

Daily dog walking income = $10.00 × 2 hours = $20.00

Step 2: Calculate how much Donna makes in a week from walking dogs.

Since Donna walks dogs every morning, that's 7 days per week.

Weekly dog walking income = $20.00 × 7 days = $140.00

Step 3: Calculate how much Donna makes each day at the card shop.

At the card shop, Donna earns $12.50 per hour and works 2 hours per day.

Daily card shop income = $12.50 × 2 hours = $25.00

Step 4: Calculate how much Donna makes in a week from the card shop.

Donna works at the card shop 5 days a week.

Weekly card shop income = $25.00 × 5 days = $125.00

Step 5: Calculate how much Donna makes from babysitting on Saturday.

For babysitting, Donna earns $10.00 per hour for 4 hours on Saturday.

Babysitting income = $10.00 × 4 hours = $40.00

Step 6: Calculate Donna's total weekly income.

We add all sources of income for the week:

Total weekly income = $140.00 (dog walking) + $135.00 (card shop) + $40.00 (babysitting) = $315.00

Final Answer:

315
