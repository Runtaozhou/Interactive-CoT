Problem Statement:

Jimmy wants to order a pizza at a new place. The large pizza costs $10.00 and is cut into 8 slices. The first topping costs $2.00, the next 2 toppings cost $1.00 each and the rest of the toppings cost $0.50. If he orders a large pizza with pepperoni, sausage, ham, olives, mushrooms, bell peppers and pineapple. How much will his pizza cost per slice?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: First, we need to calculate the cost of the pizza plus the first topping (pepperoni).
Base pizza cost + First topping cost = $10.00 + $2.00 = $12.00
Step 2: Next, we calculate the cost of the next two toppings (sausage and ham).
2 toppings × $1.00 each = 2 × $1.00 = $2.00
Step 3: Now, we calculate the cost of the remaining toppings (olives, mushrooms, bell peppers and pineapple).
4 toppings × $0.50 each = 4 × $0.50 = $1.00
Step 4: We can now calculate the total cost of the pizza with all toppings.
Initial cost + Second tier cost + Remaining toppings cost = $12.00 + $2.00 + $1.00 = $15.00
Step 5: Finally, we calculate the cost per slice by dividing the total cost by the number of slices.
Total pizza cost ÷ Number of slices = $15.00 ÷ 8 = $1.88

Final Answer:

1.88
