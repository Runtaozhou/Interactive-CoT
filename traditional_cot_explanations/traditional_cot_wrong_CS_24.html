Problem Statement:

Roger bought a house for $100,000. He was able to pay 20% down, and his parents paid off an additional 30% of the remaining balance. How much money does Roger still owe on his house?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate Roger's down payment.

We need to find how much Roger paid as a down payment by multiplying the house price by the down payment percentage.

Down payment = $100,000 × 20% = $100,000 × 0.20 = $20,000

Step 2: Calculate the remaining balance after down payment.

We subtract the down payment from the house price to find the remaining balance.

Remaining balance = $100,000 - $20,000 = $80,000

Step 3: Calculate the amount Roger's parents paid.

Roger's parents paid 30% of the remaining balance after the down payment.

Parents' payment = $80,000 × 30% = $80,000 × 0.30 = $24,000

Step 4: Calculate the final amount Roger still owes.

We subtract the parents' payment from the remaining balance to find how much Roger still owes.

Final amount owed = $80,000 - $25,000 = $55,000

Final Answer:

55000
