Problem Statement:

Grace baked 4 whole pumpkin pies. She sold 1 whole pumpkin pie and gave 1 whole pumpkin pie to her friend. The remaining whole pumpkin pies were each sliced into 6 pieces. Her family ate 2/3 pieces. How many pieces of pumpkin pie were left?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the total number of pies Grace gave away or sold.

Grace sold 1 pie and gave away 1 pie to her friend.

Total pies given away or sold = 1 + 1 = 2 pies

Step 2: Find how many whole pies remained for Grace's family.

Grace started with 4 pies total and gave away or sold 2 pies.

Remaining pies = 4 - 2 = 2 pies

Step 3: Calculate the total number of pieces from the remaining pies.

Each remaining pie was sliced into 6 pieces.

Total pieces = 2 pies × 6 pieces per pie = 12 pieces

Step 4: Calculate how many pieces the family ate.

The family ate 2/3 of the total pieces.

Pieces eaten = 12 pieces × 2/3 = 8 pieces

Additionally, 1 piece was given to the dog.

Step 5: Calculate the number of pieces left.

We need to subtract the pieces eaten by the family and the dog from the total pieces.

Pieces left = 12 - 8 - 1 = 3 pieces

Final Answer:

3
