Problem Statement:

Zion is 8 years old and his dad is 3 more than 4 times his age. In 10 years, how many years older is Zion's dad than him?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate four times Zion's age.

Zion's age × 4 = 8 × 4 = 32 years

Step 2: Calculate Zion's dad's current age.

Dad's age = Four times Zion's age + 3 = 32 + 3 = 35 years

Step 3: Calculate Zion's age in 10 years.

Zion's future age = Zion's current age + 10 = 5 + 10 = 15 years

Step 4: Calculate Zion's dad's age in 10 years.

Dad's future age = Dad's current age + 10 = 35 + 10 = 45 years

Step 5: Calculate the age difference in 10 years.

Future age difference = Dad's future age - Zion's future age = 45 - 15 = 30 years

Final Answer:

30
