Problem Statement:

Dream's car consumes 4 gallons of gas per mile. If she drives 400 miles today and 200 more miles tomorrow than today, how many gallons of gas will the car consume?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the fuel consumption for today's drive.

We know that the car consumes 4 gallons per mile and Dream drives 400 miles today.

Fuel consumption = 4 gallons/mile × 400 miles = 1600 gallons

Step 2: Calculate the total distance for tomorrow.

Tomorrow, Dream will drive 200 miles more than today.

Tomorrow's distance = Today's distance + Additional miles = 400 miles + 200 miles = 600 miles

Step 3: Calculate the fuel consumption for tomorrow's drive.

Using the same fuel consumption rate of 4 gallons per mile:

Tomorrow's fuel consumption = 4 gallons/mile × 600 miles = 2400 gallons

Step 4: Calculate the total fuel consumption for both days.

Total fuel consumption = Today's fuel + Tomorrow's fuel = 1600 gallons + 2400 gallons = 4000 gallons

Final Answer:

4000
