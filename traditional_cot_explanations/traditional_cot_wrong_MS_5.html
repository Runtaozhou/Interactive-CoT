Problem Statement:

Josh works 8 hours a day, 5 days a week, 4 weeks a month. Carl is an intern and works 2 hours less than Josh every day. Josh gets $9 an hour and Carl gets half that. How much does the company pay for the 2 together in one month?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate Josh's total working hours in a month.

Using the formula: hours per day × days per week × weeks per month

Josh works 8 hours/day * 5 days/week * 4 weeks/month = 160 hours a month

Step 2: Calculate Josh's monthly salary.

Using the formula: monthly hours × hourly rate

Josh earns 160 hours/month * $9/hour = $1440 per month

Step 3: Calculate Carl's monthly salary.

Using the formula: monthly hours × hourly rate

Carl earns 160 hours/month * $4.5/hour = $720 per month

Step 4: Calculate the total monthly payment for both employees.

Using the formula: Josh's monthly salary + Carl's monthly salary

Total payment = $1440 + $720 = $2160

Final Answer:

2160
