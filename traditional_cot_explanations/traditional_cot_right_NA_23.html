Problem Statement:

Micah can type 20 words per minute and Isaiah can type 40 words per minute. How many more words can Isaiah type than Micah in an hour?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate how many minutes are in an hour.

There are 60 minutes in an hour.

Step 2: Calculate how many words Micah can type in an hour.

To find this, we multiply Micah's typing speed by the number of minutes in an hour:

20 words per minute × 60 minutes = 1200 words

Step 3: Calculate how many words Isaiah can type in an hour.

Similarly, we multiply Isaiah's typing speed by the number of minutes in an hour:

40 words per minute × 60 minutes = 2400 words

Step 4: Find the difference between Isaiah's and Micah's typing capacity.

We subtract Micah's words per hour from Isaiah's words per hour:

2400 words - 1200 words = 1200 words

Final Answer:

1200
