Problem Statement:

Team Soccer Stars plays in a soccer league. They played a total of 20 games, and won 14 games, lost 2, and drew the rest of the matches. They earn 3 points for a win, 1 point for a draw, and nothing for a loss. How many points did they have at the end of the season?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the total number of games won and lost.

We add the number of wins and losses: 14 + 2 = 16 games

Step 2: Calculate the number of games drawn.

We subtract the games won and lost from the total games: 20 - 12 = 8 games

Step 3: Calculate the points earned from wins.

Each win is worth 3 points, so: 14 × 3 = 42 points

Step 4: Calculate the points earned from draws.

Each draw is worth 1 point, so: 8 × 1 = 8 points

Step 5: Calculate the points earned from losses.

Each loss is worth 0 points, so: 2 × 0 = 0 points

Step 6: Calculate the total points earned.

We add all the points: 42 + 8 + 0 = 50 points

Final Answer:

50
