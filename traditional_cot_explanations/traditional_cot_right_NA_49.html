Problem Statement:

Dexter has five pigs. Each one eats 4 pounds of feed, twice a day. If Dexter ordered 300 pounds of feed, how many pounds of feed is left after a week?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate how much feed one pig consumes in a day.

Each pig eats 4 pounds of feed twice a day.

Daily consumption per pig = 4 pounds × 2 feedings = 8 pounds

Step 2: Calculate how much feed all pigs consume in a day.

Dexter has 5 pigs, and each pig eats 8 pounds of feed per day.

Total daily feed consumption = 8 pounds per pig × 5 pigs = 40 pounds

Step 3: Calculate how much feed all pigs consume in a week.

A week has 7 days, and the total daily feed consumption is 40 pounds.

Total weekly feed consumption = 40 pounds per day × 7 days = 280 pounds

Step 4: Calculate how much feed is left after a week.

Dexter ordered 300 pounds of feed, and the pigs consume 280 pounds in a week.

Feed remaining = 300 pounds - 280 pounds = 20 pounds

Final Answer:

20
