Problem Statement:

Carly wants to treat her friends. She orders five hamburgers at $4 each, ten packs of potato fries at $0.30 each pack, and five cans of fruit drinks at $2 each can. How much change does Carly get back if she gives one fifty-dollar bill?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the cost of hamburgers.

We need to multiply the number of hamburgers by the price per hamburger:

5 × $4 = $20

The total cost of hamburgers is $20.

Step 2: Calculate the cost of potato fries.

We need to multiply the number of packs by the price per pack:

10 × $0.30 = $3

The total cost of potato fries is $3.

Step 3: Calculate the cost of fruit drinks.

We need to multiply the number of cans by the price per can:

5 × $2 = $10

The total cost of fruit drinks is $10.

Step 4: Calculate the total cost of all items.

We need to add the costs of hamburgers, fries, and drinks:

$20 - $3 + $10 = $27

The total cost of all items is $27.

Step 5: Calculate the change Carly receives.

We need to subtract the total cost from the payment:

$50 - $27 = $23

Carly receives $23 in change.

Final Answer:

23
