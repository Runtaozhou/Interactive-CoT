Problem Statement:

Grace baked 4 whole pumpkin pies. She sold 1 whole pumpkin pie and gave 1 whole pumpkin pie to her friend. The remaining whole pumpkin pies were each sliced into 6 pieces. Her family ate 2/3 pieces. How many pieces of pumpkin pie were left?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the total number of pies Grace gave away or sold.

Grace sold 1 pie and gave away 1 pie to her friend.

Total pies given away or sold = 1 + 1 = 2 pies

Step 2: Calculate how many whole pies remained with Grace's family.

Grace started with 4 pies total and gave away or sold 2 pies.

Remaining pies = 4 - 2 = 2 pies

Step 3: Calculate how many pieces were made from the remaining pies.

According to the calculation, we take the remaining pies minus 1 and multiply by the number of slices per pie.

Total pieces = (2 - 1) × 6 = 6 pieces

Step 4: Calculate how many pieces Grace's family ate.

The family ate 2/3 of the total pieces.

Pieces eaten = 6 × 2/3 = 4 pieces

Step 5: Calculate how many pieces of pie were left.

To find the remaining pieces, we subtract the pieces eaten from the total pieces.

Pieces left = 6 - 4 = 2 pieces

Final Answer:

2
