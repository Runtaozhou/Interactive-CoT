Problem Statement:

Faith went to a baking shop and bought flour that cost $5 and a cake stand that costs $28. She then gave the cashier two $20 bills and $3 in loose coins. How much change will she receive?

Step By Step Explanation:

We need to add the cost of flour and the cost of the cake stand:

$28 + $5 = $33

The total cost of Faith's purchases is $33.

Faith paid $40 in bills.

$40 + $4 = $44

Faith gave the cashier a total of $44.

Step 4: Calculate the change Faith will receive.

To find the change, we subtract the total cost from the total payment:
