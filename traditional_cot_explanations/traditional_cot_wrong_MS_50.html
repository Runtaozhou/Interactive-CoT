Problem Statement:

Eddie can bake 3 pies a day. His sister can bake 6 pies a day while his mother can bake 8 pies a day. How many pies will they be able to bake in 7 days?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate how many pies Eddie can bake in 7 days.

Eddie bakes 3 pies per day, so in 7 days he will bake:

3 pies/day × 7 days = 21 pies

Step 2: Calculate how many pies Eddie's sister can bake in 7 days.

Eddie's sister bakes 6 pies per day, so in 7 days she will bake:

6 pies/day × 7 days = 42 pies

Step 3: Calculate how many pies Eddie's mother can bake in 7 days.

Eddie's mother bakes 8 pies per day, so in 7 days she will bake:

8 pies/day × 7 days = 56 pies

Step 4: Add up all the pies to find the total.

We need to add Eddie's pies, his sister's pies, and his mother's pies:

3×7 + 42 + 56 = 119 pies

Final Answer:

119
