Problem Statement:

Suzie and 5 of her friends decide to rent an Airbnb at Lake Tahoe for 4 days from Thursday to Sunday. The rental rate for weekdays is $420 per day. The weekend rental rate is $540 per day. They all decide to split the rental evenly. How much does each person have to pay?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the cost for weekdays (Thursday and Friday).

We need to multiply the weekday rate by the number of weekdays:

$420 × 2 = $840

So the cost for weekdays is $840.

Step 2: Calculate the cost for weekend days (Saturday and Sunday).

We need to multiply the weekend rate by the number of weekend days:

$540 × 1 = $540

So the cost for weekend days is $540.

Step 3: Calculate the total cost of the rental.

We add the weekday cost and the weekend cost:

$840 + $540 = $1380

So the total cost of the rental is $1380.

Step 4: Calculate the cost per person.

We divide the total cost by the total number of people (Suzie and her 5 friends = 6 people):

$1380 ÷ 6 = $230

So each person has to pay $230.

Final Answer:

$230
