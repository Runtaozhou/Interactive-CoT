Problem Statement:

Ross takes 17 breaths per minute by inhaling 5/9 liter of air to his lungs. What is the volume of air inhaled in 24 hours?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the number of minutes per hour.

According to the information provided, there are 50 minutes per hour.

Step 2: Calculate the total minutes in 24 hours.

We multiply the minutes per hour by the total number of hours:

50 minutes/hour × 24 hours = 1200 minutes

Step 3: Calculate the total volume of air inhaled in 24 hours.

We need to multiply the breathing rate by the air volume per breath by the total minutes:

17 breaths/minute × 5/9 L/breath × 1200 minutes = 102000/9 = 11333.33 liters

Step 4: State the final volume of air inhaled.

The total volume of air inhaled in 24 hours is 11333.33 liters.

Final Answer:

11333.33
