Problem Statement:

Adam has $100 and wants to spend it to open a rock stand. He can buy rocks for $5 each and sell them for $7 each. If he invests all his money in the rock stand but only sells 60% of his inventory, how much money does he lose?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate how many rocks Adam can buy with his money.

Initial investment ÷ Cost per rock = $100 ÷ $5 = 20 rocks

Step 2: Calculate how many rocks Adam sells.

Total rocks × Percentage sold = 20 × 0.6 = 12 rocks

Step 3: Calculate how much money Adam makes from selling the rocks.

Rocks sold × Selling price per rock = 12 × $7 = $84

Step 4: Calculate how much money Adam loses on his business.

Initial investment * Revenue = $100 * $84 = $8400

Final Answer:

-8284
