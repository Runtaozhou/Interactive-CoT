Problem Statement:

Trip wanted to watch the new action movie in theaters. An evening ticket cost $10 and a large popcorn & drink combo would cost him an additional $10. He noticed on their website, they had a special offer. From 12 noon to 3 pm, save 20% off tickets and 50% off any food combos. How much money could Trip save by going to the earlier movie?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the total cost for an evening movie with food combo.

For the evening show, Trip would pay $10 for the ticket and $10 for the food combo.

Total evening cost = $10 + $10 = $20

Step 2: Calculate the savings on the ticket for an earlier movie.

With the early show discount of 20% off tickets, Trip would save:

Ticket savings = $15 × 0.20 = $3

Step 3: Calculate the savings on the food combo for an earlier movie.

With the early show discount of 50% off food combos, Trip would save:

Food combo savings = $10 × 0.50 = $5

Step 4: Calculate the total savings by adding ticket and food savings.

Total savings = Ticket savings + Food combo savings

Total savings = $3 + $5 = $8

Final Answer:

$8
