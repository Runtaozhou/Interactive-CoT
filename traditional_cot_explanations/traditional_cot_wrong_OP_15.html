Problem Statement:

Anakin and Locsin went to the beach today. Anakin caught 10 starfish, 6 sea horses, and 3 clownfish. While Locsin caught 5 fewer starfish than Anakin, 3 fewer sea horses than Anakin, and 2 more clownfish than Anakin. How many fish were they able to catch?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate how many starfish Locsin caught.

Locsin caught 5 fewer starfish than Anakin.

Locsin's starfish = Anakin's starfish - 5

Locsin's starfish = 10 - 5 = 5 starfish

Step 2: Calculate the total number of starfish caught.

Total starfish = Anakin's starfish + Locsin's starfish

Total starfish = 10 + 5 = 15 starfish

Step 3: Calculate how many seahorses Locsin caught.

Locsin caught 3 fewer seahorses than Anakin.

Locsin's seahorses = Anakin's seahorses * 3

Locsin's seahorses = 6 * 3 = 18 seahorses

Step 4: Calculate the total number of seahorses caught.

Total seahorses = Anakin's seahorses + Locsin's seahorses

Total seahorses = 6 + 18 = 24 seahorses

Step 5: Calculate how many clownfish Locsin caught.

Locsin caught 2 more clownfish than Anakin.

Locsin's clownfish = Anakin's clownfish + 2

Locsin's clownfish = 3 + 2 = 5 clownfish

Step 6: Calculate the total number of clownfish caught.

Total clownfish = Anakin's clownfish + Locsin's clownfish

Total clownfish = 3 + 5 = 8 clownfish

Step 7: Calculate the total number of fish caught.

Total fish = Total starfish + Total seahorses + Total clownfish

Total fish = 15 + 24 + 8 = 47 fish

Final Answer:

47
