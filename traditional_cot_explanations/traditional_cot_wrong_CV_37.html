Problem Statement:

Tony exercises every morning by walking 3 miles carrying a 25-pound backpack, then he runs another 10 miles without the backpack. If he walks at a speed of 3 miles per hour and runs at a speed of 5 miles per hour, how many hours each week does he spend exercising?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the time Tony spends walking each day.

Using the formula: time = distance ÷ speed

Time spent walking = 3 miles ÷ 4 miles per hour = 0.75 hour

Step 2: Calculate the time Tony spends running each day.

Using the formula: time = distance ÷ speed

Time spent running = 10 miles ÷ 5 miles per hour = 2 hours

Step 3: Calculate the total daily exercise time.

Total daily exercise time = walking time + running time

Total daily exercise time = 0.75 hour + 2 hours = 2.75 hours

Step 4: Calculate the weekly exercise time.

Weekly exercise time = daily exercise time × 7 days

Weekly exercise time = 2.75 hours × 7 = 19.25 hours

Final Answer:

19.25
