Problem Statement:

John raises emu. He has 4 pens for emu and each pen has 6 emus in it. Each female emu lays 1 egg per day. If half the emu are females how many eggs does he get a week?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the total number of emus.

We need to multiply the number of pens by the number of emus in each pen:

Number of pens × emus per pen = 4 × 6 = 24 emus

Step 2: Calculate the number of female emus.

Since half of the emus are females, we divide the total number of emus by 2:

Total emus × proportion of females = 24 × (1/2) = 24 ÷ 2 = 12 female emus

Step 3: Calculate the total number of eggs in a week.

Each female emu lays 1 egg per day, so we multiply the number of female emus by the number of days in a week:

Female emus × days in a week = 12 × 7 = 84 eggs

Final Answer:

84
