Problem Statement:

Emily wants to know how much it rained last week. She sees that it rained 2 inches on Monday morning and 1 more inch later that day. It rained twice that much on Tuesday. It did not rain on Wednesday but on Thursday it rained 1 inch. The biggest storm was on Friday when the total was equal to Monday through Thursday combined. What was the daily average rain total for the week?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the total rain on Monday.

Monday had 2 inches in the morning plus 1 more inch later in the day.

Morning rain + afternoon rain = 2 inches + 1 inch = 3 inches

So Monday had a total of 3 inches of rain.

Step 2: Calculate the total rain on Tuesday.

Tuesday had twice as much rain as Monday.

2 × Monday's rain = 2 × 3 inches = 6 inches

So Tuesday had a total of 6 inches of rain.

Step 3: Calculate the total rain across Wednesday and Thursday.

Wednesday had no rain, and Thursday had 1 inch of rain.

Wednesday rain + Thursday rain = 0 inches + 1 inch = 1 inch

So Wednesday and Thursday combined had 1 inch of rain.

Step 4: Calculate the total rain on Friday.

Friday's rain was equal to Monday through Thursday combined.

Monday rain + Tuesday rain + Wednesday/Thursday rain = 3 inches + 6 inches + 1 inch = 10 inches

So Friday had a total of 10 inches of rain.

Step 5: Calculate the total rain for the week.

We need to add up all the daily rainfall amounts.

Monday rain + Tuesday rain + Wednesday/Thursday rain + Friday rain = 3 inches + 6 inches + 1 inch + 10 inches = 20 inches

So the total rainfall for the week was 20 inches.

Step 6: Calculate the daily average rain.

To find the daily average, we divide the total rainfall by the number of days in the week.

Total rain ÷ number of days = 20 inches ÷ 5 days = 4 inches per day

So the daily average rainfall was 4 inches.

Final Answer:

4
