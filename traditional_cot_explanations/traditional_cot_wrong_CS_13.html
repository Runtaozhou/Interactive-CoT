Problem Statement:

James runs a TV show and there are 5 main characters and 4 minor characters. He pays the minor characters $15,000 each episode. He paid the major characters three times as much. How much does he pay per episode?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the total pay for all minor characters.

We have 4 minor characters who each earn $15,000 per episode.

Total pay for minor characters = $15,000 × 4 = $60,000

Step 2: Calculate how much each major character gets paid.

Major characters earn three times as much as minor characters.

Pay per major character = $15,000 × 3 = $45,000

Step 3: Calculate the total pay for all major characters.

We have 5 main characters who each earn $45,000 per episode.

Total pay for major characters = $45,000 × 5 = $225,000

Step 4: Calculate the total pay per episode.

We add the total pay for major characters and minor characters.

Total pay per episode = $225,000 + $50,000 = $275,000

Final Answer:

$275,000
