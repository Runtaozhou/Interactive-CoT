Problem Statement:

Steve's new book sells 1,000,000 copies. He got an advance to pay for 100,000 copies. He gets $2 for each copy of the book sold. His agent takes 10% of that. How much money did he keep not counting the money from the advance?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the total number of copies by adding the advance copies to the sales.
Sales + Advance copies = 1,000,000 + 100,000 = 1,100,000 copies
Step 2: Calculate the total money made from selling the books.
Total copies × Payment per copy = 1,100,000 × $2 = $2,200,000
Step 3: Calculate how much the agent took.
Total money × Agent's commission = $2,200,000 × 10% = $2,200,000 × 0.1 = $220,000
Step 4: Calculate how much Steve kept after the agent's commission.
Total money - Agent's commission = $2,200,000 - $220,000 = $1,980,000

Final Answer:

1980000
