Problem Statement:

There are 20 bicycles, 10 cars and 5 motorcycles in the garage at Connor's house. How many wheels are there in the garage?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the number of bicycle wheels

Each bicycle has 2 wheels, so we multiply the number of bicycles by 2:

Number of bicycle wheels = 20 × 2 = 40 wheels

Step 2: Calculate the number of car wheels

Each car has 4 wheels, so we multiply the number of cars by 4:

Number of car wheels = 10 × 4 = 40 wheels

Step 3: Calculate the number of motorcycle wheels

Each motorcycle has 2 wheels, so we multiply the number of motorcycles by 2:

Number of motorcycle wheels = 5 × 2 = 10 wheels

Step 4: Add up all the wheels to find the total

Total wheels = Bicycle wheels + Car wheels + Motorcycle wheels

Total wheels = 40 + 40 + 5 = 85 wheels

Final Answer:

85
