Problem Statement:

Ted the T-Rex was planning to bring potato salad to the dinosaur picnic. He knows that an adult dinosaur will eat 10 lbs of potato salad, and a child will eat half as much as an adult. If there will be 20 adults and 5 children at the picnic, how many pounds of potato salad does Ted need to bring to the picnic if he hopes to have enough to feed everyone?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate how much potato salad a child dinosaur eats.

Since a child eats half as much as an adult, and an adult eats 10 pounds:

Child consumption = Adult consumption ÷ 2 = 10 ÷ 2 = 5 pounds

Step 2: Calculate the total amount needed for all adult dinosaurs.

Total for adults = Number of adults × Adult consumption = 20 × 10 = 200 pounds

Step 3: Calculate the total amount needed for all child dinosaurs.

Total for children = Number of children × Child consumption = 5 × 5 = 25 pounds

Step 4: Calculate the total amount of potato salad needed.

Total potato salad = Adult total + Child total = 200 + 25 = 225 pounds

Final Answer:

225
