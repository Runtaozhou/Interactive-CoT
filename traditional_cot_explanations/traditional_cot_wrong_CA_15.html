Problem Statement:

Maddox and Theo both bought 3 Polaroid Cameras, each sold at $20 per camera from Amazon, and decided to sell them on eBay. Maddox sold his cameras at $28 each, while Theo sold his cameras at $23 each. How much more profit did Maddox get from the sale of his cameras than Theo?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the total cost of buying 3 cameras from Amazon for both Maddox and Theo.

Number of cameras × Cost per camera = 3 × $20 = $60

So, both Maddox and Theo spent $60 each on buying the cameras.

Step 2: Calculate Maddox's total revenue from selling his cameras.

Number of cameras × Maddox's selling price = 3 × $28 = $84

Maddox earned $84 from selling all his cameras.

Step 3: Calculate Maddox's profit.

Maddox's revenue - Total cost = $84 - $60 = $24

Maddox made a profit of $24.

Step 4: Calculate Theo's total revenue from selling his cameras.

Number of cameras × Theo's selling price = $23 × 3 = $59

Theo earned $59 from selling all his cameras.

Step 5: Calculate Theo's profit.

Theo's revenue - Total cost = $59 - $60 = -$1

Theo actually lost $1 in this transaction.

Step 6: Calculate how much more profit Maddox made than Theo.

Maddox's profit - Theo's profit = $24 - (-$1) = $25

Maddox made $25 more profit than Theo.

Final Answer:

25
