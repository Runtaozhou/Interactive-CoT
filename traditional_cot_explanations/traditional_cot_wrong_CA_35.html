Problem Statement:

Martin went on an eight-hour business trip. During the first half of the trip, he traveled at a speed of 70 kilometers per hour and during the second half at a speed of 85 kilometers per hour. How many kilometers did he travel during the journey?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the duration of half the trip.

Since the total trip duration is 8 hours, half of the trip would be:

8 hours × 0.5 = 4 hours

Step 2: Calculate the distance traveled during the first half of the trip.

During the first half, Martin traveled at 70 kilometers per hour for 4 hours:

4 hours × 70 kilometers per hour = 280 kilometers

Step 3: Calculate the distance traveled during the second half of the trip.

During the second half, Martin traveled at 85 kilometers per hour for 4 hours:

4 hours × 85 kilometers per hour = 320 kilometers

Step 4: Calculate the total distance traveled during the trip.

We add the distances from both halves of the trip:

280 kilometers + 320 kilometers = 600 kilometers

Final Answer:

600
