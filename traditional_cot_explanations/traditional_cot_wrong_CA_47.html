Problem Statement:

Joshua collected 80 rocks while Jose collected 14 fewer rocks. Albert has collected 20 more rocks than Jose. How many more rocks did Albert collect than Joshua?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate how many rocks Jose collected.

We know that Joshua collected 80 rocks and Jose collected 14 fewer rocks than Joshua.

Jose's rocks = Joshua's rocks - difference

Jose's rocks = 80 - 14 = 66 rocks

Step 2: Calculate how many rocks Albert collected.

We know that Albert collected 20 more rocks than Jose, and Jose has 66 rocks.

Albert's rocks = Jose's rocks + difference

Albert's rocks = 66 + 20 = 86 rocks

Step 3: Calculate how many more rocks Albert collected than Joshua.

Now we need to find the difference between Albert's rocks and Joshua's rocks.

Difference = Albert's rocks - Joshua's rocks

Difference = 86 - 80 = 7 rocks

Final Answer:

7
