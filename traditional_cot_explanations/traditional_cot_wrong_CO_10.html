Problem Statement:

A cobbler can mend 3 pairs of shoes in an hour. From Monday to Thursday, the cobbler works for 8 hours each day, and on Friday, he only works from 8am to 11am. How many pairs of shoes can the cobbler mend in a week?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate how many pairs of shoes the cobbler can mend in one day from Monday to Thursday.

The cobbler can mend 3 pairs of shoes per hour and works 8 hours per day.

Pairs per day = 3 pairs/hour × 8 hours = 24 pairs of shoes a day

Step 2: Calculate the total pairs of shoes mended from Monday to Thursday.

The cobbler works 24 pairs per day for 3 days.

Total pairs from Monday to Thursday = 24 pairs × 3 days = 72 pairs of shoes

Step 3: Calculate how many hours the cobbler works on Friday.

On Friday, the cobbler works from 8am to 11am.

Hours worked on Friday = 11 - 8 = 3 hours

Step 4: Calculate how many pairs of shoes the cobbler can mend on Friday.

The cobbler can mend 3 pairs per hour and works for 3 hours on Friday.

Pairs on Friday = 3 pairs/hour × 3 hours = 9 pairs of shoes

Step 5: Calculate the total pairs of shoes mended in a week.

Total pairs in a week = Pairs from Monday to Thursday + Pairs on Friday

Total pairs in a week = 72 pairs + 9 pairs = 81 pairs of shoes

Final Answer:

81
