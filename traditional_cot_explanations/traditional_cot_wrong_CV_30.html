Problem Statement:

Seth is twice as old as Brooke. In 2 years, the sum of their ages will be 28. How old is Seth?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Set up an equation where x represents Brooke's age and 2x represents Seth's age, noting that in three years their ages sum to 28.

(2x + 3) + (x + 3) = 28

Where:
- Brooke's age = x
- Seth's age = 2x
Step 2: Add all values together to form a simplified equation.

3x + 6 = 28
Step 3: Subtract 6 from each side of the equation.

3x + 6 - 6 = 28 - 6
3x = 22
Step 4: Divide each side by 3 to solve for x.

x = 22/3
Brooke's age = 7.33
Step 5: Determine Brooke's age from the value of x.

x = 22/3 ≈ 7.33
Brooke's age = 7.33
Step 6: Calculate Seth's age by doubling Brooke's age.

Seth's age = 2 × Brooke's age
Seth's age = 7.33 × 2 = 14.67

Final Answer:

14.67
