Problem Statement:

Troy bakes 2 pans of brownies, cut into 16 pieces per pan. The guests eat all of 1 pan, and 75% of the 2nd pan. One tub of ice cream provides 8 scoops. If all but 4 of the guests eat the brownies ala mode (with 2 scoops of vanilla ice cream), how many tubs of ice cream did the guests eat?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the total number of brownie pieces Troy bakes.

Using the formula: pans × pieces per pan

2 × 15 = 30 brownie pieces

Step 2: Calculate how many pieces are eaten from the second pan.

Using the formula: pieces per pan × percentage eaten

15 × 0.75 = 11.25 pieces

Step 3: Calculate the total number of brownie pieces eaten.

Using the formula: first pan pieces + second pan pieces eaten

15 + 11.25 = 26.25 pieces

Step 4: Calculate how many guests eat brownies ala mode.

Using the formula: total pieces eaten - guests not eating ala mode

26.25 - 4 = 22.25

Step 5: Calculate how many scoops of ice cream are eaten.

Using the formula: guests eating ala mode × scoops per guest

22.25 × 2 = 44.5 scoops

Step 6: Calculate how many tubs of ice cream are eaten.

Using the formula: total scoops ÷ scoops per tub

44.5 ÷ 8 = 5.5625 tubs

Final Answer:

5.5625
