Problem Statement:

Levi has 5 lemons. Jayden has 6 more lemons than Levi. Jayden has one-third as many lemons as Eli has while Eli has one-half as many lemons as Ian has. How many lemons do they have in all?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate how many lemons Jayden has.

Since Jayden has 6 more lemons than Levi, and Levi has 5 lemons:

Jayden's lemons = Levi's lemons + 6 = 5 + 6 = 11 lemons

Step 2: Calculate how many lemons Eli has.

Since Jayden has one-third as many lemons as Eli, we need to multiply Jayden's lemons by 3:

Eli's lemons = Jayden's lemons × 3 = 8 × 3 = 24 lemons

Step 3: Calculate how many lemons Ian has.

Since Eli has one-half as many lemons as Ian, we need to multiply Eli's lemons by 2:

Ian's lemons = Eli's lemons × 2 = 24 × 2 = 48 lemons

Step 4: Calculate the total number of lemons.

We add up all the lemons from each person:

Total lemons = Levi's lemons + Jayden's lemons + Eli's lemons + Ian's lemons

Total lemons = 5 + 11 + 24 + 48 = 88 lemons

Final Answer:

88
