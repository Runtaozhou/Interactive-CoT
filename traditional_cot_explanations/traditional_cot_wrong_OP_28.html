Problem Statement:

Cilia wants to buy a multi-level dumbbell system. The first pair of dumbbells are 3 lb weights, the second pair is 5 lb weights and the third is 8 lb pounds. How much does the entire system weigh?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the total weight of the first pair of dumbbells.

Using the formula: weight per dumbbell × number of dumbbells

2 × 3 = 6 lbs

The first pair of dumbbells weighs 6 lbs in total.

Step 2: Calculate the total weight of the second pair of dumbbells.

Using the formula: number + weight per dumbbell

2 + 5 = 7 lbs

The second pair of dumbbells weighs 7 lbs in total.

Step 3: Calculate the total weight of the third pair of dumbbells.

Using the formula: weight per dumbbell × number of dumbbells

2 × 8 = 16 lbs

The third pair of dumbbells weighs 16 lbs in total.

Step 4: Add up the weights of all three pairs to find the total weight of the system.

Using the formula: first_pair_weight + second_pair_weight + third_pair_weight

6 + 7 + 16 = 29 lbs

The entire dumbbell system weighs 29 lbs.

Final Answer:

29
