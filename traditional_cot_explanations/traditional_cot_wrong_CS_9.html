Problem Statement:

James hurt himself exercising. The pain subsided after 3 days, but he knew that the injury would take at least 5 times that long to fully heal. After that, he wanted to wait another 3 days before he started working out again. If he wants to wait 3 weeks after that to start lifting heavy again, how long until he can lift heavy again?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate how long the injury would take to fully heal.

The pain subsided after 3 days, and the injury takes 5 times that long to fully heal.

Healing time = 3 days × 5 = 15 days

Step 2: Calculate when he would start exercising again.

After the injury heals, James wants to wait an additional 3 days before starting to work out.

Time until he can start exercising = Healing time + Additional wait

Time until he can start exercising = 15 days + 3 days = 18 days

Step 3: Calculate how many days he needs to wait before lifting heavy.

James wants to wait 3 weeks after he starts exercising before lifting heavy weights.

Wait time before lifting heavy = 3 weeks × 7 days per week = 21 days

Step 4: Calculate the total time until he can lift heavy again.

Total time = Time until he can start exercising + Wait time before lifting heavy

Total time = 18 days + 24 days = 42 days

Final Answer:

42 days
