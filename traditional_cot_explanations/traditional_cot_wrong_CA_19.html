Problem Statement:

There are 325 students in a local high school. 40 percent of the students have glasses, how many students do not have glasses?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the percentage of students who do not have glasses.

If 40% of students have glasses, then the percentage of students who don't have glasses is:

100% - 40% = 60%

So 60% of students do not have glasses.

Step 2: Find 10% of the total number of students.

To find 10% of 325 students:

325 × 0.10 = 32.5 students

So 10% of the total student population is 32.5 students.

Step 3: Multiply the 10% value by 6 to get 60% (students without glasses).

Since 60% is 6 times 10%, we can multiply our 10% value by 6:

32.5 × 6 = 190 students

Therefore, 190 students do not have glasses.

Final Answer:

190
