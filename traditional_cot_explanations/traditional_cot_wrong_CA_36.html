Problem Statement:

Jackson wants to go on a shopping spree, so his mom says she will give him some spending money if he does extra chores. She promises $5 per hour spent on chores. Jackson spends 2 hours vacuuming, and decides to do this twice. He also spends 0.5 hours washing dishes, and three times as long cleaning the bathroom. How much spending money has Jackson earned?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the total time Jackson spent vacuuming.

Jackson spent 2 hours vacuuming and did this twice.

Total vacuuming time = 2 hours × 2 = 4 hours

Step 2: Calculate the money earned from vacuuming.

Jackson earns $5 per hour, so for vacuuming:

Money earned from vacuuming = 4 hours × $5 per hour = $20

Step 3: Calculate the money earned from washing dishes.

Jackson spent 0.5 hours washing dishes.

Money earned from washing dishes = 0.5 hours × $5 per hour = $2.5

Step 4: Calculate the time spent cleaning the bathroom.

Jackson spent three times as long cleaning the bathroom as washing dishes.

Bathroom cleaning time = 0.5 hours × 3 = 1.5 hours

Step 5: Calculate the money earned from cleaning the bathroom.

Money earned from bathroom cleaning = 1.5 hours × $5 per hour = $9

Step 6: Calculate the total money earned from all chores.

Total money earned = $20 (vacuuming) + $2.5 (dishes) + $9 (bathroom) = $31.5

Final Answer:

$31.5
