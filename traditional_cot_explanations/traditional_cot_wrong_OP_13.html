Problem Statement:

Valerie earns $5000 per month, 1/2 of what her brother earns. If their mother earns twice their combined salary, what's the total amount of money they all have together?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate how much Valerie's brother earns.

Since Valerie earns 1/2 of what her brother earns, her brother earns twice as much as Valerie.

Brother's earnings = Valerie's earnings × 2

Brother's earnings = $5000 × 2 = $10000

Step 2: Calculate the combined salary of Valerie and her brother.

Combined salary = Valerie's earnings + brother's earnings

Combined salary = $5000 + $10000 = $15000

Step 3: Calculate their mother's earnings.

Mother's earnings = combined salary × 2

Mother's earnings = $15000 × 2 = $30000

Step 4: Calculate the total amount of money they all have together.

Total money = combined salary + mother's earnings

Total money = $15000 + $30000 = $45000

Final Answer:

18000
