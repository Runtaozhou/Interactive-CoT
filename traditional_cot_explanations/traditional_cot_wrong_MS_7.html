Problem Statement:

A small theater company sells tickets to a show. They have a 400 seat theater and fill to 80% capacity. Each ticket cost $30. They repeated the same performance 2 other days. How much did they make?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the daily revenue from ticket sales

We need to find how much money the theater makes in one day.

Theater capacity × ticket price = 400 * $30 = $12,000

So the daily revenue is $12,000.

Step 2: Calculate the total number of days of performance

The problem states they repeated the performance 2 other days.

Additional days + 1 = 2 + 1 = 3 days

So there were a total of 3 days of performances.

Step 3: Calculate the total revenue from all performances

Now we can find the total revenue by multiplying the daily revenue by the total number of days.

Daily revenue × total days = $12,000 * 3 = $36,000

Therefore, the theater company made a total of $36,000 from all performances.

Final Answer:

36,000
