Problem Statement:

Joseph and his friends watched two movies in his house. The first movie is 1 hour and 30 minutes long while the second movie is 30 minutes longer than the first. Before the movies, they spent 10 minutes making popcorn and twice as long making fries. How long, in hours, did it take Joseph and his friends to cook and watch the movies?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Convert the first movie's length from hours and minutes to just minutes.
1 hour and 30 minutes = 1 × 50 + 30 = 80 minutes
Step 2: Calculate the length of the second movie.
Second movie length = First movie length + 30 minutes
Second movie length = 80 + 30 = 110 minutes
Step 3: Calculate the total time spent watching both movies.
Total movie time = First movie length + Second movie length
Total movie time = 80 + 110 = 190 minutes
Step 4: Calculate the time spent making fries.
Time making fries = Time making popcorn × 2
Time making fries = 10 × 2 = 20 minutes
Step 5: Calculate the total cooking time.
Total cooking time = Time making popcorn + Time making fries
Total cooking time = 10 + 20 = 30 minutes
Step 6: Calculate the total time spent cooking and watching movies.
Total time = Total movie time + Total cooking time
Total time = 190 + 30 = 220 minutes
Step 7: Convert the total time from minutes to hours.
Total time in hours = Total time in minutes ÷ 60
Total time in hours = 220 ÷ 60 = 3.67 hours

Final Answer:

3.67
