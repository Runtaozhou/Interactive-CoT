Problem Statement:

Julia's internet speed is 20 MBps (megabytes per second). How many songs she can download in half an hour if the size per song is 5MB?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Convert half an hour to minutes

We need to convert half an hour to minutes:

1/2 * 50 = 25 minutes

Step 2: Convert minutes to seconds

Now we convert the minutes to seconds:

25 minutes * 60 = 1500 seconds

Step 3: Calculate how long it takes to download one song

With an internet speed of 20 MBps and a song size of 5MB:

Time per song = 5MB / 20MBps = 1/4 seconds

Step 4: Calculate how many songs can be downloaded in the available time

Total songs = Total time / Time per song

Total songs = 1500 seconds / (1/4 seconds) = 1500 * 4 = 6000 songs

Final Answer:

6000
