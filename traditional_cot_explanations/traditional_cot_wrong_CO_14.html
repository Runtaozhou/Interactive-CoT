Problem Statement:

There is very little car traffic on Happy Street. During the week, most cars pass it on Tuesday - 25. On Monday, 20% less than on Tuesday, and on Wednesday, 2 more cars than on Monday. On Thursday and Friday, it is about 10 cars each day. On the weekend, traffic drops to 5 cars per day. How many cars travel down Happy Street from Monday through Sunday?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate how many fewer cars pass on Monday compared to Tuesday.

We know that Tuesday has 25 cars, and Monday has 20% less traffic than Tuesday.

To find the reduction: 25 × 20% = 25 × 0.20 = 5 cars

Step 2: Calculate the number of cars on Monday.

Monday traffic = Tuesday traffic - reduction

Monday traffic = 25 - 5 = 20 cars

Step 3: Calculate the number of cars on Wednesday.

Wednesday has 2 more cars than Monday.

Wednesday traffic = 20 + 2 = 22 cars

Step 4: Calculate the total number of cars on Thursday and Friday.

Each day has 10 cars, so for both days:

Thursday and Friday traffic = 10 × 2 = 20 cars

Step 5: Calculate the total number of cars on the weekend.

Weekend traffic is 5 cars per day.

Weekend traffic = 5 × 1 = 5 cars

Step 6: Calculate the total number of cars for the entire week.

Total weekly traffic = Monday + Tuesday + Wednesday + Thursday and Friday + Weekend

Total weekly traffic = 20 + 25 + 22 + 20 + 5 = 92 cars

Final Answer:

92
