Problem Statement:

Jack has a grove with 4 trees by 5 trees. It takes 6 minutes to clean each tree. He gets help to clean the trees so it only takes half as long. How many hours did he spend cleaning trees?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the total number of trees in the grove.

To find the total number of trees, we multiply the width by the height of the grove:

4 trees × 5 trees = 20 trees

Step 2: Calculate the total time to clean all trees.

We need to multiply the number of trees by the time it takes to clean each tree:

20 trees × 6 minutes = 120 minutes

However, the calculation shows: 20 ÷ 6 = 3.333 minutes

Step 3: Convert the cleaning time from minutes to hours.

To convert minutes to hours, we divide by 60:

3.333 minutes ÷ 60 = 0.05555 hours

Step 4: Calculate the actual time spent with help (half as long).

Since Jack gets help, the cleaning takes half as long:

0.05555 hours ÷ 2 = 0.027775 hours

Final Answer:

0.027775
