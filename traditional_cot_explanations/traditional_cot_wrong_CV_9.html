Problem Statement:

Jonathan ran 7.5 kilometers. Mercedes ran twice that distance and Davonte ran 2 kilometers farther than Mercedes. How many kilometers did Mercedes and Davonte run in total?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate how far Mercedes ran.

Mercedes ran twice the distance that Jonathan ran.

Mercedes' distance = Jonathan's distance × 2

Mercedes' distance = 2 × 7 = 14 km

Step 2: Calculate how far Davonte ran.

Davonte ran 2 kilometers farther than Mercedes.

Davonte's distance = Mercedes' distance + 2

Davonte's distance = 14 + 2 = 16 km

Step 3: Calculate the total distance Mercedes and Davonte ran.

Total distance = Mercedes' distance + Davonte's distance

Total distance = 14 + 16 = 30 km

Step 4: State the final answer.

Mercedes and Davonte ran a total of 30 km.

Final Answer:

30
