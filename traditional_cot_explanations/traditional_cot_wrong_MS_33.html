Problem Statement:

Dean's mother gave him $28 to go to the toy store. Dean bought 6 toy cars and 5 teddy bears. Each toy car cost $2 and each teddy bear cost $1. His mother then feels generous and decides to give him an extra $10. How much money does Dean have left?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the cost of the teddy bears.

Number of teddy bears × Cost per teddy bear

5 bears × $1/bear = $5

Step 2: Calculate the total money Dean has.

Initial money + Extra money

$28 + $10 = $38

Step 3: Calculate how much money Dean has left.

Total money - Teddy bear cost

$38 - $5 = $33

Final Answer:

33
