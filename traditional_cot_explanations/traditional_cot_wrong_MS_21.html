Problem Statement:

If Patty's dress was $10 more than Ida's dress, and Ida's dress was $30 more than Jean's dress, and Jean's dress was $10 less than Pauline's dress, and lastly Pauline's dress was $30, how much did all the ladies spend on dressed put together?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the cost of Jean's dress based on Pauline's dress.

We know that Pauline's dress costs $30 and Jean's dress is $10 less than Pauline's dress.

Jean's dress = Pauline's dress - $10 = $30 - $10 = $20

Step 2: Calculate the cost of Ida's dress based on Jean's dress.

We know that Ida's dress is $30 more than Jean's dress.

Ida's dress = Jean's dress + $30 = $30 + $30 = $60

Step 3: Calculate the cost of Patty's dress based on Ida's dress.

We know that Patty's dress is $10 more than Ida's dress.

Patty's dress = Ida's dress + $10 = $60 + $10 = $70

Step 4: Calculate the total cost of all four dresses.

Total cost = Patty's dress + Ida's dress + Jean's dress + Pauline's dress

Total cost = $70 + $60 + $20 + $30 = $180

Final Answer:

180
