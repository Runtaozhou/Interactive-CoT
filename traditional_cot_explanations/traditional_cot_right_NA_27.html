Problem Statement:

Tim spends 6 hours each day at work answering phones. It takes him 15 minutes to deal with a call. How many calls does he deal with during his 5 day work week?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Convert hours to minutes to find total minutes spent on calls per day.

We need to convert Tim's 6 hours of daily work time into minutes:

6 hours × 60 minutes = 360 minutes

Tim spends 360 minutes each day answering phones.

Step 2: Calculate how many calls Tim can handle per day.

Since each call takes 15 minutes, we can find the number of calls per day by dividing the total minutes by the time per call:

360 minutes ÷ 15 minutes per call = 24 calls

Tim can handle 24 calls each day.

Step 3: Calculate the total number of calls in a work week.

To find the total number of calls Tim handles in a 5-day work week, we multiply the daily calls by the number of work days:

24 calls per day × 5 days = 120 calls

Tim handles 120 calls during his work week.

Final Answer:

120
