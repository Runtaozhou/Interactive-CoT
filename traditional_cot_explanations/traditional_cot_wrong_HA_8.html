Problem Statement:

Marc bought 5 model cars that cost $20 each and 5 bottles of paint that cost $10 each. He also bought 5 paintbrushes that cost $2 each. How much did Marc spend in total?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the total cost of the model cars.

We need to multiply the number of cars by the cost per car:

5 cars × $20 per car = $100

So, the total cost of model cars is $100.

Step 2: Calculate the total cost of the bottles of paint.

We need to multiply the number of bottles by the cost per bottle:

5 bottles × $10 per bottle = $50

So, the total cost of paint bottles is $50.

Step 3: Calculate the total cost of the paintbrushes and add the cost of a model display case.

We need to multiply the number of paintbrushes by the cost per paintbrush and add the display case cost:

5 paintbrushes × $2 per paintbrush = $10

Plus $30 for the display case

So, the total cost of paintbrushes and display case is $10 + $30 = $40.

Step 4: Calculate the total amount Marc spent.

We need to add all the costs together:

Model cars cost + Paint bottles cost + Paintbrushes cost + Display case cost

$100 + $50 + $10 + $30 = $190

So, the total amount Marc spent is $190.

Final Answer:

$190
