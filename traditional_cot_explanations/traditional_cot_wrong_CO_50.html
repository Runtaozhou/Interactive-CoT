Problem Statement:

Ludwig works 7 days a week and he usually works half of the day during Friday, Saturday, and Sunday. If his daily salary is $10, how much does he earn every week?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate Ludwig's earnings from Monday to Thursday.

We know that Ludwig's daily salary is $10, and he works full days from Monday to Thursday (4 days).

Earnings from Monday to Thursday = Daily salary × Number of days

Earnings from Monday to Thursday = $10 × 4 = $40

Step 2: Calculate Ludwig's daily earnings for Friday, Saturday, and Sunday.

During these days, Ludwig works half days, so his daily earnings are half of his regular daily salary.

Weekend daily earnings = Daily salary ÷ 2

Weekend daily earnings = $10 ÷ 2 = $5 per day

Step 3: Calculate Ludwig's total earnings for Friday, Saturday, and Sunday.

We need to multiply the weekend daily earnings by the number of weekend days.

Weekend total earnings = Weekend daily earnings × Number of weekend days

Weekend total earnings = $5 × 2 = $10

Step 4: Calculate Ludwig's total weekly earnings.

We add the earnings from Monday to Thursday to the earnings from Friday to Sunday.

Total weekly earnings = Weekday earnings + Weekend total earnings

Total weekly earnings = $40 + $10 = $50

Final Answer:

50
