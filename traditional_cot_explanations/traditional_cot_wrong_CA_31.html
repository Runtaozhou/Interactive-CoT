Problem Statement:

Iris' family is planning a surprise birthday party for her. The party will include her 3 uncles and 4 aunts who have a son and daughter each as well as her brother and mother. In total, how many people are coming to Iris' birthday party?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the number of people in each aunt/uncle family unit.

Each family unit consists of 1 son, 1 daughter, and 1 aunt/uncle.

So each family unit has: 1 + 1 + 1 = 3 people

Therefore, each family unit has 4 people.

Step 2: Calculate the total number of aunts and uncles.

Total number of aunts and uncles = 3 uncles + 4 aunts = 7 people

Step 3: Calculate the total number of people from the aunt/uncle families.

Total people from aunt/uncle families = 7 Ã— 4 = 28 people

Step 4: Add her mother and brother to get the total number of people.

Total people = 28 + 1 (mother) + 1 (brother) = 30 people

Final Answer:

30
