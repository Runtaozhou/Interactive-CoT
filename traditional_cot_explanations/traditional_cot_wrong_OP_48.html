Problem Statement:

Jimmy is at the candy store and buys 2 candy bars for $.75 each. He then buys 4 lollipops that cost $.25 each. He spent 1/6 of the money he earned from shoveling snow. If he charges $1.5 per driveway, how many driveways did he shovel?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the total cost of candy bars.

We need to multiply the number of candy bars by the price per candy bar:

2 × $0.75 = $1.50

Step 2: Calculate the total cost of lollipops.

We need to multiply the number of lollipops by the price per lollipop:

4 × $0.25 = $1.00

Step 3: Calculate the total amount Jimmy spent at the candy store.

We add the cost of candy bars and lollipops:

$1.50 + $1.00 = $2.50

Step 4: Calculate how much money Jimmy earned from shoveling snow.

Since Jimmy spent 1/6 of his snow shoveling earnings, we can calculate the total earnings:

$2.50 × (1/6) = $0.4166666667

Step 5: Calculate the number of driveways Jimmy shoveled.

We divide Jimmy's snow shoveling earnings by the charge per driveway:

$0.4166666667 ÷ $1.50 = 0.277777778 driveways

Final Answer:

0.277777778
