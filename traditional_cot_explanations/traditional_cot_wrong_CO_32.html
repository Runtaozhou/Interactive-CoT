Problem Statement:

Mitch is a freelancer, she works 5 hours every day from Monday to Friday and 3 hours every Saturday and Sunday. If she earns $3 per hour and earns double on weekends, how much does she earn every week?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate how much Mitch earns per weekday.

We need to multiply the hours worked per weekday by the hourly rate:

5 hours × $3 per hour = $15 per weekday

Step 2: Calculate how much Mitch earns for all weekdays.

We multiply the daily weekday earnings by the number of weekdays (Monday to Friday):

$15 per day × 5 days = $75 total weekday earnings

Step 3: Calculate Mitch's hourly rate on weekends.

Since Mitch earns double on weekends, we multiply the weekday rate by 2:

$3 per hour × 2 = $6 per hour on weekends

Step 4: Calculate how much Mitch earns for her weekend hours.

We multiply the weekend hourly rate by the hours worked per weekend day:

$6 per hour × 3 hours = $18 per weekend day

Step 5: Calculate total weekend earnings.

We multiply the daily weekend earnings by the number of weekend days:

$18 per day × 1 day = $18 total weekend earnings

Step 6: Calculate total weekly earnings.

We add the total weekday earnings and the total weekend earnings:

$75 (weekday earnings) + $18 (weekend earnings) = $93 total weekly earnings

Final Answer:

93
