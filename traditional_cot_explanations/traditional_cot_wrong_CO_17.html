Problem Statement:

Ludwig works 7 days a week and he usually works half of the day during Friday, Saturday, and Sunday. If his daily salary is $10, how much does he earn every week?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate Ludwig's earnings from Monday to Thursday.

Since Ludwig works full days from Monday to Thursday and earns $10 per day, we multiply his daily salary by the number of days:

$10 × 4 = $40

Ludwig earns $40 from Monday to Thursday.

Step 2: Calculate Ludwig's daily earnings for Friday, Saturday, and Sunday.

Since Ludwig works half days on Friday, Saturday, and Sunday, his daily earnings for these days will be half of his regular daily salary:

$10 ÷ 2 = $5

Ludwig earns $5 per day on Friday, Saturday, and Sunday.

Step 3: Calculate Ludwig's total earnings for Friday, Saturday, and Sunday.

To find the total earnings for these three days, we multiply the half-day rate by the number of weekend days:

$5 × 2 = $10

Ludwig earns $10 in total for Friday, Saturday, and Sunday.

Step 4: Calculate Ludwig's total weekly earnings.

To find the total weekly earnings, we add the earnings from Monday to Thursday to the earnings from Friday, Saturday, and Sunday:

$40 + $10 = $50

Ludwig earns $50 every week.

Final Answer:

50
