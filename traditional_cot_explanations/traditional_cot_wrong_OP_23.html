Problem Statement:

Carl buys index cards for his class. He gives each student 10 index cards. He teaches 6 periods a day and each class has 30 students. If a 50 pack of index cards cost $3 how much did he spend on them all?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the total number of students.

Since Carl teaches 6 periods a day with 30 students in each class, we multiply:

30 students per class × 6 periods = 180 total students

Step 2: Calculate the total number of index cards needed.

Each student needs 10 index cards, so we multiply the total number of students by 10:

180 students × 10 index cards per student = 1800 total index cards needed

Step 3: Calculate the number of index cards to buy.

We need to determine how many index cards Carl will buy:

1800 total index cards × 50 pack size = 90000 index cards

Step 4: Calculate the total cost.

Now we can calculate how much Carl spent on all the index cards:

90000 index cards × $3 per pack = $270,000

Final Answer:

$270,000
