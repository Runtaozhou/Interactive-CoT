Bills + 3 = 2 + 3 = 5 mail-in rebates

Rebates × 2 = 5 × 2 = 10 job applications
Step 5: Now, let's calculate the total number of pieces of mail Valerie needs to send:
Thank you cards + Bills + Rebates + Applications = 3 + 2 + 5 + 10 = 20 pieces of mail
Step 6: Finally, we need to calculate the total number of stamps needed. Each piece of mail requires 1 stamp, except for the electric bill which requires 2 stamps. So the total number of stamps is:
Total mail + Extra stamp for electric bill = 20 + 1 = 21 stamps
