Problem Statement:

While Joanne is gathering apples from her family's orchard, her sister comes outside to help her. Joanne gathers 30 apples from the tallest trees, half this amount from the shortest trees, and more apples from the average trees. Compared with Joanne, her sister gathers twice as many apples from the tallest trees and 3 times as many apples from the shortest trees. She doesn't take any from the average trees. If the sisters have gathered a combined total of 500 apples, how many apples did Joanne gather from the average trees?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate how many apples Joanne gathered from the shortest trees.

Since Joanne gathered half as many apples from the shortest trees as from the tallest trees:

Joanne's shortest trees apples = 30 apples ÷ 2 = 15 apples

Step 2: Calculate how many apples her sister gathered from the tallest trees.

Her sister gathered twice as many apples from the tallest trees as Joanne did:

Sister's tallest trees apples = 30 apples × 2 = 60 apples

Step 3: Calculate how many apples her sister gathered from the shortest trees.

Her sister gathered 3 times as many apples from the shortest trees as Joanne did:

Sister's shortest trees apples = 15 apples × 3 = 45 apples

Step 4: Calculate the total apples her sister gathered.

Sister's total apples = Sister's tallest trees apples + Sister's shortest trees apples

Sister's total apples = 60 apples + 45 apples = 105 apples

Step 5: Calculate how many apples Joanne gathered from tallest and shortest trees.

Joanne's tallest and shortest trees apples = Joanne's tallest trees apples + Joanne's shortest trees apples

Joanne's tallest and shortest trees apples = 30 apples + 15 apples = 45 apples

Step 6: Calculate the combined total of apples gathered so far.

Apples gathered so far = Sister's total apples + Joanne's tallest and shortest trees apples

Apples gathered so far = 105 apples + 45 apples = 140 apples

Step 7: Calculate how many apples Joanne must have gathered from the average trees.

Joanne's average trees apples = Total apples - Apples gathered so far

Joanne's average trees apples = 500 apples - 140 apples = 360 apples

Final Answer:

360
