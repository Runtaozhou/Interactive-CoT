Problem Statement:

Every Sunday, Sean picks up 1 almond croissant and 1 salami and cheese croissant that are $4.50 each. He also grabs a plain croissant for $3.00 and a loaf of focaccia for $4.00. On his way home he stops and picks up 2 lattes for $2.50 each. How much did he spend?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the cost of the flavored croissants.

Sean buys 1 almond croissant and 1 salami and cheese croissant at $4.50 each.

Total cost for flavored croissants = 2 × $4.50 = $9.00

Step 2: Calculate the cost of the lattes.

Sean buys 2 lattes at $2.50 each.

Total cost for lattes = 2 × $2.50 = $5.00

Step 3: Recalculate the cost of lattes with an additional latte.

Adding one more latte to the previous calculation.

New total cost for lattes = 2 × $2.50 + $2.50 = $7.50

Step 4: Calculate the total cost of all items.

We need to add the cost of flavored croissants, lattes, plain croissant, and focaccia.

Total cost = $9.00 + $7.50 + $3.00 + $4.00 = $23.50

Final Answer:

$23.50
