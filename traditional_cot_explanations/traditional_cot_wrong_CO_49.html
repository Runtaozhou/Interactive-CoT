Problem Statement:

Scott runs 3 miles every Monday through Wednesday. Then he runs twice as far he ran on Monday every Thursday and Friday. How many miles will he run in a month with 4 weeks?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate how many miles Scott runs on Thursday and Friday.

Since Scott runs twice as far as Monday on Thursday and Friday, and he runs 3 miles on Monday:

Miles on Thursday and Friday = 3 × 2 = 6 miles per day

Step 2: Calculate the total miles Scott runs Monday through Wednesday in a week.

Scott runs 3 miles per day for 3 days (Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday):

Weekly miles (Mon-Wed) = 3 miles × 3 days = 9 miles

Step 3: Calculate the total miles Scott runs Thursday and Friday in a week.

Scott runs 6 miles per day for 2 days (Thursday, Friday):

Weekly miles (Thu-Fri) = 6 miles × 3 days = 18 miles

Step 4: Calculate the total miles Scott runs in a week.

Add the miles from Monday-Wednesday and Thursday-Friday:

Total weekly miles = 9 miles + 18 miles = 27 miles

Step 5: Calculate the total miles Scott runs in a month.

Since there are 4 weeks in the month:

Total monthly miles = 27 miles × 4 weeks = 108 miles

Final Answer:

108
