Problem Statement:

Dexter has five pigs. Each one eats 4 pounds of feed, twice a day. If Dexter ordered 300 pounds of feed, how many pounds of feed is left after a week?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate how much feed one pig consumes in a day.

Each pig eats 4 pounds of feed twice a day.

Feed per day = 4 + 2 = 6 pounds

Step 2: Calculate how much feed all pigs consume in a day.

Dexter has 5 pigs, and each pig consumes 6 pounds per day.

Total daily feed = 6 × 5 = 30 pounds

Step 3: Calculate how much feed all pigs consume in a week.

A week has 7 days, and the total daily feed consumption is 30 pounds.

Weekly feed consumption = 30 × 7 = 210 pounds

Step 4: Calculate how much feed is left after a week.

Dexter ordered 300 pounds of feed, and the pigs consume 210 pounds in a week.

Feed remaining = 300 - 210 = 90 pounds

Final Answer:

90
