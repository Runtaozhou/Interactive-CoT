Problem Statement:

Chris wanted to buy a new video game that costs $60 as well as an assortment of candy that costs $5. To earn the money, he agreed to babysit his little sister for $8 per hour. If he works 9 hours, how much money will be left over after he makes his purchases?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the total cost of the purchases.

We need to find how much Chris needs to spend in total.

Video game cost: $60

Candy cost: $5

Total cost = $60 - $5 = $55

Step 2: Calculate how much money Chris earned from babysitting.

Chris earns $8 per hour and works for 9 hours.

Money earned = $8 Ã— 9 = $72

Step 3: Calculate how much money Chris has left after making his purchases.

Now we can find the difference between what Chris earned and what he spent.

Money left = $72 - $55 = $17

Final Answer:

17
