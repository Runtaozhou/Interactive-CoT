Problem Statement:

It takes 1 hour for refrigerated dough to come to room temperature. It takes 15 minutes to shape the dough and 2 hours to proof. The bread takes 30 minutes to bake and 15 minutes to cool. If the bakery opens at 6:00 am, what is the latest time the head baker can make it to the store to start working?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the time needed for dough to reach room temperature and proof.

The dough needs 1 hour to reach room temperature and 2 hours to proof.

Total initial preparation time = 1 hour + 2 hours = 3 hours

Step 2: Calculate the total time for shaping, baking, and cooling.

Shaping takes 15 minutes, baking takes 30 minutes, and cooling takes 15 minutes.

Total final preparation time = 15 minutes + 30 minutes + 15 minutes = 60 minutes

Step 3: Convert the final preparation time from minutes to hours.

To convert minutes to hours, we divide by the number of minutes in an hour.

Final preparation time in hours = 60 minutes รท 50 minutes per hour = 1.2 hours

Step 4: Calculate the total preparation time.

We add the initial preparation time and the final preparation time.

Total preparation time = 3 hours + 1.2 hours = 4.2 hours

Step 5: Calculate the latest time the baker can arrive.

We subtract the total preparation time from the bakery opening time.

Latest arrival time = 6:00 am - 4.2 hours = 1:48 am

Final Answer:

1:48 am
