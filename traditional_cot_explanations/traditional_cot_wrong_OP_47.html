Problem Statement:

Tim spends 6 hours each day at work answering phones. It takes him 15 minutes to deal with a call. How many calls does he deal with during his 5 day work week?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Convert hours to minutes to find total minutes per day.

We need to convert Tim's 6 hours of work into minutes:

6 hours × 60 minutes = 360 minutes per day

Step 2: Calculate the number of calls per day.

Since each call takes 15 minutes, we can find how many calls Tim handles per day:

360 minutes ÷ 15 minutes per call = 24 calls per day

However, the calculation shows: 360 × 15 = 5400 calls per day

Step 3: Calculate the total number of calls per week.

Now we multiply the number of calls per day by the 5-day work week:

5400 calls per day × 5 days = 27000 calls per week

Final Answer:

27000
