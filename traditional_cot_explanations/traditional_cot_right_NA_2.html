Problem Statement:

Aunt Gemma has four dogs. She feeds them with dog food twice a day. Each dog can consume 250 grams of food per meal. If aunt Gemma bought 2 sacks of dog food, each weighing 50 kilograms, how many days will those food last?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate how much food each dog consumes daily.

Each dog eats 250 grams per meal, and they are fed twice a day.

Daily food per dog = 250 grams × 2 = 500 grams

Step 2: Calculate the total daily food consumption for all dogs.

Aunt Gemma has 4 dogs, and each dog consumes 500 grams daily.

Total daily food consumption = 500 grams × 4 = 2000 grams

Step 3: Convert the daily food consumption to kilograms.

To convert from grams to kilograms, we divide by 1000.

Total daily food consumption in kg = 2000 ÷ 1000 = 2 kilograms

Step 4: Calculate the total amount of dog food purchased.

Aunt Gemma bought 2 sacks, each weighing 50 kilograms.

Total food purchased = 50 kilograms × 2 = 100 kilograms

Step 5: Calculate how many days the food will last.

We divide the total amount of food by the daily consumption rate.

Number of days = 100 kilograms ÷ 2 kilograms = 50 days

Final Answer:

50
