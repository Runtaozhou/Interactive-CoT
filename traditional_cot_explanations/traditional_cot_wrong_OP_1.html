Problem Statement:

Bailey needs to buy 2 new sets of towels for the guest bathroom and 4 new sets for her master bathroom. The set of towels for the guest bathroom are $40.00 each and the master bathroom set is $50.00 each. The store is currently offering 20% off so how much will Bailey spend on towel sets?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the cost of guest bathroom towel sets.

We need to multiply the number of sets by the price per set:

2 sets × $40.00 per set = $80.00

Step 2: Calculate the cost of master bathroom towel sets.

We need to multiply the number of sets by the price per set:

4 sets × $50.00 per set = $200.00

Step 3: Calculate the total cost before discount.

We add the cost of guest bathroom towel sets and master bathroom towel sets:

$80.00 + $200.00 = $280.00

Step 4: Calculate the discount amount.

We multiply the total before discount by the discount percentage:

$280.00 × 0.20 = $56.00

Step 5: Calculate the final price after discount.

We add the total before discount and the discount amount:

$280.00 + $56.00 = $336.00

Final Answer:

336
