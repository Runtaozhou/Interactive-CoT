Problem Statement:

Yoque borrowed money from her sister. She promised to pay it back in 11 months including an additional 10% of the money she borrowed. If she pays $15 per month, how much money did she borrow?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the total amount Yoque will pay over 11 months.

Monthly payment × Number of months = $15 × 11 = $165

So the total payment Yoque will make is $165.

Step 2: Determine what percentage of the borrowed amount the total payment represents.

Since Yoque is paying back the original amount (100%) plus an additional 10%, the total payment represents 110% of the borrowed amount.

Original percentage + Additional percentage = 100% + 10% = 110%

Step 3: Calculate the value of 1% of the borrowed amount.

If 110% of the borrowed amount equals $165, then 1% equals $165 ÷ 110 = $1.50

Step 4: Calculate the original borrowed amount.

The borrowed amount is 100% of the original value, so we multiply the value of 1% by 100.

Borrowed amount = $1.50 × 100 = $148

Final Answer:

148
