Problem Statement:

The building has 42 storage units that are a total of 5040 square feet. Twenty of the units are 8 by 4 feet each. If the remaining units are all the same size, what is the area, in square feet, for one of the remaining units?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the perimeter of one of the 20 units.

The perimeter of one unit is 8 + 4 + 8 + 4 = 24 square feet.

Step 2: Calculate the total area of the 20 units.

The total area of the 20 units is 20 ร 24 = 480 square feet.

Step 3: Find the remaining area for the other units.

The remaining area is the total area minus the area of the 20 units: 5040 - 480 = 4560 square feet.

Step 4: Calculate the number of remaining units.

The number of remaining units is the total units minus 20: 42 - 20 = 22 remaining units.

Step 5: Calculate the area of one remaining unit.

The area of one remaining unit is the remaining area divided by the number of remaining units: 4560 รท 22 = 207.2727 square feet.

Step 6: State the final answer.

One of the remaining units is 207.2727 square feet.
