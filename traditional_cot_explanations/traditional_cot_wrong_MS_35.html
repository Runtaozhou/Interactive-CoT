Problem Statement:

There is a playground that is surrounded by a square fence that has a side length of 27 yards. There is a 12 yard by 9 yard garden that has fencing around it. How many yards of fencing do the playground and garden have together?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the total length of the square fence around the playground.

To find the perimeter of a square, we multiply the side length by 4.

Perimeter = 27 yards × 4 = 108 yards

So the playground fence is 108 yards long.

Step 2: Calculate the fencing for the garden.

Using the formula: 2 × (side length)

Garden fence = 2 × (27) = 54 yards

Step 3: Add the playground fence and garden fence to find the total fencing.

Total fencing = Playground fence + Garden fence

Total fencing = 108 yards + 54 yards = 162 yards

Final Answer:

162
