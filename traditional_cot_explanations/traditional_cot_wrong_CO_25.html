Problem Statement:

Scott runs 3 miles every Monday through Wednesday. Then he runs twice as far he ran on Monday every Thursday and Friday. How many miles will he run in a month with 4 weeks?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate how many miles Scott runs on Thursday and Friday each day.

Since Scott runs twice as far on Thursday and Friday as he does on Monday:

Monday miles × 2 = 3 × 2 = 6 miles

So Scott runs 6 miles each day on Thursday and Friday.

Step 2: Calculate the total miles Scott runs Monday through Wednesday in a week.

Scott runs 3 miles per day for 3 days (Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday):

3 miles × 3 days = 9 miles

So Scott runs a total of 9 miles from Monday through Wednesday each week.

Step 3: Calculate the total miles Scott runs Thursday and Friday in a week.

Scott runs 6 miles per day for 2 days (Thursday and Friday):

6 miles × 2 days = 12 miles

So Scott runs a total of 12 miles on Thursday and Friday each week.

Step 4: Calculate the total miles Scott runs in one week.

Add the miles from Monday through Wednesday to the miles from Thursday and Friday:

9 miles + 12 miles = 21 miles

So Scott runs a total of 21 miles each week.

Step 5: Calculate the total miles Scott runs in a month.

Multiply the weekly total by the number of weeks in the month:

21 miles × 4 weeks = 84 miles

So Scott runs a total of 84 miles in a month with 4 weeks.

Final Answer:

108
