Problem Statement:

A marathon is 26 miles. He can run the first 10 miles in 1 hour. For the remaining miles he runs at 80% that pace. How long does the race take?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the remaining miles after the first 10 miles.

Total miles - First miles = Remaining miles

26 - 10 = 16 miles

Remaining miles: 16 miles

Step 2: Calculate the new pace for the remaining miles.

Initial pace * 0.8 = New pace

10 miles per hour * 0.8 = 8 miles per hour

New pace: 8 miles per hour

Step 3: Calculate the time needed for the remaining miles.

Remaining miles / New pace = Remaining time

16 / 8 = 2 hours

Remaining time: 2 hours

Step 4: Calculate the total time for the marathon.

First section time + Remaining time = Total time

1 + 2 = 3 hours

Total time: 3 hours
