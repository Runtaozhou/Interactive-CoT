Problem Statement:

On Tuesday, 12,000 ice cream cones were sold. On Wednesday, the number of ice cream cones sold was double the amount sold on Tuesday. How many ice cream cones have been sold in total?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the number of ice cream cones sold on Wednesday.

Since Wednesday's sales were double Tuesday's sales:

Wednesday sales = Tuesday sales × 2

Wednesday sales = 12,000 × 2 = 24,000 ice cream cones

Step 2: Find the total number of ice cream cones sold on both days.

Total sales = Tuesday sales + Wednesday sales

Total sales = 12,000 + 24,000 = 36,000 ice cream cones

Final Answer:

36,000
