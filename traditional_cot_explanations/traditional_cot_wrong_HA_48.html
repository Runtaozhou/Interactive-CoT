Problem Statement:

Kat decides she wants to start a boxing career. She gets a gym membership and spends 1 hour in the gym 3 times a week doing strength training. She also trained at the boxing gym 4 times a week for 1.5 hours. How many hours a week does she train?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the total hours spent on strength training per week.

Kat spends 1 hour in the gym 3 times a week for strength training.

Total strength training hours = Hours per session × Sessions per week

Total strength training hours = 1 × 3 = 3 hours a week

Step 2: Calculate the additional stretching hours per week.

Stretching hours per session × Sessions per week

3 × 0.5 = 1.5 hours per week

Step 3: Calculate the total hours spent on boxing training per week.

Kat trains at the boxing gym 4 times a week for 1.5 hours each session.

Total boxing hours = Hours per session × Sessions per week

Total boxing hours = 1.5 × 4 = 6 hours a week

Step 4: Add up all training hours to find the total training time per week.

Total training hours = Strength training hours + Stretching hours + Boxing hours

Total training hours = 3 + 1.5 + 6 = 10.5 hours a week

Final Answer:

10.5
