Problem Statement:

Leo had 400 marbles in a jar. He packed the marbles with ten marbles in each pack, and he gave some of them to his two friends, Manny and Neil. He gave Manny 1/4 of the number of packs of marbles, Neil received 1/8 of the number of packs of marbles, and he kept the rest. How many packs of marbles did Leo keep?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the total number of packs Leo had initially.

We need to divide the total number of marbles by the number of marbles in each pack:

Total packs = 400 ÷ 10 = 40 packs

Step 2: Calculate how many packs Manny received.

Manny received 1/4 of the total number of packs:

Manny's packs = 40 × 1/4 = 10 packs

Step 3: Calculate how many packs Neil received.

Neil received 1/8 of the total number of packs:

Neil's packs = 40 × 1/8 = 5 packs

Step 4: Calculate the total number of packs Leo gave away.

We need to find the total number of packs Leo gave to his friends:

Total packs given away = Manny's packs - Neil's packs = 10 - 5 = 5 packs

Step 5: Calculate how many packs Leo kept.

We can find how many packs Leo kept by subtracting the total given away from the initial number of packs:

Leo's packs = 40 - 5 = 35 packs

Final Answer:

35
