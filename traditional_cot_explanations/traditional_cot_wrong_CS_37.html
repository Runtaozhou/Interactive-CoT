Problem Statement:

Stefan goes to a restaurant to eat dinner with his family. They order an appetizer that costs $10 and 4 entrees that are $20 each. If they tip 20% of the total for the waiter, what is the total amount of money that they spend at the restaurant?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the total cost of the entrees.

We need to multiply the number of entrees by the cost per entree:

4 entrees × $20 per entree = $80

Step 2: Calculate the total cost of the dinner before tip.

We add the cost of the entrees to the cost of the appetizer:

$80 (entrees) + $10 (appetizer) = $90

Step 3: Calculate the tip amount.

We multiply the dinner cost by the tip percentage:

$100 × 0.20 = $20

Step 4: Calculate the total cost including tip.

We add the dinner cost to the tip amount:

$90 (dinner cost) + $20 (tip) = $110

Final Answer:

110
