Problem Statement:

On Thursday, Joe caught 3 pounds of crawfish. He caught 4 times that amount on Friday and half the amount of his Friday's catch on Saturday. If 1 serving of crawfish is 3 pounds, how many servings does he have?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate how much crawfish Joe caught on Friday.

Friday's catch = Thursday's catch × 4

Friday's catch = 3 pounds × 4 = 12 pounds

Step 2: Calculate how much crawfish Joe caught on Saturday.

Saturday's catch = Friday's catch × 2

Saturday's catch = 12 pounds × 2 = 24 pounds

Step 3: Calculate the total amount of crawfish Joe caught over the three days.

Total catch = Thursday's catch + Friday's catch + Saturday's catch

Total catch = 3 pounds + 12 pounds + 24 pounds = 39 pounds

Step 4: Calculate how many servings Joe has.

Number of servings = Total catch ÷ Serving size

Number of servings = 39 pounds ÷ 3 pounds = 13 servings

Final Answer:

13
