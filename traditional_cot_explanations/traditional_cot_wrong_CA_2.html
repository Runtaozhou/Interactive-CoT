Problem Statement:

Jean has 60 stuffies. She keeps 1/3 of them and then gives away the rest. She gives 1/4 of what she gave away to her sister Janet. How many stuffies did Janet get?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate how many stuffies Jean kept.

Jean keeps 1/3 of her total stuffies.

Number of stuffies Jean kept = 60 × 1/3 = 60/3 = 20 stuffies

Step 2: Calculate how many stuffies Jean gave away.

Jean gave away the rest of her stuffies after keeping some.

Number of stuffies Jean gave away = 60 - 20 = 35 stuffies

Step 3: Calculate how many stuffies Janet received.

Janet received 1/4 of the stuffies that Jean gave away.

Number of stuffies Janet received = 35 × 1/4 = 35/4 = 8.75 stuffies

Final Answer:

8.75
