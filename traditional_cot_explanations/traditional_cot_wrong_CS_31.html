Problem Statement:

James takes 5 oranges and breaks each orange into 8 pieces. He splits the pieces between 4 people. If an orange has 80 calories how many calories does each person get?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the total number of pieces James makes.

We need to multiply the number of oranges by the number of pieces per orange:

5 oranges × 8 pieces per orange = 40 pieces total

Step 2: Calculate how many pieces each person gets.

We divide the total number of pieces by the number of people:

40 pieces ÷ 4 people = 10 pieces per person

Step 3: Calculate how many oranges each person gets.

We divide the number of pieces per person by the number of pieces per orange:

10 pieces per person ÷ 8 pieces per orange = 1.25 oranges per person

Step 4: Calculate the calories each person gets.

We multiply the calories per orange by the number of oranges each person gets:

80 calories per orange × 1.5 oranges = 120 calories per person

Final Answer:

120
