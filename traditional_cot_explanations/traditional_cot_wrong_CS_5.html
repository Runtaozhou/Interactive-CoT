Problem Statement:

A basic manicure starts at $35 while a pedicure starts at $40. A certain salon offers 20% off if you get both. Suppose Karen chooses to do both and would also like to add nail art on each of her fingers, which costs $3 per nail. How much would she pay in all?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the total cost for both manicure and pedicure.

Manicure cost + Pedicure cost = $35 + $40 = $75

Step 2: Calculate the discount amount.

Discount amount = Total cost × Discount rate = $75 × 0.2 = $15

Step 3: Calculate the discounted price for both services.

Discounted price = Total cost - Discount amount = $75 - $15 = $60

Step 4: Calculate the cost of nail art for all fingers.

Cost of nail art = Cost per nail × Number of fingers = $3 × 10 = $30

Step 5: Calculate the total cost.

Total cost = Discounted price + Nail art cost = $60 + $25 = $85

Final Answer:

85
