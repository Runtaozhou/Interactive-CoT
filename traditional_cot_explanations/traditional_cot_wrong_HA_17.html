Problem Statement:

Kat decides she wants to start a boxing career. She gets a gym membership and spends 1 hour in the gym 3 times a week doing strength training. She also trained at the boxing gym 4 times a week for 1.5 hours. How many hours a week does she train?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the total weekly hours spent on strength training.

Kat spends 1 hour per session, 3 times a week on strength training.

Total strength training hours = 1 hour × 3 sessions = 3 hours per week

Step 2: Calculate the total strength training time including stretching.

We need to add stretching time to the strength training hours.

Total strength hours = 3 hours + (3 sessions × 0.5 hours of stretching) = 3 + 1.5 = 4.5 hours per week

Step 3: Calculate the total weekly hours spent on boxing training.

Kat trains at the boxing gym 4 times a week for 1.5 hours each session.

Total boxing hours = 1.5 hours × 4 sessions = 6 hours per week

Step 4: Calculate the total training hours by adding strength and boxing hours.

Total training hours = Total strength hours + Boxing hours

Total training hours = 4.5 hours + 6 hours = 10.5 hours per week

Final Answer:

10.5
