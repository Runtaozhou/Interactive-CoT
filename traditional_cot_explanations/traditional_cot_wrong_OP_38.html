Problem Statement:

Trevor and Joe were working together to finish a 500 piece puzzle. They put the border together first and that was 75 pieces. Trevor was able to place 105 pieces of the puzzle. Joe was able to place three times the number of puzzle pieces as Trevor. How many puzzle pieces are missing?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the number of pieces left after placing the border.

Total pieces + Border pieces = 500 + 75 = 575 pieces

Step 2: Calculate how many pieces Joe placed.

Joe placed three times as many pieces as Trevor.

Trevor's pieces × 3 = 105 × 3 = 315 pieces

Step 3: Calculate the total pieces placed by Trevor and Joe.

Trevor's pieces + Joe's pieces = 105 + 315 = 420 pieces

Step 4: Calculate the number of missing pieces.

Pieces left - Total placed = 575 - 420 = 155 pieces

Final Answer:

155
