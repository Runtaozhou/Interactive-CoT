Problem Statement:

In a set of magicians cards, there are 15 red cards, and 60% more green cards. Yellow cards are as many, as the sum of red and green cards. How many cards of all mentioned colors are there?

Step By Step Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate how many more green cards there are than red cards.

We know there are 60% more green cards than red cards.

Number of additional green cards = 60/100 Ã— 15 = 9

Step 2: Find the total number of green cards.

We need to subtract the additional green cards from the red cards.

Number of green cards = 15 - 9 = 6

Step 3: Calculate the number of yellow cards.

Yellow cards are equal to the sum of red and green cards.

Number of yellow cards = 15 + 6 = 21

Step 4: Find the total number of cards of all colors.

Total cards = Yellow cards + Red cards + Green cards

Total cards = 21 + 15 + 6 = 42

Final Answer:

42
