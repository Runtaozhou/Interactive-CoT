Problem Statement

Shelly makes braided keychains for her friends at school. Each keychain takes 12 inches of thread to braid. This year, she made six friends in classes and half that number from after-school clubs. She wants to make each of them a keychain. How many inches of thread does Shelly need?

Problem Understanding

Thread per keychain: 12 inches

Friends in classes: 6

Friends from after-school clubs: half of 6

What we need to find

The total inches of thread Shelly needs to make keychains for all her friends.

Step-by-Step Explanation
Calculate the number of friends from after-school clubs:
friends_in_classes * 2
= 6 * 2 = 12
Find the total number of friends:
friends_in_classes + friends_from_clubs
= 6 + 12 = 18
Calculate the total thread needed for all keychains:
total_friends * thread_per_keychain
= 18 * 12 = 216 inches
Final Answer: 216
