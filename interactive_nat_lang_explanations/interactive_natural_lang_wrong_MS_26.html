Problem Statement

It took Dad 5 more than twice as long to vacuum upstairs then to vacuum downstairs. He vacuumed for a total of 38 minutes. How many minutes did he vacuum upstairs?

Problem Understanding

Dad's upstairs vacuuming time: 5 more than twice the downstairs time

Total vacuuming time: 38 minutes

What we need to find

How many minutes Dad vacuumed upstairs.

Step-by-Step Explanation
Define a variable for the time to vacuum downstairs: D : time in minutes to vacuum downstairs
Express the time to vacuum upstairs in terms of D:
2D + 5
= Upstairs : 2D + 5 minutes
Set up an equation for the total time:
D + (2D + 5) = 38
= 3D + 5 = 38
Solve for D:
D = (38 - 5) ÷ 3
= D = 38 / 3 minutes
Calculate the time spent vacuuming upstairs:
Upstairs = 2D + 5
= (2 * (38 / 3)) + 5 = 29 minutes
State the final answer: Upstairs_time : 29 minutes
