Problem Statement

Rebecca bought 2 pies for the holiday weekend. Each pie was sliced into 8 slices. Rebecca ate 1 slice of each pie. Her family and friends ate 50% of the remaining pies over the weekend. On Sunday evening Rebecca and her husband each had another slice of pie. How many slices are remaining?

Problem Understanding

Number of pies: 2

Slices per pie: 8

Rebecca's initial consumption: 1 slice of each pie

Family and friends consumption: 50% of remaining pies

Sunday evening consumption: 1 slice each for Rebecca and husband

What we need to find

How many slices of pie are remaining after all the consumption.

Step-by-Step Explanation
Calculate the total number of slices from all pies:
number of pies × slices per pie
= 2 × 8 = 16 slices
Subtract the slices Rebecca initially ate:
total_slices - slices eaten by Rebecca
= 16 slices - 2 slices = 14 slices
Calculate how many slices family and friends ate:
remaining_after_initial × percentage eaten
= 14 slices × 50% = 7 slices
Calculate remaining slices after family and friends ate:
remaining_after_initial - family_friends_ate
= 14 slices - 7 slices = 7 slices
Subtract the slices eaten by Rebecca and her husband on Sunday:
remaining_before_sunday - slices eaten on Sunday
= 7 slices - 3 slices = 4 slices
Final Answer: 4
