Problem Statement

On Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays, college student Kimo has three 1-hour classes each day. On Tuesdays and Thursdays, he has two 2-hour classes each day. In one semester, there are 16 weeks of school. In one semester, how many hours does Kimo spend attending classes?

Problem Understanding

Monday, Wednesday, Friday: three 1-hour classes each day

Tuesday, Thursday: two 2-hour classes each day

Semester length: 16 weeks

What we need to find

The total number of hours Kimo spends attending classes in one semester.

Step-by-Step Explanation
Calculate hours spent in class on a single Monday, Wednesday, or Friday:
number of classes × hours per class
= 3 × 1 = 3 hours
Calculate total hours spent in class on Monday, Wednesday, and Friday each week:
daily_hours_MWF × number of days
= 3 hours × 3 = 9 hours
Calculate total hours spent in class on Monday, Wednesday, and Friday for the semester:
weekly_hours_MWF × number of weeks
= 9 hours × 16 = 144 hours
Calculate hours spent in class on a single Tuesday or Thursday:
number of classes × hours per class
= 2 × 2 = 4 hours
Calculate total hours spent in class on Tuesday and Thursday each week:
daily_hours_TTh × number of days
= 4 hours × 1 = 4 hours
Calculate total hours spent in class on Tuesday and Thursday for the semester:
weekly_hours_TTh × number of weeks
= 4 hours × 16 = 64 hours
Calculate total hours spent in class for the entire semester:
semester_hours_MWF + semester_hours_TTh
= 144 hours + 64 hours = 208 hours
Final Answer: 208
