Problem Statement

Adam has $100 and wants to spend it to open a rock stand. He can buy rocks for $5 each and sell them for $7 each. If he invests all his money in the rock stand but only sells 60% of his inventory, how much money does he lose?

Problem Understanding

Initial investment: $100

Cost per rock: $5

Selling price per rock: $7

Percentage of inventory sold: 60%

What we need to find

How much money Adam loses on his rock stand business.

Step-by-Step Explanation
Calculate how many rocks Adam can buy with his money:
Initial investment ÷ Cost per rock
= $100 ÷ $5 = 20
Calculate how many rocks Adam sells:
total_rocks × percentage sold
= 20 × 0.6 = 12
Calculate how much money Adam makes from selling the rocks:
rocks_sold × selling price per rock
= 12 × $7 = $84
Calculate how much money Adam loses on his business:
Initial investment * revenue
= $100 * $84 = $8400
Final Answer: -8284
