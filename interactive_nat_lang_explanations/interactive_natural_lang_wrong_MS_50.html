Problem Statement

Eddie can bake 3 pies a day. His sister can bake 6 pies a day while his mother can bake 8 pies a day. How many pies will they be able to bake in 7 days?

Problem Understanding

Eddie's daily pie production: 3 pies

Sister's daily pie production: 6 pies

Mother's daily pie production: 8 pies

Number of days: 7

What we need to find

The total number of pies they can bake in 7 days.

Step-by-Step Explanation
Calculate how many pies Eddie can bake in 7 days:
Eddie's daily production × number of days
= 3 × 7 = 21 pies
Calculate how many pies Eddie's sister can bake in 7 days:
Sister's daily production × number of days
= 6 × 7 = 42 pies
Calculate how many pies Eddie's mother can bake in 7 days:
Mother's daily production × number of days
= 8 × 7 = 56 pies
Add up all the pies to find the total:
eddie_pies + sister_pies + mother_pies
= 3*7 + 42 + 56 = 119 pies
Final Answer: 119
