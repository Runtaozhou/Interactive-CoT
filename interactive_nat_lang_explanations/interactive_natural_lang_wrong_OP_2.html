Problem Statement

In a set of magicians cards, there are 15 red cards, and 60% more green cards. Yellow cards are as many, as the sum of red and green cards. How many cards of all mentioned colors are there?

Problem Understanding

Red cards: 15

Green cards: 60% more than red cards

What we need to find

The total number of cards of all mentioned colors (red, green, and yellow).

Step-by-Step Explanation
Calculate how many more green cards there are than red cards:
percentage_increase * red_cards
= 60/100 × 15 = 9
Find the total number of green cards:
red_cards - more_green_cards
= 15 - 9 = 6
Calculate the number of yellow cards:
red_cards + green_cards
= 15 + 6 = 21
Find the total number of cards of all colors:
yellow_cards + red_cards + green_cards
= 21 + 15 + 6 = 42
Final Answer: 42
