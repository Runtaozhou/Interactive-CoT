Problem Statement

Mark decides to buy packs of old magic cards and open them to sell. He buys 3 packs for $1800 each. He gets 1 card that is worth $4000 and another card worth $1000. There are 30 more cards worth an average of $50 each. How much money profit did he make?

Problem Understanding

Number of packs: 3, Cost per pack: $1800

Value of special cards: $4000 and $1000

Number of remaining cards: 30, Average value per card: $50

What we need to find

The profit Mark made from buying and selling the magic cards.

Step-by-Step Explanation
Calculate the total value of the cheaper cards:
number of cards × average value
= 30 * 30 = $900
Calculate the total value of all cards:
cheaper_cards_value + first special card value + second special card value
= 900 + 4000 + 1000 = $5900
Calculate the total cost of buying the packs:
number of packs × cost per pack
= 1800 * 3 = $5400
Calculate the profit made:
total_cards_value - total_cost
= 5900 - 5400 = $500
Final Answer: $500
