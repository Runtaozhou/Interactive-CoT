Problem Statement

A basic manicure starts at $35 while a pedicure starts at $40. A certain salon offers 20% off if you get both. Suppose Karen chooses to do both and would also like to add nail art on each of her fingers, which costs $3 per nail. How much would she pay in all?

Problem Understanding

Basic manicure cost: $35

Pedicure cost: $40

Discount for both services: 20%

Nail art cost: $3 per nail

What we need to find

The total amount Karen would pay for both services with the discount and nail art on all fingers.

Step-by-Step Explanation
Calculate the total cost for both manicure and pedicure:
manicure_cost + pedicure_cost
= $35 + $40 = $75
Calculate the discount amount:
both_services_cost * discount_rate
= $75 * 0.2 = $15
Calculate the discounted price for both services:
both_services_cost - discount_amount
= $75 - $15 = $60
Calculate the cost of nail art for all fingers:
cost_per_nail * number_of_fingers
= $3 * $10 = $30
Calculate the total cost:
discounted_price + nail_art_cost
= $60 + $25 = $85
Final Answer: $85
