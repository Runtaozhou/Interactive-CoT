Problem Statement

Ishmael, Ponce, and Jalen measured their weight and realized Ishmael was 20 pounds heavier than Ponce and that Ponce was 10 pounds lighter than Jalen. If Jalen weighed 160 pounds, calculate the average weight of the three?

Problem Understanding

Ishmael is 20 pounds heavier than Ponce

Ponce is 10 pounds lighter than Jalen

Jalen's weight: 160 pounds

What we need to find

The average weight of Ishmael, Ponce, and Jalen.

Step-by-Step Explanation
Calculate Ishmael's weight based on his relation to Ponce:
Jalen's weight + 20
= 160 + 20 = 180 pounds
Calculate the total weight of all three people:
Jalen's weight + Jalen's weight + Ishmael's weight
= 160 + 160 + 180 = 500 pounds
Calculate the average weight by dividing the total weight by the number of people:
total_weight ÷ 3
= 500 ÷ 3 = 166.67 pounds
Final Answer: 166.67
