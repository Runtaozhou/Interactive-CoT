Problem Statement

Trip wanted to watch the new action movie in theaters. An evening ticket cost $10 and a large popcorn & drink combo would cost him an additional $10. He noticed on their website, they had a special offer. From 12 noon to 3 pm, save 20% off tickets and 50% off any food combos. How much money could Trip save by going to the earlier movie?

Problem Understanding

Evening ticket cost: $10

Large popcorn & drink combo cost: $10

Early show discount on tickets: 20% off

Early show discount on food combos: 50% off

What we need to find

How much money Trip could save by going to the earlier movie instead of the evening show.

Step-by-Step Explanation
Calculate the total cost for an evening movie with food combo:
evening_ticket + food_combo
= $10 + $10 = $20
Calculate the savings on the ticket for an earlier movie:
normal_ticket_price * discount_percentage
= $15 * .20 = $3
Calculate the savings on the food combo for an earlier movie:
food_combo_price * discount_percentage
= $10 * .50 = $5
Calculate the total savings by adding ticket and food savings:
ticket_savings + food_savings
= $3 + $5 = $8
Final Answer: $8
