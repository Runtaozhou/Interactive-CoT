Problem Statement

Faith went to a baking shop and bought flour that cost $5 and a cake stand that costs $28. She then gave the cashier two $20 bills and $3 in loose coins. How much change will she receive?

Problem Understanding

Cost of flour: $5

Cost of cake stand: $28

Payment in bills: two $20 bills

Payment in coins: $3

What we need to find

The amount of change Faith will receive.

Step-by-Step Explanation
Calculate the total cost of the items Faith bought:
cost of flour + cost of cake stand
= $5 + $28 = $33
Calculate the amount Faith paid in bills:
number of bills × value of each bill
= 2 × $20 = $40
Calculate the total amount Faith gave to the cashier:
bill_payment + coin_payment
= $40 + $4 = $44
Calculate the change Faith will receive:
total_payment - total_cost
= $44 - $33 = $11
Final Answer: $11
