Problem Statement

John had a son James when he was 19. James is now twice as old as his sister Dora, who will turn 12 in 3 years. How old will John's youngest son, who was born when John was 32, be in 3 years?

Problem Understanding

John's age when James was born: 19

Dora will turn 12 in 3 years

John's age when youngest son was born: 32

Time period to calculate future age: 3 years

What we need to find

The age of John's youngest son in 3 years.

Step-by-Step Explanation
Calculate Dora's current age:
Future age - Years until that age
= 12 - 3 = 9
Calculate James' current age based on Dora's age:
Dora's future age × 2
= 15 * 2 = 30
Calculate John's current age:
James' age + John's age when James was born
= 30 + 19 = 49
Calculate the youngest son's current age:
John's current age - John's age when youngest son was born
= 49 - 32 = 17
Calculate the youngest son's age in 3 years:
Youngest son's current age + 3
= 17 + 3 = 20
Final Answer: 20
