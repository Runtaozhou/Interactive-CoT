Problem Statement

Martin went on an eight-hour business trip. During the first half of the trip, he traveled at a speed of 70 kilometers per hour and during the second half at a speed of 85 kilometers per hour. How many kilometers did he travel during the journey?

Problem Understanding

Trip duration: 8 hours

First half speed: 70 kilometers per hour

Second half speed: 85 kilometers per hour

What we need to find

The total kilometers Martin traveled during the journey.

Step-by-Step Explanation
Calculate the duration of half the trip:
total_time × 0.5
= 8 * 0.5 = 4 hours
Calculate the distance traveled during the first half of the trip:
half_trip_duration × first_half_speed
= 4 * 70 = 280 kilometers
Calculate the distance traveled during the second half of the trip:
half_trip_duration × second_half_speed
= 4 * 85 = 320 kilometers
Calculate the total distance traveled during the trip:
first_half_distance + second_half_distance
= 280 + 320 = 600 kilometers
Final Answer: 600
