Problem Statement

It takes 1 hour for refrigerated dough to come to room temperature. It takes 15 minutes to shape the dough and 2 hours to proof. The bread takes 30 minutes to bake and 15 minutes to cool. If the bakery opens at 6:00 am, what is the latest time the head baker can make it to the store to start working?

Problem Understanding

Time for dough to reach room temperature: 1 hour

Time to shape dough: 15 minutes

Proofing time: 2 hours

Baking time: 30 minutes

Cooling time: 15 minutes

Bakery opening time: 6:00 am

What we need to find

The latest time the head baker can arrive at the store to start working and still have the bread ready when the bakery opens.

Step-by-Step Explanation
Calculate the time needed for dough to reach room temperature and proof:
room_temp_time + proofing_time
= 1 hour + 2 hours = 3 hours
Calculate the total time for shaping, baking, and cooling:
shaping_time + baking_time + cooling_time
= 15 minutes + 30 minutes + 15 minutes = 60 minutes
Convert the final preparation time from minutes to hours:
final_prep_time_minutes ÷ minutes_per_hour
= 60 minutes ÷ 60 = 1 hour
Calculate the total preparation time:
initial_prep_time + final_prep_time_hours
= 3 hours + 1 hour = 4 hours
Calculate the latest arrival time for the baker:
opening_time - total_prep_time
= 6:00 am - 4 hours = 2:00 am
Final Answer: 2:00 am
