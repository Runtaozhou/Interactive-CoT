Problem Statement

Pam and Fred went to a carnival. Pam rode the roller coaster 2 times while Fred rode it 4 times. After that, each of them decided to ride the luge 2 times. If each ride cost 6 tickets, how many tickets did they use that day?

Problem Understanding

Pam's roller coaster rides: 2 times

Fred's roller coaster rides: 4 times

Each person's luge rides: 2 times

Cost per ride: 6 tickets

What we need to find

The total number of tickets they used that day.

Step-by-Step Explanation
Calculate the total roller coaster rides:
Pam's rides + Fred's rides
= 2 + 4 = 6 times
Calculate the total luge rides:
Pam's luge rides + Fred's luge rides
= 2 + 2 = 4 times
Calculate the total number of rides:
total_roller_coaster_rides + total_luge_rides
= 6 + 4 = 10 times
Calculate the total number of tickets used:
total_rides × cost per ride
= 10 × 6 = 50 tickets
Final Answer: 50
