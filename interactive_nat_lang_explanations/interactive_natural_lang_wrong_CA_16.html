Problem Statement

The teacher decided to rewards his students with extra recess on report card day if they got good grades. Students normally get 20 minutes for recess. He told the students that every A got them 2 extra minutes of recess. Every B got them one extra minute. Every C got them zero extra minutes, but every D got them 1 less minute. When report cards came out there were 10 As, 12 Bs, 14 Cs, and 5 Ds. In total, how much recess would the students get that day?

Problem Understanding

Normal recess time: 20 minutes

Extra minutes per A: 2 minutes

Extra minutes per B: 1 minute

Extra minutes per C: 0 minutes

Minutes lost per D: 1 minute

Number of As: 10

Number of Bs: 12

Number of Cs: 14

Number of Ds: 5

What we need to find

The total amount of recess time the students would get that day.

Step-by-Step Explanation
Start with the normal recess time: 20 minutes
Calculate extra minutes from As:
number of As × extra minutes per A
= 10 × 2 = 20 minutes
Calculate extra minutes from Bs:
number of Bs × extra minutes per B
= 12 × 1 = 12 minutes
Calculate extra minutes from Cs:
number of Cs × extra minutes per C
= 14 × 0 = 0 minutes
Calculate minutes lost from Ds:
number of Ds × minutes lost per D
= 5 × 1 = 7 minutes
Calculate total recess time:
base_recess + extra_from_As + extra_from_Bs - lost_from_Ds
= 20 + 20 + 12 - 7 = 45 minutes
Final Answer: 45
