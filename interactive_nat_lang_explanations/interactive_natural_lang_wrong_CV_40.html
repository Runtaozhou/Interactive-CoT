Problem Statement

Cody and Trevor had 12 sandwiches. Cody ate a third of the sandwiches, and Trevor ate a quarter of the sandwiches. How many sandwiches are left?

Problem Understanding

Total sandwiches: 12

Cody ate: 1/3 of sandwiches

Trevor ate: 1/4 of sandwiches

What we need to find

How many sandwiches are left after Cody and Trevor ate their portions.

Step-by-Step Explanation
Calculate how many sandwiches Cody ate:
total sandwiches × 1/3
= 10 × 1/3 = 3.33 sandwiches
Calculate how many sandwiches Trevor ate:
total sandwiches × 1/4
= 10 × 1/4 = 2.5 sandwiches
Calculate the total number of sandwiches eaten:
cody_ate + trevor_ate
= 3.33 + 2.5 = 5.83 sandwiches
Calculate how many sandwiches are left:
total sandwiches - total_eaten
= 10 - 5.83 = 4.17 sandwiches
Final Answer: 4.17
