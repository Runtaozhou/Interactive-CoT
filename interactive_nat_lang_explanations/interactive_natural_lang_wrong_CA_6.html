Problem Statement

Leo collects stickers. Two years ago, he had 100 stickers in his collection. Last year, Leo collected 50 stickers. This year, he collected twice the number of stickers as the previous year. How many stickers does Leo have in his collection?

Problem Understanding

Two years ago stickers: 100 stickers

Last year collected: 50 stickers

This year collected: twice the number as previous year

What we need to find

The total number of stickers Leo has in his collection.

Step-by-Step Explanation
Calculate how many stickers Leo had after last year:
initial_stickers + last_year_stickers
= 100 + 50 = 150 stickers
Calculate how many stickers Leo collected this year:
last_year_stickers × 2
= 50 × 2 = 120 stickers
Calculate the total number of stickers in Leo's collection:
stickers_after_last_year + this_year_stickers
= 150 + 120 = 270 stickers
Final Answer: 270
