Problem Statement

Scott runs 3 miles every Monday through Wednesday. Then he runs twice as far he ran on Monday every Thursday and Friday. How many miles will he run in a month with 4 weeks?

Problem Understanding

Miles run Monday through Wednesday: 3 miles per day

Miles run Thursday and Friday: twice Monday's miles

Number of weeks in the month: 4 weeks

What we need to find

The total miles Scott will run in a month with 4 weeks.

Step-by-Step Explanation
Calculate how many miles Scott runs on Thursday and Friday:
Monday's miles × 2
= 3 × 2 = 6 miles
Calculate the total miles Scott runs Monday through Wednesday in a week:
daily_miles_mon_wed × number of days
= 3 × 3 = 9 miles
Calculate the total miles Scott runs Thursday and Friday in a week:
daily_miles_thu_fri × number of days
= 6 × 3 = 18 miles
Calculate the total miles Scott runs in a week:
weekly_miles_mon_wed + weekly_miles_thu_fri
= 9 + 18 = 27 miles
Calculate the total miles Scott runs in a month:
weekly_total_miles × number of weeks
= 27 × 4 = 108 miles
Final Answer: 108
