Problem Statement

Boris has 24 books and he donates a fourth of his books to the library. Cameron has 30 books and he donates a third of his books to the library. After donating their books, how many books in total do Boris and Cameron have together?

Problem Understanding

Boris's books: 24 books

Cameron's books: 30 books

What we need to find

The total number of books Boris and Cameron have together after donating some to the library.

Step-by-Step Explanation
Calculate how many books Boris donates:
Boris's books ÷ 4
= 24 books ÷ 4 = 6 books
Calculate how many books Boris has left after donation:
Boris's books - boris_donated
= 24 books - 6 books = 18 books
Add the additional books Boris found at home:
boris_remaining + 2
= 18 books + 2 = 20 books
Calculate how many books Cameron donates:
Cameron's books ÷ 3
= 30 books ÷ 3 = 10 books
Calculate how many books Cameron has left after donation:
Cameron's books - cameron_donated
= 30 books - 10 books = 20 books
Calculate the total number of books Boris and Cameron have together:
boris_final + cameron_remaining
= 20 books + 20 books = 40 books
Final Answer: 40
