Problem Statement

Doris works at the Widget Factory in the packing department. She puts 3 widgets in each carton, which are 4 inches wide, 4 inches long, and 5 inches tall. She then packs those cartons into a shipping box before sending it to the loading bay. The shipping boxes are 20 inches wide, 20 inches long, and 20 inches high. How many widgets get shipped in each shipping box?

Problem Understanding

Widgets per carton: 3

Carton dimensions: 4 inches wide, 4 inches long, 5 inches tall

Shipping box dimensions: 20 inches wide, 20 inches long, 20 inches high

What we need to find

How many widgets get shipped in each shipping box.

Step-by-Step Explanation
Calculate the volume of each carton:
width × length × height
= 4 * 4 * 5 = 80 cubic inches
Calculate the area of the shipping box:
width + length + height + width + length + height
= 20 + 20 + 20 + 20 + 20 + 20 = 120 square inches
Find how many cartons fit in each shipping box:
shipping_box_area ÷ carton_volume
= 120 / 80 = 1.5
Calculate the total number of widgets in each shipping box:
widgets_per_carton × cartons_per_box
= 3 * 1.5 = 4.5
Final Answer: 4.5
