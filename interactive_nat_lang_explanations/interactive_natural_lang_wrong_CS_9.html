Problem Statement

James hurt himself exercising. The pain subsided after 3 days, but he knew that the injury would take at least 5 times that long to fully heal. After that, he wanted to wait another 3 days before he started working out again. If he wants to wait 3 weeks after that to start lifting heavy again, how long until he can lift heavy again?

Problem Understanding

Pain subsided after: 3 days

Injury healing time: 5 times pain subsiding time

Additional wait before working out: 3 days

Wait before lifting heavy: 3 weeks

What we need to find

The total number of days until James can lift heavy again.

Step-by-Step Explanation
Calculate how long the injury would take to fully heal:
Pain subsided days × 5
= 3 days × 5 = 15 days
Calculate when he would start exercising again:
Healing time + Additional wait
= 15 days + 3 days = 18 days
Calculate how many days he needs to wait before lifting heavy:
3 weeks × 7 days per week
= 3 weeks × 7 = 21 days
Calculate the total time until he can lift heavy again:
Exercise start time + Heavy lifting wait
= 18 days + 24 days = 42 days
Final Answer: 42 days
