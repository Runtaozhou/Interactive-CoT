Problem Statement

Dexter has five pigs. Each one eats 4 pounds of feed, twice a day. If Dexter ordered 300 pounds of feed, how many pounds of feed is left after a week?

Problem Understanding

Number of pigs: 5

Feed per pig: 4 pounds, twice a day

Total feed ordered: 300 pounds

Time period: 1 week (7 days)

What we need to find

How many pounds of feed is left after a week.

Step-by-Step Explanation
Calculate how much feed one pig consumes in a day:
feed per meal × number of meals
= 4 + 2 = 6 pounds
Calculate how much feed all pigs consume in a day:
daily_feed_per_pig × number of pigs
= 6 pounds × 5 = 30 pounds
Calculate how much feed all pigs consume in a week:
daily_feed_total × days in a week
= 30 pounds × 7 = 210 pounds
Calculate how much feed is left after a week:
total feed ordered - weekly_feed_total
= 300 pounds - 210 pounds = 90 pounds
Final Answer: 90
