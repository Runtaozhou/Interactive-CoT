Problem Statement

Team Soccer Stars plays in a soccer league. They played a total of 20 games, and won 14 games, lost 2, and drew the rest of the matches. They earn 3 points for a win, 1 point for a draw, and nothing for a loss. How many points did they have at the end of the season?

Problem Understanding

Total games: 20

Games won: 14

Games lost: 2

Points per win: 3

Points per draw: 1

Points per loss: 0

What we need to find

The total number of points Team Soccer Stars had at the end of the season.

Step-by-Step Explanation
Calculate the total number of games won and lost:
wins + losses
= 14 + 2 = 16 games
Calculate the number of games drawn:
total games - games won and lost
= 20 - 12 = 8 games
Calculate the points earned from wins:
wins × points per win
= 14 × 3 = 42 points
Calculate the points earned from draws:
draws × points per draw
= 8 × 1 = 8 points
Calculate the points earned from losses:
losses × points per loss
= 2 × 0 = 0 points
Calculate the total points earned:
points from wins + points from draws + points from losses
= 42 + 8 + 0 = 50 points
Final Answer: 50
