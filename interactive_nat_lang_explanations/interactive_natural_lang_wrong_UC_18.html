Problem Statement

Luke is planning a trip to London and wants to see how long it will take him to travel there. He decides that he will take a bus to the town center, walk for 15 minutes to the train center, wait twice this long for the train to arrive, and then take the 6-hour train ride to London. If Luke's entire trip takes him 8 hours, how many minutes does his bus ride take?

Problem Understanding

Walking time: 15 minutes

Waiting time: twice the walking time

Train ride: 6 hours

Total trip time: 8 hours

What we need to find

How many minutes Luke's bus ride takes.

Step-by-Step Explanation
Calculate the time spent on activities other than the bus ride:
total trip time - train ride time
= 8 hours - 6 hours = 2 hours
Convert the non-bus time from hours to minutes:
non_bus_time × minutes per hour
= 2 hours * 50 minutes/hour = 100 minutes
Calculate the waiting time for the train:
walking time × 2
= 15 minutes * 2 = 30 minutes
Calculate the bus ride time by subtracting other activities from the non-bus time:
non_bus_time_minutes - waiting_time - walking_time
= 100 minutes - 30 minutes - 15 minutes = 55 minutes
Final Answer: 55
