Problem Statement

Lisa has 36 candies. On Mondays and Wednesdays, she eats 2 candies for each day and on the other days of the week she eats 1 candy for each day. How many weeks does it take for Lisa to eat all of the candies?

Problem Understanding

Total candies: 36

Candies eaten on Monday and Wednesday: 2 per day

Candies eaten on other days: 1 per day

What we need to find

How many weeks it takes for Lisa to eat all of the candies.

Step-by-Step Explanation
Calculate how many candies Lisa eats on Mondays and Wednesdays:
candies per day × number of days
= 2 * 2 = 4 candies
Calculate how many candies Lisa eats on the other days of the week (Thursday to Sunday):
candies per day × number of days
= 4 * 1 = 4 candies
Calculate the total candies Lisa eats in a week:
monday_wednesday_candies + other_days_candies
= 4 + 4 = 8 candies
Calculate how many weeks it takes to eat all candies:
total candies ÷ weekly candies
= 36 / 8 = 4.5 weeks
Final Answer: 4.5
