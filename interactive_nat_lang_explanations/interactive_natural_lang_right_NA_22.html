Problem Statement

Mrs. Young makes bonnets for kids in the orphanage. On Monday, she made 10 bonnets, on Tuesday and Wednesday combined she made twice more than on Monday, while on Thursday she made 5 more than on Monday, and on Friday she made 5 less than on Thursday. Then, she divided up the bonnets evenly and sent them to 5 orphanages. How many bonnets did Mrs. Young send to each orphanage?

Problem Understanding

Monday bonnets: 10

Tuesday and Wednesday combined: twice more than Monday

Thursday bonnets: 5 more than Monday

Friday bonnets: 5 less than Thursday

Number of orphanages: 5

What we need to find

How many bonnets Mrs. Young sent to each orphanage.

Step-by-Step Explanation
Calculate how many bonnets Mrs. Young made on Tuesday and Wednesday combined:
Monday bonnets × 2
= 10 × 2 = 20 bonnets
Calculate how many bonnets Mrs. Young made on Thursday:
Monday bonnets + 5
= 10 + 5 = 15 bonnets
Calculate how many bonnets Mrs. Young made on Friday:
Thursday bonnets - 5
= 15 - 5 = 10 bonnets
Calculate the total number of bonnets Mrs. Young made:
Monday bonnets + Tuesday and Wednesday bonnets + Thursday bonnets + Friday bonnets
= 10 + 20 + 15 + 10 = 55 bonnets
Calculate how many bonnets Mrs. Young sent to each orphanage:
Total bonnets ÷ Number of orphanages
= 55 ÷ 5 = 11 bonnets
Final Answer: 11
