Problem Statement

A baker bakes 5 loaves of bread an hour in one oven. He has 4 ovens. From Monday to Friday, he bakes for 5 hours, but on Saturday and Sunday, he only bakes for 2 hours. How many loaves of bread does the baker bake in 3 weeks?

Problem Understanding

Loaves per hour per oven: 5

Number of ovens: 4

Hours of baking Monday-Friday: 5

Hours of baking Saturday-Sunday: 2

Number of weeks: 3

What we need to find

The total number of loaves of bread the baker bakes in 3 weeks.

Step-by-Step Explanation
Calculate how many loaves the baker bakes in one hour using all ovens:
loaves_per_hour_per_oven × number_of_ovens
= 5 × 4 = 20 loaves of bread
Calculate how many loaves the baker bakes per day from Monday to Friday:
hours_per_day × loaves_per_hour
= 5 × 20 = 100 loaves of bread per day
Calculate the total loaves baked from Monday to Friday:
loaves_per_weekday × number_of_weekdays
= 100 × 5 = 500 loaves of bread
Calculate how many loaves the baker bakes per day on Saturday and Sunday:
weekend_hours × loaves_per_hour
= 2 × 20 = 40 loaves of bread per day
Calculate the total loaves baked on Saturday and Sunday:
loaves_per_weekend_day × number_of_weekend_days
= 40 × 2 = 80 loaves of bread
Calculate the total loaves baked in one week:
loaves_weekdays + loaves_weekend
= 500 + 80 = 580 loaves of bread
Calculate the total loaves baked in three weeks:
loaves_per_week × number_of_weeks
= 580 × 3 = 1740 loaves of bread
Final Answer: 1740
