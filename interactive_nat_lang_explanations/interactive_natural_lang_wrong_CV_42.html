Problem Statement

3 customers were kicked out of the Walmart for refusing to wear masks. A number equals to four times that many minus 5 were kicked out for shoplifting. Three times the number of shoplifters were kicked out for physical violence over goods on sale. If a total of 50 people were kicked out of the Walmart, how many were kicked out for other reasons?

Problem Understanding

Customers kicked out for not wearing masks: 3

Shoplifters formula: 4 times mask-refusers minus 5

Violence formula: 3 times shoplifters

Total people kicked out: 50

What we need to find

We need to find how many people were kicked out for other reasons.

Step-by-Step Explanation
First quadruple the number of customers kicked out for not wearing masks:
4 * mask_refusers
= 4 * 3 customers = 12 customers
Then subtract 4 from this number to find shoplifters:
quadrupled_mask_refusers - 4
= 12 customers - 4 customers = 8 customers
Then triple the number of shoplifters to find the number of people kicked out for violence:
3 * shoplifters
= 3 * 8 customers = 24 customers
Then subtract all known reasons from total to find the number kicked out for other reasons:
total - mask_refusers - shoplifters - violence
= 50 customers - 3 customers - 8 customers - 24 customers = 15 customers
Final Answer: 15
