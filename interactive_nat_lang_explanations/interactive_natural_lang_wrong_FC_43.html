Problem Statement

The building has 42 storage units that are a total of 5040 square feet. Twenty of the units are 8 by 4 feet each. If the remaining units are all the same size, what is the area, in square feet, for one of the remaining units?

Problem Understanding

Total storage units: 42

Total area: 5040 square feet

Twenty units dimensions: 8 by 4 feet each

What we need to find

The area, in square feet, for one of the remaining units.

Step-by-Step Explanation
Calculate the perimeter of one of the 20 units:
8 + 4 + 8 + 4
= 8 + 4 + 8 + 4 = 24 square feet
Calculate the total area of the 20 units:
unit_area × 20
= 24 square feet × 20 = 480 square feet
Calculate the remaining area for the other units:
total_area - twenty_units_area
= 5040 square feet - 480 square feet = 4560 square feet
Calculate the number of remaining units:
total_units - 20
= 42 - 20 = 22 remaining units
Calculate the area of one remaining unit:
remaining_area ÷ remaining_units
= 4560 square feet ÷ 22 = 207.27 square feet
State the final answer: One of the remaining units is 207.27 square feet.
