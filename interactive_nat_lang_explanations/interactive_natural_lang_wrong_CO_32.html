Problem Statement

Mitch is a freelancer, she works 5 hours every day from Monday to Friday and 3 hours every Saturday and Sunday. If she earns $3 per hour and earns double on weekends, how much does she earn every week?

Problem Understanding

Weekday hours: 5 hours per day, Monday to Friday

Weekend hours: 3 hours per day, Saturday and Sunday

Weekday rate: $3 per hour

Weekend rate: double the weekday rate

What we need to find

How much Mitch earns every week.

Step-by-Step Explanation
Calculate how much Mitch earns per weekday:
hours per day × hourly rate
= 5 × $3 = $15
Calculate how much Mitch earns for all weekdays:
daily_weekday_earnings × number of weekdays
= $15 × 5 = $75
Calculate Mitch's hourly rate on weekends:
weekday rate × 2
= $3 × 2 = $6
Calculate how much Mitch earns for her weekend hours:
weekend_hourly_rate × hours per weekend day
= $6 × 3 = $18
Calculate total weekend earnings:
daily_weekend_earnings × number of weekend days
= $18 × 1 = $18
Calculate total weekly earnings:
total_weekday_earnings + total_weekend_earnings
= $75 + $18 = $93
Final Answer: $93
