Problem Statement

Tom and Devin have been teaching for a total of 70 years. Devin has been teaching for 5 less than half the number of years that Tom has. How many years has Tom been teaching?

Problem Understanding

Total teaching years: 70 years

Devin's teaching years: 5 less than half of Tom's years

What we need to find

How many years Tom has been teaching.

Step-by-Step Explanation
Define a variable for Tom's teaching years: x : Tom's teaching years
Express Devin's teaching years in terms of Tom's:
(1/2)x - 5
= Devin's years : (1/2)x - 5
Set up an equation using the total years:
70 = x + ((1/2)x - 5)
= 70 = x + ((1/2)x - 5)
Simplify the equation:
70 = (3/2)x - 5
= 70 = (3/2)x - 5
Add 5 to both sides:
80 = (3/2)x
= 70 + 5 = 75, not 80
Solve for x:
x = 80 ÷ (3/2)
= x = 80 ÷ (3/2) = 53.333333333333336
State the final answer: Tom's teaching years : 53.333333333333336
