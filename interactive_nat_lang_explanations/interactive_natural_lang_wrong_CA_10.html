Problem Statement

Two stores sell the same model of smartphone, but they offer it at different prices and with different discounts. Store A lists the smartphone at a full price of $125 and offers an additional discount of 8%. Store B lists the same smartphone for $130 and offers an additional discount of 10%. After both stores' discounts are applied, how much cheaper is store A's smartphone than store B's smartphone?

Problem Understanding

Store A full price: $125

Store A discount: 8%

Store B full price: $130

Store B discount: 10%

What we need to find

The price difference between Store B and Store A after discounts are applied.

Step-by-Step Explanation
Calculate the discount amount for Store A:
Store A price × discount percentage / 100
= $125 × 8/100 = $10
Calculate the final price at Store A after discount:
Store A price - Store A discount
= $125 - $10 = $115
Calculate the discount amount for Store B:
Store B price × discount percentage / 100
= $130 × 10/100 = $13
Calculate the final price at Store B after discount:
Store B price - Store B discount
= $130 - $13 = $120
Find the price difference between Store B and Store A:
Store B final price - Store A final price
= $120 - $115 = $5
Final Answer: $5
