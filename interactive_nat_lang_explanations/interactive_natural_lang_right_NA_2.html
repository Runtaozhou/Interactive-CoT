Problem Statement

Aunt Gemma has four dogs. She feeds them with dog food twice a day. Each dog can consume 250 grams of food per meal. If aunt Gemma bought 2 sacks of dog food, each weighing 50 kilograms, how many days will those food last?

Problem Understanding

Number of dogs: 4

Feeding frequency: 2 times per day

Food consumption per dog per meal: 250 grams

Dog food purchased: 2 sacks of 50 kilograms each

What we need to find

How many days the dog food will last.

Step-by-Step Explanation
Calculate how much food each dog consumes daily:
food per meal × meals per day
= 250 grams × 2 = 500 grams
Calculate the total daily food consumption for all dogs:
daily_food_per_dog × number of dogs
= 500 grams × 4 = 2000 grams
Convert the daily food consumption to kilograms:
total_daily_food ÷ 1000
= 2000 grams ÷ 1000 = 2 kilograms
Calculate the total amount of dog food purchased:
sacks × weight per sack
= 2 sacks × 50 kilograms = 100 kilograms
Calculate how many days the food will last:
total_food ÷ total_daily_food_kg
= 100 kilograms ÷ 2 kilograms = 50 days
Final Answer: 50 days
