Problem Statement

After tracking his workout routine for 8 weeks, Josh discovered that he had spent 4 hours working out every week except on some occasions. At one point, he spent 5 hours each for two consecutive weeks. Another time, he spent 6 hours in one week. What is the total number of hours he has spent working out across the 8 weeks?

Problem Understanding

Total tracking period: 8 weeks

Regular workout time: 4 hours per week

Special case 1: 5 hours each for 2 consecutive weeks

Special case 2: 6 hours in 1 week

What we need to find

The total number of hours Josh has spent working out across the 8 weeks.

Step-by-Step Explanation
Calculate the total hours for the two consecutive weeks with 5 hours each:
5 hours × 2 weeks
= 5 hours × 2 weeks = 10 hours
Add the hours from the week with 6 hours to the previous total:
special_case_1_hours + 6 hours
= 10 hours + 6 hours = 16 hours
Calculate the total number of special weeks:
2 weeks + 1 week
= 2 weeks + 1 week = 3 weeks
Calculate the number of regular weeks:
total weeks - special weeks
= 8 weeks - 3 weeks = 5 weeks
Calculate the total hours for the regular weeks:
regular hours per week × regular weeks
= 4 hours × 6 weeks = 24 hours
Calculate the total workout hours across all 8 weeks:
special_weeks_hours + regular_weeks_hours
= 16 hours + 24 hours = 40 hours
Final Answer: 40 hours
