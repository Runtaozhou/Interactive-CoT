Problem Statement

The bus driver drives an average of 2 hours each day, 5 days a week. From Monday to Wednesday he drove at an average speed of 12 kilometers per hour, and from Thursday to Friday at an average speed of 9 kilometers per hour. How many kilometers did the driver travel during these 5 days?

Problem Understanding

Daily driving hours: 2 hours

Working days: 5 days

Speed Monday-Wednesday: 12 kilometers per hour

Speed Thursday-Friday: 9 kilometers per hour

What we need to find

The total kilometers the driver traveled during these 5 days.

Step-by-Step Explanation
Calculate the total driving hours from Monday to Wednesday:
number of days × hours per day
= 3 × 2 hours = 6 hours
Calculate the distance traveled from Monday to Wednesday:
hours × speed
= 6 hours × 12 kilometers per hour = 72 kilometers
Calculate the total driving hours for Thursday and Friday:
number of days × hours per day
= 1 × 2 hours = 2 hours
Calculate the distance traveled on Thursday and Friday:
hours × speed
= 2 hours × 9 kilometers per hour = 18 kilometers
Calculate the total distance traveled during the five days:
mon_wed_distance + thu_fri_distance
= 72 kilometers + 18 kilometers = 90 kilometers
Final Answer: 90
