Problem Statement

Leo has to write a 400-word story for his literature class. 10 words fit on each line of his notebook and 20 lines fit on each page. Lucas has filled one and a half pages. How many words does he have left to write?

Problem Understanding

Total words needed: 400 words

Words per line: 10 words

Lines per page: 20 lines

Pages written: 1.5 pages

What we need to find

How many words Leo has left to write for his story.

Step-by-Step Explanation
Calculate how many lines are in half a page:
Lines per page ÷ 2
= 20 / 2 = 10 lines
Find the total number of lines written:
Full page lines + half page lines
= 20 + 10 = 30 lines
Calculate the total number of words written:
Total lines × words per line
= 30 * 10 = 300 words
Find how many words are left to write:
Total words needed - total words written
= 400 - 300 = 80 words
Final Answer: 80
