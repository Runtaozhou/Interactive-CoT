Problem Statement

In 5 years, Heath will be 3 times as old as Jude. If Heath is 16 years old today, how old is Jude today?

Problem Understanding

Heath's current age: 16 years old

What we need to find

Jude's current age.

Step-by-Step Explanation
Calculate Jude's age in 5 years by dividing Heath's future age by 3:
Heath's future age ÷ 3
= 16 / 3 = 5.333333333333333 years old
Find the difference in their ages:
Heath's current age - Jude's future age
= 16 - 5.333333333333333 = 10.666666666666666 years
Calculate Jude's current age by subtracting the age difference from Heath's current age:
Heath's current age - age difference
= 16 - 10.666666666666666 = 5.333333333333334 years
Final Answer: 5.333333333333334
