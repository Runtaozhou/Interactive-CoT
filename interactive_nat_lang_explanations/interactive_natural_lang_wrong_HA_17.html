Problem Statement

Kat decides she wants to start a boxing career. She gets a gym membership and spends 1 hour in the gym 3 times a week doing strength training. She also trained at the boxing gym 4 times a week for 1.5 hours. How many hours a week does she train?

Problem Understanding

Strength training: 1 hour, 3 times a week

Boxing training: 4 times a week for 1.5 hours

What we need to find

The total number of hours per week that Kat trains.

Step-by-Step Explanation
Calculate the total weekly hours spent on strength training:
hours per session × sessions per week
= 3 * 1 = 3 hours a week
Calculate the total strength training time including stretching:
strength_training_hours + (sessions per week × stretching time)
= 3 + (3 * 0.5) = 4.5 hours a week
Calculate the total weekly hours spent on boxing training:
hours per session × sessions per week
= 4 * 1.5 = 6 hours a week
Calculate the total training hours by adding strength and boxing hours:
total_strength_hours + boxing_hours
= 4.5 + 6 = 10.5 hours a week
Final Answer: 10.5
