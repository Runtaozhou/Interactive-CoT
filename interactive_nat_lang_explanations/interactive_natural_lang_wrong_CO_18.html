Problem Statement

Suzie and 5 of her friends decide to rent an Airbnb at Lake Tahoe for 4 days from Thursday to Sunday. The rental rate for weekdays is $420 per day. The weekend rental rate is $540 per day. They all decide to split the rental evenly. How much does each person have to pay?

Problem Understanding

Number of friends: 5

Number of days: 4

Weekday rate: $420

Weekend rate: $540

What we need to find

The amount each person has to pay after splitting the rental cost evenly.

Step-by-Step Explanation
Calculate the cost for weekdays (Thursday and Friday):
weekday_rate × number_of_weekdays
= $420 * 2 = $840
Calculate the cost for weekend days (Saturday and Sunday):
weekend_rate × number_of_weekend_days
= $540 * 1 = $540
Calculate the total cost of the rental:
weekday_cost + weekend_cost
= $840 + $540 = $1380
Calculate the cost per person:
total_cost ÷ total_number_of_people
= $1380 / 6 = $230
Final Answer: $230
