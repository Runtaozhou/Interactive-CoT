Problem Statement

Christina walks 7km to school every day from Monday to Friday. She returns home covering the same distance. Last Friday her mother asked her to pass by her friend, which is another 2km away from the school in the opposite distance from home. How many kilometers did Christina cover that week?

Problem Understanding

Distance to school: 7km every day from Monday to Friday

Distance to friend: 2km away from school

What we need to find

The total number of kilometers Christina covered that week.

Step-by-Step Explanation
Calculate the distance for one round trip to school:
distance to school × 2
= 7 km × 2 = 14km
Calculate the total distance for the week (Monday to Friday):
daily_distance × number of days
= 14 km/day × 5 days/week = 70km
Calculate the extra distance to visit her friend:
distance to friend × 2
= 2 km × 2 = 4km
Calculate the total distance for the week including the visit to her friend:
weekly_distance + extra_distance
= 70 km + 4 km = 74 km
Final Answer: 74
