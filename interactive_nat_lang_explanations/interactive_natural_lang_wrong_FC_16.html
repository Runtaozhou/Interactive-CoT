Problem Statement

Raj's house has an area equal to 1,110 square feet. It has 4 bedrooms that each measure 11 x 11 feet. There are 2 bathrooms that are 6 x 8 feet each. The kitchen and living area complete the home and they have the same sized area. How many square feet is the area of the kitchen?

Problem Understanding

Total house area: 1,110 square feet

Bedrooms: 4 rooms, each 11 x 11 feet

Bathrooms: 2 rooms, each 6 x 8 feet

What we need to find

The area of the kitchen in square feet.

Step-by-Step Explanation
Calculate the area of one bedroom:
length + width + length + width
= 11 + 11 + 11 + 11 = 44 square feet
Calculate the total area of all bedrooms:
one_bedroom_area × 4
= 44 * 4 = 176 square feet
Calculate the area of one bathroom:
length × width
= 6 * 8 = 48 square feet
Calculate the total area of all bathrooms:
one_bathroom_area × 2
= 48 * 2 = 96 square feet
Calculate the combined area of all bedrooms and bathrooms:
all_bedrooms_area + all_bathrooms_area
= 176 + 96 = 272 square feet
Calculate the combined area of kitchen and living room:
total_house_area - bedrooms_and_bathrooms_area
= 1,110 - 272 = 838 square feet
Calculate the area of the kitchen:
kitchen_and_living_area ÷ 2
= 838 / 2 = 419 square feet
Final Answer: 419
